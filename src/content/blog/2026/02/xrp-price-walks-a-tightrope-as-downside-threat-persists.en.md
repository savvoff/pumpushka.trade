## Overview

XRP's price is currently facing significant downward pressure after failing to surpass a key resistance level. The cryptocurrency has entered a corrective phase, raising concerns about its ability to maintain critical support levels.

## Current Price Movement

XRP's price experienced a notable decline after it could not break through the resistance at $1.4650. Following this, the price corrected downward, dipping below $1.40 and now trading below $1.380. This movement indicates a potential bearish trend, as XRP is also under the influence of a declining channel with resistance forming at approximately $1.3880 on the hourly chart of the XRP/USD pair, as sourced from Kraken.

As the price continues to adjust, it has tested the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the upward movement from the swing low of $1.1356 to the high of $1.5435. Currently, the bulls appear to be active near the $1.340 mark, but the overall market sentiment remains cautious.

If XRP can hold above $1.30, there may be a chance for a recovery; however, the resistance near $1.3880 poses a significant barrier. The first major resistance level is identified at $1.40, which, if cleared, could lead to further testing of the $1.420 mark. A successful move above this level might direct the price toward the $1.450 resistance, with further gains potentially reaching $1.4820. The bulls would likely encounter a major hurdle near the $1.50 level.

Conversely, if XRP fails to overcome the $1.40 resistance zone, it risks initiating a fresh decline. Initial support is observed near $1.340, with the next significant support level at $1.30 or the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level at $1.2920. A downside break and close below this level could lead to further declines toward $1.2650, with additional support near the $1.250 zone. If the price drops below this, it could continue to weaken, potentially approaching $1.2250.
