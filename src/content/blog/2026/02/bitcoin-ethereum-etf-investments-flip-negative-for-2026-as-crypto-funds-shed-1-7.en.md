## Crypto Funds Experience Withdrawals

In a recent turn of events, U.S.-listed crypto funds have seen substantial withdrawals totaling $1.7 billion. This trend comes in the wake of a decrease in prices for Bitcoin and Ethereum. The decline was notably triggered by Donald Trump’s nomination of Kevin Warsh for the position of Federal Reserve chair. The nomination of Warsh is significant as it introduces uncertainty regarding future monetary policy decisions. This reaction underscores the sensitive nature of crypto investments to external economic and political influences.
