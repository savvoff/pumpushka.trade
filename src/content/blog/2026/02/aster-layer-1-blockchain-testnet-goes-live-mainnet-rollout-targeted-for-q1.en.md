The testnet for Aster's layer-1 blockchain has now gone live, with plans for a mainnet rollout expected in the first quarter of the upcoming year.

This shift is crucial as it positions Aster to capitalize on the increasing demand for decentralized trading solutions. The decision to focus on perpetual futures trading aligns Aster with current trends in the decentralized finance (DeFi) space. Perpetual futures contracts have gained popularity due to their unique features, such as the ability to trade without an expiration date and the potential for significant leverage.
