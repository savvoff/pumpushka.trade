## Overview

Kris Marszalek, the founder of Crypto.com, has made headlines by acquiring the domain name AI.com for a significant sum. This transaction marks one of the largest deals ever recorded for a website name, with Marszalek reportedly purchasing the domain for $70 million. The acquisition is part of his strategy to establish a new online platform, which he plans to promote with a Super Bowl advertisement this weekend.
