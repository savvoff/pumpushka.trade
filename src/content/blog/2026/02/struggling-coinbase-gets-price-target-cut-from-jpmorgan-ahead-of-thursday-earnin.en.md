Furthermore, analysts have voiced concerns that the current state of the crypto market may persist, further exacerbating the challenges faced by Coinbase. The combination of reduced trading activity and declining crypto prices presents a worrying outlook for the company's financial performance, especially ahead of its upcoming earnings report.
