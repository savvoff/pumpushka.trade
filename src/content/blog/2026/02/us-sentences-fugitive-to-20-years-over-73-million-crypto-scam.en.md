## Details of the Case

The sentence was issued in absentia, meaning the individual was not present in court at the time of the ruling. Prosecutors detailed that the defendant had previously been under supervision but absconded by cutting off an ankle monitor. This act of fleeing supervision underscores the seriousness of the charges and the defendant's intent to avoid accountability for their actions.

The scam in question involved a staggering amount of $73 million, indicating the scale and impact of the fraudulent activities.
