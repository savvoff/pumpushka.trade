## Overview

XRP has experienced a notable decline in its price, dropping below $1.60 after failing to maintain levels above $1.80. The cryptocurrency is currently consolidating its losses and may face further downward pressure if it does not recover above key resistance levels.

## Current Price Movement

XRP price has extended its losses, trading below the $1.60 mark. This decline follows a failure to hold above the $1.80 threshold, similar to the trends seen in Bitcoin and Ethereum. After entering a short-term bearish zone, XRP fell below the $1.720 and $1.650 levels, eventually reaching a low of $1.50. 

Currently, XRP is consolidating its losses after a brief recovery above the $1.550 level. However, it remains below the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average and has not regained the $1.60 level, which serves as a key resistance point. The presence of a bearish trend line, with resistance at $1.650, indicates that the price could continue to decline if it fails to break through this level.

If XRP manages to initiate a fresh upward move, the immediate resistance is expected around $1.60, followed by the $1.650 level. A close above this resistance could potentially lead the price toward the $1.720 level or the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent downward movement from the swing high of $1.938 to the low of $1.50. Further resistance is noted at $1.770 and $1.80, with a significant hurdle for bulls at the $1.90 level.

Conversely, if XRP does not overcome the $1.60 resistance, it risks entering a fresh decline. Initial support is identified near the $1.540 level, followed by the $1.5150 level. A downside break below this support could lead to further declines toward $1.50 and potentially lower to the $1.4650 zone.
