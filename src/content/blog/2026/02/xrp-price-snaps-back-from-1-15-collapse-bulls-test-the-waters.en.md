## Overview

XRP has experienced a significant decline, trading below the $1.30 mark after failing to maintain higher levels. The price is currently consolidating losses, facing resistance at key levels, which could dictate its future movements.

## Price Movement and Technical Analysis

XRP price has seen a substantial drop, extending losses and trading below the $1.30 threshold. Following this decline, the price has entered a phase of consolidation but continues to encounter hurdles near $1.30 and $1.350. Specifically, XRP has dropped below the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a bearish trend.

A key trend line is forming, showing resistance at $1.380 on the hourly chart of the XRP/USD pair. If XRP remains below the $1.40 mark, it may continue its downward trajectory. The price has dipped over 15% from previous highs, failing to sustain levels above $1.50, mirroring the downward trends seen in Bitcoin and Ethereum.

The price decline saw XRP fall below both the $1.450 and $1.40 levels, entering a short-term bearish zone. XRP even reached a low of $1.1356, after which it began to consolidate losses. A minor upward movement was observed above the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level, calculated from the downward move starting at the $1.6320 swing high down to the recent low of $1.1350.

Currently, XRP is trading below $1.30, with the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average also positioned above the current price. The near-term resistance is identified at the $1.320 level. A sustained close above this level could potentially push the price towards $1.380, where the bearish trend line and the 50% Fibonacci retracement level intersect.

If XRP fails to break through the $1.320 resistance, it may initiate another decline. Initial support is identified near the $1.240 level, with major support levels found at $1.2250 and $1.20. A significant downside break below the $1.2250 mark could lead the price to continue its decline toward $1.20 and potentially lower to the $1.1650 zone.

### Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for XRP/USD is currently losing momentum in the bearish zone.
- **Hourly RSI**: The Relative Strength Index (RSI) for XRP/USD is below the 50 level, indicating a bearish sentiment.
- **Major Support Levels**: Identified at $1.220 and $1.20.
- **Major Resistance Levels**: Located at $1.320 and $1.380.
