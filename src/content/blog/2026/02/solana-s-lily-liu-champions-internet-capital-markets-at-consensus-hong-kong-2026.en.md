## Overview

At Consensus Hong Kong 2026, Lily Liu of Solana engaged in a fireside chat with Michael Lau, emphasizing the importance of finance and the central role of Asia in the evolving landscape of internet capital markets. This discussion highlighted the potential for revenue-driven growth within the digital asset ecosystem.

## Key Highlights from the Fireside Chat

Lily Liu, representing Solana, participated in a dialogue with Michael Lau that revolved around the transformative nature of internet capital markets. The conversation underscored how these markets are evolving and the significant implications they hold for the future of finance. Liu articulated her vision of how digital assets and blockchain technology could reshape traditional financial systems, offering new pathways for capital formation and investment.

The discussion also placed considerable emphasis on Asia's pivotal role in the global financial landscape. As a region that is rapidly adopting digital technologies and cryptocurrencies, Asia is seen as a critical player in the development of internet capital markets. Liu pointed out that the region's innovative spirit and technological advancements foster an environment conducive to the growth of digital finance.

Moreover, the conversation touched on the concept of revenue-driven growth within the digital asset space. Liu highlighted the importance of creating sustainable business models that prioritize revenue generation, rather than solely focusing on speculative investments. This perspective aligns with a broader trend in the cryptocurrency industry that seeks to establish legitimacy and long-term viability.
