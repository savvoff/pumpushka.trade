## Overview

MetaMask has announced the integration of Ondo, enabling users to access over 200 tokenized U.S. stocks within its crypto wallet. This integration represents a significant milestone in the accessibility of tokenized equities and exchange-traded funds (ETFs), marking one of the first instances where these financial instruments are available through a major self-custodial wallet.

## What Happened

MetaMask, a leading self-custodial wallet, has partnered with Ondo to provide its users with the ability to trade tokenized U.S. equities and ETFs directly within the wallet interface. This development is noteworthy as it not only enhances the functionality of MetaMask but also broadens the scope of investment options available to users. By incorporating tokenized stocks, MetaMask is positioning itself at the forefront of the convergence between traditional finance and the emerging world of decentralized finance (DeFi).

Tokenization of assets has been gaining traction in recent years, as it allows for fractional ownership and more accessible investment opportunities. The integration of Ondo into MetaMask facilitates this trend by allowing users to hold and trade tokenized representations of U.S. stocks, thereby bridging the gap between conventional equity markets and the burgeoning crypto ecosystem.

The availability of over 200 tokenized stocks signifies a substantial expansion in the range of assets that can be managed within a crypto wallet. This move is likely to attract a wider audience, including those who may be more familiar with traditional investment practices but are curious about the benefits of blockchain technology and digital assets.
