## Overview

Ethereum's price has recently faced challenges, slipping below significant support levels after a brief recovery attempt. As it hovers around the $2,000 mark, the cryptocurrency is at risk of further declines, with technical indicators suggesting a bearish outlook.

## Current Market Conditions

Ethereum's price initially showed signs of recovery, managing to rise above $2,000 and forming a temporary base above $1,950. During this recovery wave, Ethereum traded above key resistance levels, including $1,980 and $2,020. It even reached a peak of $2,168 before experiencing a downward correction. Currently, Ethereum is trading below $2,050 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a potential bearish trend.

The price action has revealed a contracting triangle pattern on the hourly chart of ETH/USD, with resistance identified at $2,040. The significance of this triangle lies in its potential implications for future price movements. If Ethereum remains below the $2,120 threshold, it may trigger a fresh decline, further challenging the bulls' ability to maintain upward momentum.

Ethereum's recent price dip has also tested the 38.2% Fibonacci retracement level of the upward movement from a swing low of $1,745 to the high of $2,168. This technical level could serve as a pivotal point for traders and investors, as it often indicates potential reversal areas.

Should Ethereum manage to maintain its position above $2,000, there may be an opportunity for another increase. Immediate resistance is noted near the $2,050 level, and a break above the $2,065 resistance could open the door to higher targets. The next major resistance levels are positioned near $2,120 and $2,165. A decisive move above the latter could lead to additional gains, with potential upward targets suggested in the $2,250 to $2,280 range.

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to overcome the $2,065 resistance, it risks initiating a new decline. The initial support level is identified at $2,000, followed by significant support near the $1,950 zone, which corresponds to the 50% Fibonacci retracement level. A clear breach below this support could lead to further declines, with potential targets at $1,900 and $1,850. The main support level is positioned at $1,820.
