This shift is designed to optimize the functionality and efficiency of the platform, enabling developers to create unique implementations tailored to their specific needs. By establishing its own architecture, Base is positioning itself to better serve its community and adapt to the evolving landscape of blockchain technology.

The decision to remain open-source is particularly noteworthy. It reflects a commitment to transparency and collaboration within the developer community. By encouraging developers to continue building on the platform, Base aims to leverage collective creativity and innovation, which can lead to a diverse range of applications and solutions.

Second, the open-source nature of the protocol fosters a collaborative environment.
