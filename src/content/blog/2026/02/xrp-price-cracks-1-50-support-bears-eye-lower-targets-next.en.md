## Overview

XRP has recently experienced a significant decline, trading below the $1.50 mark. This downward movement has raised concerns among traders, as the cryptocurrency faces multiple resistance levels that could hinder any potential recovery.

## Recent Price Movements

XRP's price extended its losses, dropping below the critical support level of $1.50. Following this decline, the price has been consolidating its losses but continues to encounter resistance at levels around $1.5320 and $1.550. The cryptocurrency had initially started another decline, moving below the $1.5250 zone and failing to maintain its position above $1.5320, entering a short-term bearish zone. 

The price has dipped further, reaching below $1.4850, with a low formed at $1.4330. Currently, it is consolidating losses while trading below both the $1.50 support and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. A key bearish trend line is forming, indicating resistance at $1.5850 on the hourly chart for the XRP/USD pair. The ongoing trend suggests that the pair could continue to decline if it remains below the $1.60 threshold.

In recent trading sessions, XRP's price has struggled to recover, particularly failing to surpass the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward move from the $1.6322 swing high to the $1.4330 low. If the price attempts a recovery, it may encounter resistance near the $1.480 level. The primary resistance stands at the $1.5320 mark or the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the same downward move.

Should XRP manage to close above $1.5320, it could potentially rise towards the $1.5850 resistance. However, significant hurdles remain at $1.60 and beyond, with a clear move above this level possibly leading to targets around $1.620 and $1.650. The next major resistance for bulls is identified near the $1.70 level.
