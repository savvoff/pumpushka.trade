The decision to acquire Bitcoin during a price dip indicates confidence in the asset's long-term value.

In addition to Bitcoin, Indian investors are also focusing on layer 1 tokens. Layer 1 refers to the base layer of a blockchain network, which can support various applications and transactions. By diversifying their investments across both Bitcoin and layer 1 tokens, these investors are potentially mitigating risks associated with market volatility. This strategy is essential in the cryptocurrency landscape, where price swings can be significant.

CoinDCX’s insights shed light on the behavior of Indian investors, showcasing their inclination towards maintaining a balanced portfolio. This approach may signify a growing maturity in the investment strategies of crypto enthusiasts in India, as they seek to capitalize on opportunities while managing risk effectively.
