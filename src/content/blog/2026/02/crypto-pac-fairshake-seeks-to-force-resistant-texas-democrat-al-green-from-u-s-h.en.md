## Overview

Crypto PAC Fairshake is launching a significant campaign aimed at ousting Representative Al Green from the U.S. House of Representatives. The super PAC is allocating $1.5 million to support a primary challenger against Green, who has been vocal in his criticism of the cryptocurrency industry.

## Campaign Details

Fairshake, a super PAC focused on cryptocurrency interests, has made a bold move by committing $1.5 million to influence the political landscape in Texas. This financial commitment is aimed at defeating Al Green, a Democrat who has expressed skepticism towards the crypto sector. The PAC's strategy centers around backing a primary challenger who can effectively contest Green's position in the upcoming election.

The decision to target Al Green stems from his critical stance on cryptocurrency, which has not aligned with the interests of many stakeholders in the crypto community.
