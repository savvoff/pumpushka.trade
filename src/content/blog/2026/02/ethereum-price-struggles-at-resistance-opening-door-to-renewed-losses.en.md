## Overview

Ethereum has faced significant price challenges, declining below critical support levels and struggling to maintain upward momentum. Currently, the cryptocurrency is attempting to recover from a recent low, but it encounters substantial resistance that could hinder its recovery efforts.

## Recent Price Movement

Ethereum's price has dropped sharply, falling below the $2,420 and $2,300 marks. As of now, it is trying to recover from a low of $2,155. However, the recovery is fraught with challenges, particularly around the $2,365 resistance level. The cryptocurrency has not been able to sustain a position above $2,350, leading to a fresh decline.

A bearish trend line is forming, indicating that resistance is present at the $2,350 level on the hourly chart of ETH/USD. The price is currently trading below both the $2,350 mark and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. This situation raises concerns about the potential for further declines if Ethereum remains below the $2,400 zone.

Despite the bearish trend, there was a brief recovery attempt as the price moved above $2,250, testing the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent decline from a higher swing point to the recent low. However, bearish momentum has been strong, particularly near the $2,365 level, which is proving to be a significant barrier to upward movement.

If Ethereum bulls can maintain momentum above the $2,250 level, there is a possibility of another attempt to increase the price. The immediate resistance is seen around $2,350, with the next key resistance level at $2,365. Surpassing the $2,450 threshold could lead to a price movement toward the $2,600 resistance level.

## From author

The current technical indicators suggest that Ethereum is in a precarious position. This data reflects the struggles of Ethereum to regain stability after a significant decline. The market sentiment appears to be cautious, and traders are closely monitoring the price action around the key resistance and support levels.
