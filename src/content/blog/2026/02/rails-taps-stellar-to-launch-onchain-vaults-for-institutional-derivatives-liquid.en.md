## What Happened

Rails has made a significant decision to utilize Stellar’s blockchain technology for the development of smart contract vaults. This initiative is designed to address the needs and concerns of institutional investors regarding derivatives trading. The integration of on-chain proofs is a crucial component of this strategy, as it aims to provide transparency and verifiability in transactions, potentially increasing trust among institutional participants.

By implementing segregated collateral, Rails intends to mitigate risks associated with liquidity and counterparty exposure. This approach could help in making high-speed perpetual contracts more attractive to institutions, which often prioritize security and reliability in their trading activities. The focus on institutional adoption signals a shift in the crypto landscape, where traditional financial entities are increasingly looking for ways to engage with blockchain technology and digital assets.
