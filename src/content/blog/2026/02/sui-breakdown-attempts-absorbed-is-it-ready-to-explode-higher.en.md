## Volatility and Breakdown Attempts

In terms of volatility, SUI's price against Bitcoin reached a level of 0.00001351, prompting immediate market reactions. Despite the volatility, every attempt to close below this level has been unsuccessful, with each breakdown being met with absorption. This resulted in a period of tight consolidation lasting approximately two days and eight hours, characterized by 14 consecutive candles maintaining support. Such behavior indicates active defense in the market rather than random fluctuations.

Currently, SUI's price is starting to push higher, but confirmation of this upward trend is still necessary. A significant trigger for this potential shift lies in the BTC pair. Sustained closes above 0.00001372 would break the RSI trendline, signaling a possible structural change in momentum. If this breakout occurs, it may lead to the USDT pair reclaiming the 50 Simple Moving Average (SMA), recovering the black box resistance zone, and activating an inverse head and shoulders pattern.

Until a decisive break in structure occurs with the BTC pair, the USDT pair remains constrained, trading near its range lows and below the 50 SMA. In this market setup, the direction of the BTC pair is crucial, as the USDT pair tends to follow its lead.
