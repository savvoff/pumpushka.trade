## Overview

Toobit, a cryptocurrency exchange, and LALIGA, the official Football League of Spain, have announced a strategic partnership aimed at enhancing engagement in the MENA region. This collaboration leverages the region's growing interest in digital assets and sports, with a focus on creating immersive experiences for fans and traders alike.

## Partnership Announcement

On February 4, 2026, Toobit and LALIGA held a press event where they unveiled their plans for the MENA region. The partnership is centered on the theme "Play on a bigger stage," which signifies the merging of elite football excitement with the secure environment of modern trading platforms. Given that the sports interest in MENA is projected to grow at an annual rate of 8.7%, significantly higher than the global average of 3.3%, this partnership is positioned to capitalize on that momentum.

The MENA region is notable for its high digital asset adoption, with stablecoins representing over 52% of transaction activity. This statistic underscores the significant appetite for digital assets within the region. By becoming the Official Regional Partner of LALIGA in MENA, Toobit aims to connect with a demographic that values strategic thinking and rapid decision-making. Research indicates that football fans are 78% more likely to engage with cryptocurrency compared to the general population, making this partnership particularly relevant.

## Initiatives and Rewards

To celebrate the partnership, Toobit has introduced a $2 million rewards initiative designed to foster community engagement and promote trading excellence. This initiative includes the Super Match Carnival and the Elite Championship, offering various incentives such as tiered bonuses and 150% cashback for active participants. The rewards program extends beyond digital incentives, aiming to equip fans with the knowledge and tools to navigate the digital economy securely while engaging with their favorite football clubs.

The strategic vision, articulated by the leadership of both organizations, emphasizes a fan-centric approach, highlighting the shared values between a cryptocurrency exchange and a world-class football league. The initiative includes key experiential rewards such as:

- **VIP Matchday Experiences**: All-expenses-paid trips to Spain to watch LALIGA matches live.
- **Memorabilia Giveaways**: Signed jerseys and limited-edition items from renowned clubs.
- **Legend Activations**: Events featuring LALIGA ambassadors at significant regional milestones.

These rewards aim to create a deeper, more organic connection between Toobit and the passionate fanbase of LALIGA, enhancing the overall experience for fans in the MENA region.
