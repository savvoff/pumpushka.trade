## Overview

The IP-focused blockchain has announced a delay in the unlocking of team and investor tokens, which is now set to occur in August 2026. This decision comes as the blockchain seeks to address concerns regarding token supply and market conditions, particularly amidst a backdrop of weak sentiment in the crypto market.

## Delayed Token Unlocking

The team behind the IP-focused blockchain has decided to push back the unlocking of tokens allocated to both team members and investors. Originally, these tokens were anticipated to enter circulation sooner, but the new timeline indicates that they will not be unlocked until August 2026. This strategic move is intended to mitigate the potential impact of increased supply on the market, especially given the current climate of low usage and market uncertainty.

The reasoning behind this delay is multifaceted. Primarily, it reflects a conscious effort to tighten the token economics associated with the blockchain. By postponing the release of these tokens, the team aims to create a more stable environment for existing token holders and to reduce fears of a market dump that could occur if a large number of tokens were to flood the market at once.

Additionally, this decision is part of a broader strategy to buy time for the development and enhancement of network usage. The team recognizes that a stronger foundation of active users and applications is crucial for the long-term success of the blockchain. By delaying the token unlock, they hope to foster a more robust ecosystem that can absorb new supply without causing significant price fluctuations.
