## Overview

Coinbase has introduced a new wallet specifically designed for AI agents, featuring a sandboxed and self-custodial architecture. This innovative approach aims to enhance security by keeping private keys isolated, responding to the growing trend of autonomous bots in the technology landscape.

## What Happened

Coinbase's latest offering targets a burgeoning sector of autonomous technology through the launch of a wallet tailored for AI agents. The wallet's design incorporates a self-custodial model, which means users maintain control over their private keys rather than relying on a centralized entity. This design choice is critical as it addresses the security concerns associated with the increasing use of autonomous bots.

The introduction of this wallet comes at a time when the use of AI agents is on the rise. As these agents become more prevalent in various applications, the need for secure management of digital assets has become paramount. Coinbase's wallet allows these AI agents to operate securely while ensuring the integrity of users' private keys.

The sandboxed aspect of the wallet is particularly noteworthy. It creates an isolated environment for the AI agents to function, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access or potential breaches that could compromise the user's assets. By implementing these guardrails, Coinbase aims to provide a safer framework for users who wish to integrate AI technology into their cryptocurrency activities.
