## Overview

Ethereum has experienced a significant decline in price, dropping approximately 20% after failing to maintain a level above $2,550. As of now, the cryptocurrency is struggling to hold above the critical support level of $2,200, which indicates potential further instability in the market.

## Price Decline and Current Status

The recent downturn in Ethereum's price began after it was unable to breach the $2,500 mark, leading to a fresh decline. Following this failure, Ethereum's price fell below $2,400, entering a bearish zone, and further dropped past $2,250. A low was established at $2,220. Currently, the price is trading below $2,350 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a weakening momentum.

A bearish trend line is forming with resistance positioned at $2,415 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD. If Ethereum can maintain its position above the $2,200 mark, there is potential for a rebound. However, immediate resistance is observed near the $2,350 level, with additional resistance at $2,420 and the trend line. If Ethereum successfully breaks through the $2,500 resistance, it could open the door for further gains, potentially driving the price toward the $2,620 resistance, which aligns with the 50% Fibonacci retracement level from the recent decline.

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to surpass the $2,420 resistance, it may initiate another decline. The initial support on the downside is identified at the $2,220 level, with the first major support located around $2,200. A breach below this support could lead to a drop toward the $2,120 support level, and further losses might push the price down to the $2,050 region, with the main support being at $2,000.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for ETH/USD is gaining traction in the bearish zone, suggesting that selling pressure is increasing.
- **Hourly RSI**: The Relative Strength Index (RSI) for ETH/USD is currently below the 50 zone, indicating a bearish sentiment.

The current price action of Ethereum reflects a critical juncture for the cryptocurrency.
