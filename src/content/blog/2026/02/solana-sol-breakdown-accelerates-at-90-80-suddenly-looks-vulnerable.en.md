## Overview

Solana (SOL) has faced significant downward pressure, struggling to maintain stability above critical price levels. Following a failure to settle above $102, the cryptocurrency has experienced a decline, leading to concerns about further losses as it consolidates below $95. 

## Recent Price Movements

In recent trading sessions, Solana's price has dropped below several significant support levels, including $100 and $95. This decline follows a broader trend observed in other cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin and Ethereum, which have also faced bearish momentum. Currently, SOL is trading below the 100-hourly simple moving average, indicating a prevailing negative trend.

A key bearish trend line has formed, presenting resistance near the $98 level on the hourly chart of the SOL/USD pair. The price has struggled to recover, particularly as it failed to stabilize above $105 earlier. A notable low was recorded at $89, and now the price is consolidating losses below the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent downward move from a swing high of $106 to the low of $89.

Market participants are watching for immediate resistance at around $93, while the next significant resistance levels are identified at $97 and $102. A successful close above the $102 resistance zone could potentially set the stage for a more sustained recovery. Conversely, if the price continues to struggle against the $98 resistance, further declines may be imminent.

## Possible Support Levels

Current analysis indicates that the initial support level is near $90, with the first major support identified at $85. Should the price fall below $85, it may further decline toward the $82 support zone. A close beneath $82 could lead to a more significant downturn, potentially targeting the $74 support level in the near term.

## Technical Indicators

The technical indicators reflect a bearish sentiment in the market. The hourly MACD is gaining momentum within the bearish zone, highlighting the ongoing negative trend. Additionally, the Relative Strength Index (RSI) for SOL/USD is currently below the 50 level, reinforcing the bearish outlook.
