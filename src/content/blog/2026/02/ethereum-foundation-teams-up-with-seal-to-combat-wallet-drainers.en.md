## Overview

The Ethereum Foundation has partnered with SEAL to enhance the security of Ethereum by launching a new dashboard aimed at addressing the issue of wallet drainers. This initiative is part of a broader effort to improve the overall safety of the Ethereum ecosystem.

## Collaboration for Enhanced Security

The collaboration between SEAL and the Ethereum Foundation has resulted in the development of the Trillion Dollar Security dashboard. This tool is designed to monitor and track security-related incidents within the Ethereum network. Wallet drainers, which are malicious actors that exploit vulnerabilities to steal funds from users' wallets, have become a significant concern in the crypto space. 

The creation of the dashboard signifies a proactive approach to tackling these threats. By providing real-time tracking of security issues, the dashboard aims to equip users and developers with the necessary information to safeguard their assets against potential theft. 

The Ethereum Foundation's involvement underscores the importance of community-driven efforts in enhancing security measures. With a focus on transparency and collaboration, the initiative aims to raise awareness about wallet drainers and educate users on best practices to protect their wallets.
