## Overview

The memecoin platform Pump.fun has announced the launch of its investment arm, known as Pump Fund. This new initiative aims to support emerging projects within the memecoin ecosystem through a hackathon, providing substantial funding opportunities for selected projects.

## Details of the Launch

Pump Fund will initiate its operations with a hackathon designed to encourage innovation and development within the memecoin space. Participants in this hackathon will have the opportunity to secure funding for their projects. Specifically, the fund will allocate up to $250,000 to each of the selected projects, with a total of 12 projects expected to receive this financial backing.

The valuation for these projects is set at $10 million, which signifies the fund's commitment to fostering growth and development in the memecoin sector. By offering such significant funding, Pump Fund aims to attract a diverse range of projects, potentially leading to the emergence of new and innovative memecoins or related applications. 

This initiative reflects a growing trend within the cryptocurrency market, where platforms are increasingly looking to support grassroots projects that can contribute to the overall ecosystem. By providing financial resources and encouraging creativity through a hackathon format, Pump Fund is positioning itself as a significant player in the memecoin landscape.
