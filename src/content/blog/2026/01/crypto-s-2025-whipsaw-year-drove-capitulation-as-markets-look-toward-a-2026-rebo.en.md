## Overview

The crypto market has experienced significant turbulence, particularly in 2025, according to insights from a recent outlook report. As non-bitcoin tokens have faced considerable declines, the report suggests that this downturn may set the stage for a potential rebound in 2026.

## Market Decline in 2025

In its report titled ‘Navigating Crypto in 2026,’ the fund highlights that non-bitcoin tokens have been on a downward trajectory since late 2024. This decline has been attributed to various factors that have collectively hindered the market's performance. 

### Factors Influencing the Downturn

1. **Weak Value Capture**: The report indicates that non-bitcoin tokens have been struggling with weak value capture. This may suggest that these tokens are not generating sufficient utility or demand, leading to a decrease in their overall market value.

2. **Slowing On-Chain Activity**: Another significant factor contributing to the decline is the slowing on-chain activity. This implies that fewer transactions and interactions are occurring on the blockchain for these non-bitcoin tokens, which can be a critical indicator of market health and investor interest.

3. **Fading Retail Flows**: The report also notes a decrease in retail flows, which refers to the involvement of individual investors in the crypto market. A reduction in retail participation can greatly impact the liquidity and vibrancy of the market, further exacerbating the decline of non-bitcoin tokens.

These factors combined have led to a challenging environment for non-bitcoin tokens, resulting in what the report describes as a "whipsaw" year in 2025. This term reflects the volatility and abrupt changes in market conditions that have characterized this period.

## From author

The insights provided in the report serve as a sobering reminder of the challenges faced by the crypto market, particularly for non-bitcoin assets. The conditions outlined suggest a period of adjustment for the market as it navigates through these difficulties. Understanding the reasons behind the decline is crucial for investors and stakeholders as they prepare for future market dynamics.

While the report emphasizes the challenges of 2025, it also hints at a potential recovery in 2026. The notion of a rebound suggests that there may be opportunities for growth and resurgence, contingent upon improving market conditions and renewed interest from investors.
