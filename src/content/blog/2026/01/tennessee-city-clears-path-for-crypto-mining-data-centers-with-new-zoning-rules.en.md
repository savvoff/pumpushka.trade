## New Zoning Laws

The recent ordinance introduced by the city aims to create a structured framework for cryptocurrency mining activities. This amendment to the zoning laws specifically delineates the areas where crypto mining operations can be established. By doing so, the city seeks to regulate the growth of this industry while ensuring that it aligns with community standards and expectations.

In addition to defining operational zones, the ordinance also addresses the issue of noise generated by mining activities. The guidelines stipulate acceptable noise levels, which is critical for maintaining the quality of life for residents in proximity to mining facilities. This aspect of the ordinance reflects an understanding of the potential disturbances that such operations can cause, and it signifies the city’s commitment to balancing economic development with community well-being.
