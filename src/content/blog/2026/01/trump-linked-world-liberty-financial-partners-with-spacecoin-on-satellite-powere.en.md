## Overview

World Liberty Financial, associated with former President Donald Trump, has partnered with Spacecoin to enhance decentralized finance (DeFi) through satellite technology. This collaboration focuses on providing internet access via a satellite constellation, particularly aimed at reaching remote and underserved communities.

## The Partnership

Spacecoin is at the forefront of innovation in the DeFi space, with a mission to deliver permissionless internet access through its satellite constellation. By leveraging satellite technology, Spacecoin seeks to address the digital divide that affects many areas lacking reliable internet connectivity. The partnership with World Liberty Financial is positioned to amplify these efforts, potentially making significant strides in expanding internet access to marginalized populations.

The implications of this partnership are profound, particularly in the context of the ongoing push for greater financial inclusion. Access to the internet is a prerequisite for engaging in DeFi activities, which often require online connectivity for transactions, information access, and participation in decentralized applications. By targeting remote and underserved communities, Spacecoin aims to empower individuals who have previously been excluded from the digital economy.

World Liberty Financial's involvement adds a layer of credibility and visibility to the initiative. The company's association with a high-profile political figure may attract attention and investment, further fueling the growth of Spacecoin's satellite-based internet solutions. This partnership underscores the intersection of technology, finance, and socio-economic development, highlighting the potential for innovative solutions to bridge gaps in access and opportunity.
