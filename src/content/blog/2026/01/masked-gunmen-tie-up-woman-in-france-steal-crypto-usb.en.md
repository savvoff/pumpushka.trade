## Overview

France has recently gained attention due to a series of violent incidents linked to cryptocurrency. The country has become a focal point for what are known as "crypto wrench attacks," where individuals are targeted for their digital assets. A recent incident involved masked gunmen who tied up a woman and stole a USB device containing cryptocurrency.

## Incident Details

In a troubling escalation of crime associated with cryptocurrency, masked gunmen invaded a private residence in France. During this violent encounter, they tied up a woman, illustrating the extreme measures some individuals are willing to take to obtain digital assets. The assailants stole a USB device believed to contain cryptocurrency, highlighting the risks that cryptocurrency holders face in their personal lives.

This incident is not isolated; it is part of a broader trend in France. The country has witnessed a surge in crypto-related attacks, with over 14 such incidents documented in the previous year alone. These attacks are characterized by their violent nature and the specific targeting of individuals known to possess cryptocurrency. The growing number of incidents raises concerns about the safety of individuals engaged in the cryptocurrency space.
