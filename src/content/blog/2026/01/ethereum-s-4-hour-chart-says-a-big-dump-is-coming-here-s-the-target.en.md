## Current Market Analysis

A recent analysis by crypto expert Tyrex highlights concerns regarding Ethereum's 4-hour chart, which suggests the cryptocurrency may be on the verge of a significant price decline if buyers do not regain control. The analysis points out that Ethereum recently experienced a brief dip below a key support level, triggering a liquidity sweep, but this move was reversed quickly, indicating that it was more of a fakeout than a genuine bearish breakdown.

Despite this reversal, Tyrex emphasizes that the broader pattern on the 4-hour chart remains largely unchanged. Ethereum has repeatedly returned to the same support area, raising alarms about weakening demand. The analyst notes that when the price continually revisits the same lows, it often signifies increasing pressure rather than strength. Currently, Ethereum is consolidating just above the highlighted support zone, but momentum appears to have slowed compared to earlier rallies. The market is hesitating at a critical area rather than showing signs of continuation.

Tyrex warns that this hesitation poses a significant risk. The repeated testing of the same lows makes the market more susceptible to a deeper price drop. Each retest allows sellers to gain an advantage, thereby increasing the likelihood of a breakdown in support as buyers gradually lose control. The chart analysis also suggests a potential downward trajectory if support fails, with a drop below the purple zone putting Ethereum at risk of declining towards the next downside area.

At the time of Tyrex's analysis, Ethereum was trading around a certain price level, which would suggest a minor decline if it moves to a specified lower range. However, as of the latest update, Ethereum has already fallen below this initial target, indicating further weakening of upward momentum and hinting at the possibility of a larger decline.

## Analyst Recommendations

In light of the bearish trends facing Ethereum, Tyrex has recommended that investors adopt a wait-and-see approach. He indicates that the outlook for ETH is not entirely negative.
