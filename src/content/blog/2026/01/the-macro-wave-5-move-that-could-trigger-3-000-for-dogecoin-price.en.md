Despite this downward trajectory, the broader bullish structure has not been invalidated, which is crucial for the potential future performance of the asset.

The descending channel serves as a significant aspect of this technical analysis. Historical patterns show that similar corrective behaviors occurred before Dogecoin's last major price expansion in 2021. The analyst highlights a specific range between two price levels, which has shown sustained buying pressure and has effectively prevented deeper price declines.
