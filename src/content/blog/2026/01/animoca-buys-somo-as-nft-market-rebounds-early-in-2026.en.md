Animoca's decision to acquire Somo is part of a broader strategy to enhance its position in the digital collectibles space. By bringing Somo into its portfolio, Animoca aims to leverage the strengths and capabilities of Somo to further develop and expand its offerings in the NFT market. This acquisition reflects a growing interest in NFTs as they continue to evolve and capture the attention of collectors and investors alike.

The NFT market has experienced significant fluctuations over the past few years, with periods of rapid growth followed by downturns. However, recent indications suggest that trading activity within the NFT sector is beginning to pick up again. This renewed interest is pivotal for companies like Animoca, which are deeply invested in the digital collectibles ecosystem.
