## Overview

Sygnum, a Swiss bank, has successfully raised over 750 Bitcoin for its market-neutral BTC Alpha Fund. This fund has reported annualized returns of 8.9% in the fourth quarter, attracting interest from professional and institutional investors.

This performance is particularly compelling given the volatility often associated with cryptocurrency investments.
