## Overview

Market analyst Egrag Crypto has provided insights into the current price structure of XRP, indicating that it remains largely bullish despite recent challenges in surpassing the $2 mark. The analysis highlights a critical decision zone that XRP is approaching, which could play a significant role in determining its next price movement.

## XRP Price Structure Analysis

Egrag Crypto shared observations on XRP's 3-day chart, noting strong bullish signals. Despite experiencing prolonged periods of consolidation following its recent rebound above $2, XRP's price structure is said to retain a bullish outlook. The cryptocurrency is currently compressing within a descending channel as it approaches a key decision zone situated between $2.30 and $2.40. This compression is often seen after a strong price movement and may lead to a larger price expansion.

In his analysis, Egrag Crypto pointed out several key trends on the 3-day chart of XRP. The 50 Exponential Moving Average (EMA) has started to flatten, suggesting a potential easing of selling pressure for XRP. Additionally, the 200 EMA continues to trend upward, reinforcing the analysis that the macro trend for XRP remains bullish. Importantly, XRP is trading above the EMA cluster, which indicates structural strength rather than weakness.

Egrag Crypto further emphasized that the upper boundary of the descending channel aligns closely with crucial resistance levels at $2.30. He noted that if XRP achieves a clean 3-day close above $2.40, it may confirm a breakout from the descending channel. Such a breakout could potentially lead to a continuation towards higher price levels.

Conversely, if XRP faces rejection at the channel’s resistance, it is anticipated that the price may remain range-bound. Egrag Crypto concluded that as long as XRP maintains trading above $2, its bullish structure will persist, viewing the ongoing consolidation phase as a precursor to a potential major price expansion.

The chart analysis also indicates that the lower boundary of the descending channel touches a significant support area. If XRP fails to hold above the $2 mark and drops below it, this could invalidate the bullish thesis laid out by the analyst. Should this occur, XRP could potentially decline toward the next support level at $1.65, which represents a notable drop from current levels. A further decline below $1.65 could lead XRP towards a critical support level around $1.00, reflecting a substantial decrease from its recent price.
