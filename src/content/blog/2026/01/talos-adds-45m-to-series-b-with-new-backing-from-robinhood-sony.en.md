## Overview

Talos, a New York-based crypto infrastructure company, has successfully added $45 million to its Series B funding round. This extension has garnered significant backing from notable institutional investors, including Robinhood and Sony, which has contributed to an increased valuation of approximately $1.5 billion for the company.

The recent extension of Talos's Series B funding reflects the growing interest and investment in the cryptocurrency sector from established financial entities. With this additional capital, Talos aims to enhance its services and expand its offerings in the rapidly evolving crypto market. The involvement of high-profile investors such as Robinhood and Sony highlights the confidence that established companies have in the potential of Talos and its infrastructure solutions.

The Series B funding round initially attracted investments from various institutional investors, indicating a strong belief in Talos's business model and its potential to play a significant role in the crypto ecosystem.
