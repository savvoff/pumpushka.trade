## Overview

As the cryptocurrency market navigates a phase of uncertainty and consolidation, market expert Sam Daodu has released a report analyzing the potential of XRP, Ethereum, and Solana to stand out as leading contenders if a new altcoin season materializes in 2026. His insights highlight key performance indicators and various scenarios that could shape the future of these altcoins.

## Analysis of Predictions

Daodu's analysis begins with the current state of the crypto market, where Bitcoin's dominance is around 59%, accompanied by an Altcoin Season Index reading of 55. These metrics suggest a significant potential for a rotation toward altcoins, reminiscent of notable market shifts observed during previous cycles in 2016-2017 and 2020-2021.

### XRP Forecast

For XRP, Daodu envisions a bullish scenario where the price could exceed a range of $6 to $8. This potential surge hinges on the continuation of exchange-traded fund (ETF) inflows averaging over $400 million monthly, coupled with RippleNet's growing influence in global banking. In more stable conditions, XRP might consolidate between $2.50 and $3.50, assuming steady institutional adoption without major catalysts.

### Ethereum Outlook

Ethereum could witness a climb toward a range of $12,000 to $18,000 if Layer 2 (L2) adoption leads to increased usage and if ETF inflows rebound. Daodu points out that active addresses are currently at cycle highs, indicating a base level of organic demand that could result in higher prices once institutional sentiment turns favorable. In a more tempered scenario, ETH might trade between $5,000 and $9,000, benefiting from ongoing demand driven by staking yields and decentralized finance (DeFi) growth.

### Solana Potential

Solana's outlook is also optimistic, with the potential to reach a price range of $500 to $800. This projection is based on its impressive transaction finality of 150 milliseconds and low fees, which could attract a new wave of applications. Additionally, the increase in ETF filings may lead to significant capital inflows. Under more stable conditions, Solana could trend between $200 and $350, provided that developer growth and retail adoption maintain their current pace.

## Risks and Challenges

While the forecasts are generally bullish, Daodu highlights potential risks ahead for each altcoin. For XRP, a decline below $1.50 could occur if ETF demand diminishes or if regulatory uncertainties arise. Ethereum could fall below $2,500 if scalability issues are not addressed or if regulatory challenges intensify. Similarly, Solana might drop below $100 if it experiences outages or faces heightened competition from other Layer 1 platforms.
