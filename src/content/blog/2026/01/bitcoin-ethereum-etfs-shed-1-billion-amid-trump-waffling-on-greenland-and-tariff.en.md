This decline coincided with President Donald Trump's withdrawal from his previously stated threats regarding Greenland, stirring uncertainty among market participants.

In a notable turn of events, President Trump retracted his threats concerning the acquisition of Greenland. This unexpected shift in rhetoric appears to have influenced investor sentiment, prompting many to seek safety by reducing their exposure to cryptocurrencies. As a result, Bitcoin and Ethereum ETFs saw a considerable outflow of funds, totaling approximately $1 billion.
