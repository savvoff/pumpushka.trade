## Overview

Ethereum has recently demonstrated a steady upward movement, breaking above the $3,120 mark and consolidating gains. This positive momentum indicates potential further increases, particularly if the price surpasses the $3,250 resistance level.

## Recent Developments

Ethereum's price trajectory began to shift positively after it settled above the $3,050 zone. The cryptocurrency experienced a notable increase, surpassing both the $3,120 and $3,150 resistance levels. This bullish momentum propelled the price toward $3,220, with a peak reaching $3,264. Following this high, Ethereum has entered a consolidation phase, fluctuating around the $3,200 mark.

The current trading scenario places Ethereum above the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, signaling a robust short-term bullish trend. Additionally, a short-term bullish trend line is forming, with a support level identified at $3,185 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD. The price action suggests that if Ethereum can maintain support above $3,150, it may attempt further upward movement.

Immediate resistance is currently situated near the $3,240 level, with the first key resistance identified at $3,250. Should Ethereum manage to break above this threshold, it could pave the way for a potential increase toward the $3,265 resistance. A decisive move above the $3,265 resistance level could lead to further gains, possibly targeting the $3,350 region and beyond.

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to breach the $3,250 resistance level, a downside correction may ensue. Initial support is found near the $3,200 level, which corresponds to the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent increase. Major support exists at the $3,185 zone, and a significant decline below this level could push the price down toward $3,120 or even $3,050.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for ETH/USD is currently losing momentum within the bullish zone, indicating potential weakening of the upward trend.
- **Hourly RSI**: The Relative Strength Index (RSI) for ETH/USD remains above the 50 zone, suggesting that bullish sentiment is still present in the market.
