## Overview

Solana has recently demonstrated a significant upward movement, breaking above the $142 resistance zone. This increase highlights the potential for further gains as the price consolidates above this level, with a focus on reaching the $150 mark.

## Recent Price Movements

Solana's price action indicates a fresh upward trend, having settled above the $135 zone. This movement mirrors the positive trends seen in other cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. The SOL price has successfully breached the $140 level, which has marked its entry into a short-term bullish phase. Following this, the price surpassed the $142 resistance, driven by strong buying pressure from bulls who also pushed the price above $145.

The recent high reached by Solana was at $148, after which the price has been consolidating. Notably, this consolidation occurs above the $142 mark and the 100-hourly simple moving average, indicating a robust support structure. A bullish trend line is also forming, with support established at the $140 level on the hourly chart of the SOL/USD pair. 

As the price continues to face resistance near the $148 level, the next major resistance is identified at $150. A successful breakthrough above this level could pave the way for further upward momentum, with subsequent resistance levels noted at $155 and $162. 

However, the analysis also points to potential downside risks. If Solana fails to overcome the $148 resistance, it may experience a decline. Initial support is observed near the $144 zone, while a significant support level is identified at $143, corresponding to the 50% Fibonacci retracement of the recent upward movement. Should the price drop below $143, it could decline toward the $140 support area, and further weakness might lead to a test of the $135 support level.

## Technical Indicators

Several technical indicators are currently in play for Solana:

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for SOL/USD is gaining momentum within the bullish zone, suggesting a favorable outlook for the price.
