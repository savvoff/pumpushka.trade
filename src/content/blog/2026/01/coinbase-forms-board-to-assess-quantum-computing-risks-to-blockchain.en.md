## Overview

Coinbase has established an independent advisory board aimed at evaluating the potential risks that quantum computing poses to blockchain technology. This initiative involves a collaboration of researchers and industry experts who will focus on the implications of quantum advancements on digital security.

## Formation of the Advisory Board

The newly formed independent advisory board is tasked with examining the intersection of quantum computing and blockchain. This board will consist of a diverse group of researchers and industry professionals, each bringing unique expertise to the discussion. Their primary objective is to analyze the potential vulnerabilities that quantum computing may introduce to blockchain systems.

The board intends to engage in comprehensive research and will publish papers that address digital-security risks associated with the rise of quantum computing. These publications will also provide guidance tailored for developers, organizations, and users, helping them navigate the evolving landscape of digital security in light of quantum advancements.

## Importance of the Initiative

The establishment of this advisory board is significant for several reasons. First, it highlights the proactive steps that Coinbase is taking to ensure the security and integrity of its platform and the broader blockchain ecosystem. By addressing the challenges posed by quantum computing, Coinbase aims to safeguard its users and maintain trust in its services.

Furthermore, as quantum computing technology continues to advance, the potential for it to disrupt existing cryptographic methods becomes a pressing concern. The advisory board's work will be crucial in identifying and mitigating these risks, thereby contributing to the overall resilience of blockchain technology.
