## The Expert's Perspective

In an exchange on X, a user inquired about when Bitcoin would experience another significant price increase. The expert responded, stating that depending on a single price explosion to amass wealth is misguided. Instead, they advocated for a strategy based on accumulating Bitcoin over time, which they referred to as “time plus stacking.” This approach emphasizes the importance of regular, smaller purchases of Bitcoin, which can help investors avoid the emotional highs and lows associated with market volatility.

The expert pointed out that Bitcoin is a fixed supply asset, capped at 21 million coins. This characteristic is crucial because it positions Bitcoin as a store of value, contrasting with fiat currencies that tend to lose purchasing power over time. For long-term holders like Jeremie, the focus should be on using Bitcoin to preserve value earned from other sources, rather than treating it as a speculative asset.

The concept of “stacking” involves buying small amounts of Bitcoin regularly, which can mitigate the pressure to time the market accurately. By holding these assets for several years, investors can adopt a more mechanical and less emotional investment strategy. In contrast, many newcomers to Bitcoin often chase quick gains, which can lead to erratic buying and selling behaviors.
