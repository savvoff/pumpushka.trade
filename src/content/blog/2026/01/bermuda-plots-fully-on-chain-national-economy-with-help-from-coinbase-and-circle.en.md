## Overview

Bermuda is embarking on an ambitious initiative to transition its entire economy onto the blockchain. The government aims to leverage the expertise and resources of Coinbase and Circle to facilitate this transformation. This move signifies a significant step toward integrating cryptocurrency and blockchain technology into a national economic framework.

## Bermuda's Initiative

The government of Bermuda has announced its intention to create a fully on-chain national economy. This project is poised to revolutionize how economic transactions are conducted within the territory. By moving its economy onto a blockchain platform, Bermuda is exploring the potential benefits of increased transparency, efficiency, and security in financial operations.

To achieve this goal, Bermuda is collaborating with two prominent players in the cryptocurrency space: Coinbase and Circle. Both companies have established reputations in the industry and bring valuable experience in blockchain technology and digital asset management. Their involvement is expected to provide Bermuda with the necessary infrastructure and insights to implement this transformative initiative effectively.

The decision to pursue a fully on-chain economy aligns with Bermuda's broader strategy to position itself as a leader in the global digital economy. By embracing blockchain technology, the island aims to attract businesses and investors looking for innovative solutions in financial services, insurance, and other sectors. This initiative could potentially enhance Bermuda's reputation as a forward-thinking jurisdiction that is adaptable to the evolving landscape of global finance.
