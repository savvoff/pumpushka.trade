## Overview

The world of non-fungible tokens (NFTs) is not as lifeless as some may believe. According to Yat Siu, co-founder of Animoca Brands and a dedicated NFT collector, a robust community of affluent collectors continues to thrive, purchasing NFTs for ownership rather than resale. This insight sheds light on the ongoing interest and activity within the NFT market, despite broader trends that may suggest otherwise.

## The Current State of the NFT Market

Yat Siu's comments highlight a significant aspect of NFT ownership: the motivation behind purchases. Unlike traditional markets where flipping assets for profit is commonplace, Siu emphasizes that many collectors are in it for the long haul, valuing the personal connection to their digital assets. This community of collectors is characterized by a commitment to ownership over speculation, which may provide stability to the NFT market amidst fluctuating trends.

The emphasis on ownership indicates a shift in how NFTs are perceived. Rather than viewing these digital assets solely as investment opportunities, collectors are increasingly focusing on the unique value and personal significance of their purchases. This trend challenges the narrative that NFTs are merely a passing fad or a speculative bubble, suggesting instead that there is a sustainable market driven by genuine interest and passion.

Siu's perspective serves as a reminder that the NFT space is not solely defined by financial metrics. The emotional and cultural significance tied to NFTs can play a crucial role in their enduring appeal. As collectors continue to invest in digital art and assets, the market may find a new equilibrium that prioritizes long-term engagement over short-term gains.
