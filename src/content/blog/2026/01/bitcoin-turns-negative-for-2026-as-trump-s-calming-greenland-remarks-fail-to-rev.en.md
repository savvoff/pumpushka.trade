The cryptocurrency market witnessed a notable response to President Trump's remarks about Greenland. He stated that the United States had no intention of taking the territory by force. This comment momentarily alleviated some market concerns, leading to a modest bounce in Bitcoin's price. However, this uptick was fleeting, and the cryptocurrency quickly resumed its decline shortly thereafter.
