This uptick in whale activity is seen as a sign of reaccumulation among larger holders of Bitcoin. The timing of this increase is particularly relevant as it aligns with a resurgence of spot Bitcoin ETF inflows, which have garnered attention from investors seeking exposure to the cryptocurrency.

Whales, typically defined as entities holding large amounts of Bitcoin, appear to be taking advantage of the recent price fluctuations to accumulate more of the asset. This behavior often indicates a belief in the long-term value of Bitcoin, as these large holders tend to have a significant influence on market trends and price movements.

The simultaneous rise in whale balances and spot ETF inflows suggests a growing confidence in the market. The reference to a potential support level at $100,000 highlights the optimistic sentiment among some market participants.
