## Overview

XRP has recently exhibited a significant shift in its relative strength against Ethereum (ETH), as highlighted by crypto analyst Matt Hughes. This development is underscored by a notable change in the 2-week Ichimoku cloud dynamics, which hasn't demonstrated reliable support since 2021. The current market situation presents a critical moment for XRP, presenting both an opportunity and a risk for traders.

## XRP's Relative Strength Against ETH

According to Hughes, XRP's performance against ETH is signaling a potential regime change. The XRP/ETH pair is currently pressing into the upper range of the 2-week Ichimoku cloud, which is indicative of a shift that could lead to XRP flipping the cloud from resistance to support. This transformation is crucial, as the Ichimoku cloud has historically posed a significant barrier for XRP. A sustained hold above this cloud could mark a pivotal moment for XRP's future performance in the market.

Hughes notes that for XRP to solidify this bullish outlook, it must reclaim a specific price level. If XRP can successfully hold above the Ichimoku cloud, it would indicate a newfound strength and potential upward momentum for the asset. Conversely, failing to maintain this level could suggest that the breakout narrative remains premature.

## XRP's Price Dynamics

In addition to its performance against ETH, Hughes analyzes XRP's price action in USD. He observes that XRP has been oscillating within a defined range, showing signs of consolidation. This behavior reflects a market that has not yet resolved its larger price structure. The price has been respecting certain horizontal levels and sloping fan lines, which highlight the challenges XRP faces in its upward attempts.

Hughes identifies a crucial threshold for XRP's upward movement: a sustained close above a defined price level. This level is critical for triggering further price appreciation. If XRP can break through this barrier, it opens the door for additional price targets that could lead to significant increases.

## From author

The recent developments in XRP's price dynamics and its relative strength against ETH mark a noteworthy juncture for the cryptocurrency.
