## Що сталося?

The MVRV Z-score, which measures the ratio of Bitcoin's market value to its realized value, has hit unprecedented lows. This metric is utilized by analysts to assess whether Bitcoin is overvalued or undervalued relative to its historical performance. A lower MVRV Z-score indicates that the asset is undervalued, which can often signal potential buying opportunities for investors.

The current MVRV Z-score suggests that Bitcoin's price is in a state of undervaluation, surpassing the lows recorded during prior bear markets. This development is noteworthy as it implies that Bitcoin's current market conditions may present a favorable environment for accumulation, similar to previous instances when the market eventually rebounded after prolonged downturns.
