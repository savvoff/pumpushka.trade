## Importance of Tokenization

Tokenization involves converting physical assets into digital tokens that can be traded on blockchain platforms. This process can enhance liquidity, enable fractional ownership, and make it easier for investors to access high-value assets that were previously difficult to invest in. ETHZilla's focus on this area suggests a recognition of the growing demand for innovative investment options within the cryptocurrency sector.

By investing in aircraft engines, ETHZilla may be laying the groundwork for future projects that could include the tokenization of these engines or similar high-value assets.
