## Overview

Ethereum is making strides towards enhancing its security against potential quantum computing threats. Researcher Antonio Sanso highlighted the platform's commitment to post-quantum security, detailing a structured approach that includes significant upgrades across various components of the network.

## Ethereum's Plan for Quantum Resilience

In a recent discussion, Antonio Sanso elaborated on Ethereum's roadmap for achieving post-quantum security. The researcher emphasized that Ethereum possesses a “clear plan” to address the challenges posed by advancements in quantum computing. This plan is centered around major upgrades that will enhance the network's execution and consensus mechanisms, as well as improvements in data management through the use of data blobs.

The importance of these upgrades cannot be overstated. As quantum computing technology progresses, it poses a potential risk to current cryptographic standards that underpin blockchain security. By proactively developing a strategy to bolster its defenses, Ethereum is positioning itself to maintain the integrity and security of its network in the face of these emerging threats.

Sanso's insights suggest that Ethereum is already approximately “20%” of the way toward achieving its goal of quantum resilience. This progress indicates that the Ethereum development team is actively working on the necessary upgrades and innovations to ensure that the network remains robust against the capabilities of quantum computers.
