## Overview

XRP has experienced a notable decline, trading below the $2.00 mark after a significant drop. The price is currently consolidating, with the potential for further losses if it does not regain key resistance levels.

## Recent Price Movements

XRP's price trajectory has taken a downturn as it extended its losses, falling below the $2.00 threshold. This decline marks a fresh bearish trend for the cryptocurrency, which has struggled to maintain its position above $2.10. The recent price action mirrors a broader market trend, where notable cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum have also exhibited declines.

The XRP price broke below a contracting triangle formation with a support level at $2.050. Following this break, it plunged to a low of $1.847 before showing signs of a temporary recovery above $1.920. Despite this bounce, XRP remains below the critical $2.00 level and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, which suggests a bearish sentiment persists in the market.

As the price attempts to stabilize, it is facing significant resistance near the $1.980 level and the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level from the previous swing high of $2.065 down to the low of $1.847. For XRP to regain upward momentum, it must clear the $2.00 resistance. A close above this mark could potentially lead to a rise toward the $2.065 level, with subsequent hurdles at $2.10 and $2.120.

Conversely, if XRP fails to breach the $2.00 resistance, it may face further declines. Initial support levels are observed near $1.9320 and $1.90. A downward break and close below $1.90 could trigger a more significant decline toward the $1.850 mark, with further support located at $1.820.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for XRP/USD is currently losing momentum within the bearish zone, indicating a potential continuation of the downward trend.

- **Major Support Levels**: Current support levels are identified at $1.920 and $1.90.
- **Major Resistance Levels**: Resistance levels are noted at $1.980 and $2.00.
