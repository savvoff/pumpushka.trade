## Overview

Solana Mobile has announced the launch date for its SKR token, which is set for January 21. Alongside this launch, the team has confirmed that an airdrop will take place, with a significant portion of the total token supply allocated for eligible users and developers.

## Launch Details

The upcoming launch of the SKR token by Solana Mobile is a significant event in the crypto space. The team has indicated that 20% of the total supply of the SKR token will be reserved specifically for users and developers who meet eligibility criteria. This allocation is intended to incentivize participation and engagement within the Solana ecosystem, fostering a community that can actively contribute to the platform's growth and development.

The decision to reserve a portion of the token supply for users and developers highlights the importance of community involvement in the success of blockchain projects. By distributing tokens through an airdrop, Solana Mobile aims to encourage wider adoption and usage of the SKR token, creating a more vibrant and active user base.
