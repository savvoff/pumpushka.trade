## Overview

Solana Mobile is set to launch its SKR token on January 21, with plans to airdrop up to 20% of these tokens to users of the Seeker phone. In addition to the token launch, the company will introduce a new feature known as Guardians, aimed at enhancing the security of its network.

## SKR Token Airdrop and Launch

On January 21, Solana Mobile will initiate the distribution of its SKR token, which is linked to the Seeker phone. This airdrop will allocate up to 20% of the total SKR tokens to users of the device, incentivizing early adoption and engagement within the Solana ecosystem. The decision to airdrop a significant portion of the tokens reflects Solana Mobile's strategy to foster a community around its mobile platform and reward users for their participation.

The SKR token is expected to play a crucial role in the functionality of the Seeker phone, which is designed to enhance user experience in the crypto space. By integrating the SKR token into the Seeker phone's ecosystem, Solana Mobile aims to provide users with various benefits, potentially including access to specific features or services that utilize the token.
