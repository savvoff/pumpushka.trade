## What Happened

Ilya Lichtenstein, who was implicated in the theft of a significant amount of Bitcoin, has been transferred from prison to home confinement. The White House has responded to inquiries about this transition, clarifying that while they support the decision, they do not take credit for it. This situation arises from Lichtenstein's involvement in one of the largest cryptocurrency heists in history, which has had lasting implications on the security of digital assets and the regulatory landscape surrounding them.

The decision to place Lichtenstein in home confinement rather than maintaining him in a traditional prison setting has raised eyebrows.
