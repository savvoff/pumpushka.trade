## Overview

XRP is at a crucial juncture, as analysts highlight a potential volatility-compression setup on both the monthly and daily charts. Analysts such as Matt Hughes and Charting Guy suggest that if XRP maintains key high-timeframe supports, it could be poised for a significant upward movement.

## Current Market Situation

The analysis indicates that XRP has successfully defended its long-term trend support, specifically bouncing off the 20-month moving average. This bounce is seen as a strong indicator of potential expansion. According to Matt Hughes, XRP is currently in a phase where the Bollinger Bands are tightening, which historically precedes explosive price movements. He emphasizes that the market sentiment appears bearish, despite the price being positioned above important support levels.

On the monthly chart, XRP is depicted trading around a specific price point, above the Bollinger basis. The analysis notes that the upper band and lower band of the Bollinger Bands are at defined levels, and the tightening of these bands signifies a potential for significant directional movement when the current phase resolves. Hughes draws parallels to XRP's price behavior in 2017, suggesting that the current situation reflects a similar consolidation period before a potential price surge.

Additionally, the recent market activity following a liquidation event on October 10 saw XRP pierce the 20-month moving average. Since that moment, XRP has consolidated above this key level, which could indicate a bullish trend if it continues to hold.

Charting Guy's daily analysis offers a different perspective, employing a Wyckoff-style approach to describe XRP's price action as a re-accumulation phase. This interpretation suggests that XRP is currently facing overhead supply while needing to decisively reclaim and maintain a specific price level to validate bullish momentum. The analysis identifies $2.08 as a critical level that XRP must surpass to continue its upward trajectory.

In this framework, a controlled pullback is anticipated, where XRP will test demand and validate whether buyers are absorbing supply effectively. If this test is successful, the path could lead to a breakout through the descending channel, progressing towards higher price targets.
