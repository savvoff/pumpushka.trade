## Overview

The Bitcoin utility blockchain, VerifiedX, has launched its new payment application called Butterfly, which aims to provide a user-friendly experience similar to Venmo but for cryptocurrency transactions. This app marks a significant step for VerifiedX in enhancing cryptocurrency usability through strategic partnerships with established entities in the crypto space.

## Butterfly App Launch

The Butterfly app has officially gone live, offering a streamlined platform for users to send and receive cryptocurrency payments easily. This initiative is part of VerifiedX's broader strategy to increase the practical use of Bitcoin in everyday transactions. The app is designed to cater to both crypto enthusiasts and newcomers, simplifying the process of handling digital currencies.

The launch of Butterfly is underscored by partnerships with notable companies in the cryptocurrency ecosystem, including Crypto.com, Moonpay, and Blockdaemon. These collaborations are crucial as they provide the necessary infrastructure and support to facilitate secure and efficient transactions within the app.

### Significance of the Partnerships

The involvement of Crypto.com, Moonpay, and Blockdaemon highlights the importance of collaboration in the crypto industry. Crypto.com is known for its comprehensive financial services and has a large user base, which can help promote the Butterfly app. Moonpay specializes in enabling easy crypto purchases with fiat currencies, making it easier for users to convert traditional money into digital assets. Blockdaemon, on the other hand, provides blockchain infrastructure, ensuring that the app operates efficiently and securely.

Together, these partnerships enhance the functionality and reliability of the Butterfly app, potentially attracting a wider audience and increasing overall adoption of cryptocurrency for everyday transactions.
