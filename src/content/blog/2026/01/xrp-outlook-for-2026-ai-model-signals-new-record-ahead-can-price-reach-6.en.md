## Overview

A new artificial intelligence model has generated projections for XRP's price trajectory through the end of 2026. Market analyst Sam Daodu shared insights derived from Claude AI, indicating three potential price paths influenced by various market factors, including demand for exchange-traded funds (ETFs), regulatory clarity, and network activity on the XRP Ledger (XRPL).

## Projections for XRP

The Claude AI model presents three distinct scenarios that could shape XRP's performance in the coming years. Each scenario is contingent upon key market catalysts that may either bolster or hinder the cryptocurrency's price movement.

### Optimistic Scenario: Potential Rally

In the most favorable scenario, Claude AI forecasts that XRP could rise to a range between $4 and $6. This scenario suggests a potential increase of approximately 215% from a baseline price of around $2.15. The bullish outcome is predicated on ETF inflows surpassing $5 billion and declining exchange balances, which would indicate less selling pressure in the market. Under these conditions, institutional accumulation is expected to enhance spot market demand, while clearer regulatory frameworks would potentially improve overall market sentiment. 

Moreover, the model indicates that if XRP manages to break above a resistance level of $3.20, tightening liquidity on major trading platforms could amplify even minor buying activities. By late 2026, the potential for long-term holders to limit supply could further decrease market depth, allowing prices to rise more swiftly. However, this scenario hinges on unexpected positive developments and exceeds the forecasts of many other AI models.

### Base Case Prediction: Moderate Growth

The base case prediction offers a more conservative assessment, suggesting XRP could trade between $2.00 and $3.00. In this scenario, ETF inflows are anticipated to be steady but not exceptional, with adoption growing at a gradual pace rather than experiencing explosive growth. The model indicates that XRP would likely maintain support above the $2.00 mark, facilitated by manageable escrow token releases and incremental improvements to the XRPL that support ongoing transaction growth. Price fluctuations are expected to remain contained, with accumulation occurring subtly rather than through sharp price rallies. By the end of 2026, XRP may stabilize near the midpoint of this range, reflecting balanced participation from both retail traders and institutional investors.

### Bearish Outlook: Decline Possible

In a bearish scenario, Claude AI predicts XRP could drift toward the $1.50 to $1.80 range. This outcome is likely if ETF demand diminishes and broader macroeconomic pressures escalate. A sustained price drop below the $2.00 level could lead to extended consolidation around the $1.60 support zone. Although network activity on the XRPL might continue, momentum in price action could diminish as market participants await clearer catalysts for movement.
