## Overview

Bitcoin has experienced a halt in its upward momentum as sellers have emerged to suppress the price near the $95,000 mark. This development has established a significant resistance level, coinciding with a key weekly close target for the cryptocurrency.

## Price Action and Resistance

The recent price action surrounding Bitcoin indicates that it has struggled to maintain its gains in the face of selling pressure. The presence of sellers near the $95,000 level has created what is described as a "big boy sell wall." This term refers to a significant concentration of sell orders at that price point, which can impede further upward movement in price.

As Bitcoin approaches this critical resistance level, the implications for traders and investors are noteworthy. The establishment of a sell wall suggests that there is a substantial amount of selling interest at this price, which could deter potential buyers from entering the market. The inability to break through this level may signal a period of consolidation or downward pressure if selling continues.

The current situation highlights the importance of psychological price levels in the cryptocurrency market. Resistance levels, such as the $95,000 mark for Bitcoin, often serve as key indicators for traders. A failure to overcome such a barrier can lead to a reevaluation of market sentiment and trading strategies among participants.
