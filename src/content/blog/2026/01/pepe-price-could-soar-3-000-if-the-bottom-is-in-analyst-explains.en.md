This technical indicator suggests a shift in momentum, as the MACD lines have crossed upward, transitioning from a red to a green signal. This upward movement follows an extended downtrend, coinciding with a stabilization in price, which is typically associated with a rounded bottom pattern.

The analyst noted that many traders and investors may not fully appreciate the implications of such a MACD cross occurring at a true market bottom. Historically, these moments have marked transitions from accumulation phases to sustained uptrends. In previous cycles, similar setups have resulted in substantial price increases for PEPE, with gains of 200% to 300% noted as momentum shifted in favor of buyers.

CryptoLinx has suggested that if PEPE has indeed found its true bottom, the potential for a price rally could be even more explosive than in past cycles. The bullish MACD cross is anticipated to not only facilitate a price recovery but also to initiate a significant surge that could overturn the current downtrend and potentially lead to a new all-time high (ATH).
