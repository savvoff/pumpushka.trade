## Overview

XRP has experienced significant price movement, rising from $0.5 in 2024 to over $3 in 2025. This surge marks a historical moment for the cryptocurrency, bringing it close to its all-time high. A crypto analyst has suggested that XRP may replicate its impressive 2017 rally, potentially leading to a new all-time high in the upcoming months.

## Recent Price Movements

The price of XRP has recently shown explosive growth, reaching levels that have not been seen for some time. After being suppressed for an extended period, the cryptocurrency has made headlines by nearing its previous all-time high. Analysts are closely watching this trend, as it could indicate a significant bullish momentum in the near future. 

A market expert has shared insights regarding XRP's current price structure, comparing it to the 2017 market cycle. In that year, XRP began a powerful breakout when it reached near $3.60, ultimately peaking at around $3.84 in early 2018. The analyst noted that a similar pattern is emerging today, suggesting that XRP could be on the verge of another substantial rally.

## Technical Analysis

The analyst points out that XRP has recently broken out from a clearly defined base, maintaining its position above a rising diagonal support line established during the 2017 cycle. This breakout is significant as it coincides with XRP reclaiming the 52-week Exponential Moving Average (EMA), a critical level that previously signaled the start of strong upward momentum.

Fibonacci Extensions on the current price chart indicate that the 1.618 level is a key target for potential upward movement, consistent with the peak structure observed in 2017. The chart also highlights specific target zones, TP1 and TP2, where XRP paused in 2017 before continuing its upward trajectory. These shallow pullbacks were indicative of strong trend control, suggesting that the current market could exhibit similar behavior.

## Momentum Indicators

XRP is currently trading above $2.2, having gained more than 21% in the past week. Despite the historical slowness in price movements, the bullish forecast presented by the analyst for XRP to reach targets of $23.2 and $136.3 has been met with skepticism among some members of the crypto community. Nonetheless, the analyst points to momentum indicators that support a positive outlook. 

In 2017, XRP's Relative Strength Index (RSI) hit oversold levels before experiencing a significant price surge. A similar pattern is emerging now, reinforcing the belief that the next few months could see parabolic growth for XRP.
