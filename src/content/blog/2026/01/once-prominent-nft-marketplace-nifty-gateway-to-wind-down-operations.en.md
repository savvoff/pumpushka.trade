## Overview

Nifty Gateway, a prominent NFT marketplace owned by Gemini, is set to cease operations on February 23, 2026. The platform will transition into a withdrawal-only mode, marking a significant development in the ongoing challenges faced by the NFT sector.

## Closure Announcement

The decision to wind down operations comes amid a prolonged downturn in the NFT market. This downturn has impacted various platforms and projects, with Nifty Gateway being one of the latest casualties in this evolving landscape. The announcement indicates that the platform will allow users to withdraw their assets, but no new transactions or listings will be permitted as of the closure date.

The NFT market has experienced significant fluctuations over the past few years, with many platforms struggling to maintain user engagement and transaction volumes. Nifty Gateway's closure underscores the difficulties that even established players face in this competitive environment. As the market grapples with these challenges, the decision to enter withdrawal-only mode reflects the realities of sustaining operations amid declining interest and activity in NFTs.
