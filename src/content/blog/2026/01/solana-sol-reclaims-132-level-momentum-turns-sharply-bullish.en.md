## Overview

Solana has recently experienced a notable increase in its price, surpassing the $130 mark and establishing a consolidation phase above $132. This upward momentum is significant as it may indicate a shift in market sentiment towards bullishness for the cryptocurrency.

## Price Movement and Technical Analysis

Solana's price began to rise after it established a position above the $125 level, which coincided with similar movements in major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. Following this, Solana's price climbed above the $130 level, entering what is characterized as a short-term positive zone. The upward trajectory continued as the price broke through the $132 resistance, with bullish activity pushing it above the $135 level.

Currently, Solana is trading above $135 and has crossed the 100-hourly simple moving average, suggesting a favorable technical posture. A bullish trend line is also forming, providing support at the $135 level on the hourly chart of the SOL/USD pair. However, the price is encountering resistance near the $138 mark, with a further significant resistance point located around $140. 

Should Solana manage to surpass the $140 resistance, it may pave the way for additional gains, with the next notable resistance being at $145. A successful close above this level could potentially set the stage for a more sustained upward movement, targeting the $150 mark and beyond.

Conversely, if Solana fails to rise above the $185 resistance, it could trigger a decline. Initial support is positioned near the $134 zone and the trend line, while the first major support level is around $130. Should the price break below this level, it may lead to further declines towards the $128 support zone, with a close below $128 potentially indicating a drop towards the $120 support.
