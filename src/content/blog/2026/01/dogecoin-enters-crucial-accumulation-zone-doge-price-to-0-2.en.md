## Overview

The altcoin market is poised for a significant resurgence as 2026 begins, with Dogecoin, the largest meme coin by market capitalization, experiencing a notable price increase. Recent on-chain data indicates that Dogecoin has entered a vital accumulation zone, suggesting the potential for an upward trend in the coming months.

## Dogecoin's Recent Performance

Dogecoin has seen a substantial price increase of nearly 24% since the beginning of the year. This surge comes after a challenging end to the previous year for the altcoin market. The current price movement indicates that Dogecoin is in a crucial region, which is essential for determining its future trajectory. The implications of this zone are significant, as it may indicate that Dogecoin is just beginning an extended upward trend.

Joao Wedson, the CEO and founder of Alphractal, highlighted in a post that Dogecoin has entered its most important accumulation zone. This observation is based on changes in the Cumulative Value Days Destroyed (CVDD) Channel, an on-chain indicator that tracks the volume of aged capital entering the market. The CVDD metric is particularly useful for identifying long-term support or resistance zones based on the movement of aged coins on the blockchain.

According to Wedson, the CVDD channels are influenced by Fibonacci-based levels overlaying the CVDD curve. This creates historical value zones where the price tends to react significantly. The blue CVDD lines, for instance, have been observed to act as strong structural support for Dogecoin's price. Recent price action shows that Dogecoin's rise to approximately $0.15 followed a bounce from the first blue level, indicating that these lower blue levels have historically served as effective support cushions for the price of Dogecoin.

Wedson further noted that these lower CVDD Fibonacci zones often align with long-term accumulation phases for Dogecoin. As the price is currently situated within these blue CVDD zones, it may be an opportune time for investors to consider accumulating the meme coin.
