## Overview

Rain, a stablecoin platform linked to Visa, has successfully raised $250 million in funding, resulting in a valuation of $1.95 billion. This significant capital injection is anticipated to facilitate the platform's expansion across various continents following substantial growth in its card offerings.

## What Happened

Rain's recent funding round has positioned the company at a valuation of $1.95 billion. The platform has experienced a remarkable 30-fold increase in its card offerings, marking a pivotal moment in its growth trajectory. This surge in card growth is a crucial factor that has attracted investor interest, leading to the successful raise of $250 million.

The funds raised will be utilized to support Rain's ambitious plans for expansion. The platform is set to broaden its reach across multiple continents, indicating a strategic move to tap into new markets and enhance its global presence. This expansion is particularly significant as it aligns with the growing interest in stablecoin solutions within the broader financial ecosystem.

The connection to Visa adds a layer of credibility and potential for collaboration, enhancing Rain's position in the competitive landscape of fintech and cryptocurrency. As major financial institutions continue to explore and integrate digital currencies, Rain's partnership with Visa could play a pivotal role in its future success and adoption.
