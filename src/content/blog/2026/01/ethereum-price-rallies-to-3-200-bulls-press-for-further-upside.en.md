## Overview

Ethereum has recently experienced a notable price increase, surpassing the $3,050 mark and consolidating above $3,100. The cryptocurrency is currently eyeing further gains, with immediate resistance levels identified around $3,200 and beyond. 

## Price Movement and Technical Analysis

Ethereum's price began its upward trajectory after successfully settling above the $3,000 threshold. Following this move, ETH surged past the $3,050 and $3,120 resistance levels, which indicated a bullish sentiment among traders. The price reached a high of $3,218 before entering a consolidation phase. 

As of now, Ethereum is trading above $3,100 and is positioned above the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, which is a key indicator of short-term price trends. A short-term bullish trend line has formed, with support identified at $3,120 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD. This trend line suggests that as long as the price remains above this support level, there is potential for further upward movement.

If Ethereum manages to clear the immediate resistance at $3,200, it could pave the way for further gains. The next key resistance level is observed near $3,220, followed by another significant resistance level at $3,250. A decisive move above the $3,250 level might lead to a rally towards the $3,350 resistance zone. In such a scenario, there is potential for Ethereum to reach the $3,450 or even $3,500 levels in the near future.

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to overcome the $3,220 resistance, it may initiate a downward correction. Initial support is seen around the $3,165 level, which corresponds to the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent price increase from the $3,116 swing low to the $3,218 high. If the price dips below the crucial support at $3,120, it could lead to a further decline towards the $3,050 support level, with additional losses potentially bringing it closer to the $3,000 region.
