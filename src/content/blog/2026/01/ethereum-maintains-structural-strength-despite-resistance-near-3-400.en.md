## Overview

Ethereum is demonstrating notable resilience as it maintains its position above crucial support levels, despite encountering strong resistance near the $3,400 mark. The recent price action indicates a strengthening market structure, suggesting that buyers are still in control. This development is significant as it highlights the potential for continued upside, even amidst short-term challenges.

## Market Resilience and Structural Strength

Ethereum has shown considerable strength in its market structure over recent days. The cryptocurrency has successfully held above the 1D Bull Market Support Band, which has historically served as a reliable reversal zone. This consistent performance indicates growing market confidence and a decrease in immediate downside risk, suggesting that buyers are asserting their influence.

Additionally, Ethereum has reclaimed the 0.618 Fibonacci point of interest around the $3,100 region. This level is critical during corrective phases, and maintaining a position above it typically signals that buyers are gaining control. However, despite these positive developments, Ethereum's price recently encountered resistance near the 0.5 Fibonacci level around $3,400. This rejection was anticipated, as this area has historically been a significant decision point, often resulting in selling pressure and temporary pullbacks.

Looking ahead, the outlook for Ethereum remains constructive as long as it continues to trade above both the 1D Bull Market Support Band and the 0.618 Fibonacci level. Sustaining these support levels would keep the door open for potential upside attempts, even in the face of short-term consolidations.

## Daily Moving Averages and Gradual Advancements

According to analysis from Daan Crypto Trades, Ethereum is gradually advancing while respecting its Daily 200-day moving average against Bitcoin. This slow and methodical progression often signifies underlying strength, suggesting that buyers remain in control even without aggressive momentum. Such prolonged consolidations and steady climbs typically culminate in an acceleration phase, which could trigger renewed interest in the altcoin market.

A breakout in Ethereum's price accompanied by stronger upward momentum could lift overall market sentiment and price action across altcoins. However, the maintenance of the Daily 200MA is critical for preserving this constructive setup. Concurrently, Bitcoin must remain above a certain threshold to uphold the broader low-timeframe bullish structure, ensuring that conditions remain favorable for Ethereum's upward trajectory.
