## Overview

Payroll and HR platform Gusto has initiated a pilot program to implement stablecoin payouts, utilizing the technology from Zerohash. This strategic move is designed to enhance the efficiency of cross-border payments, potentially revolutionizing payroll processes for companies operating internationally.

## What Happened

Gusto, a prominent player in the payroll and human resources sector, is testing a new payment method that incorporates stablecoins. This pilot program is powered by Zerohash, a technology provider that specializes in cryptocurrency transactions. The primary objective of this initiative is to reduce the time required for cross-border payments.

The current landscape for international payroll processing can often involve delays, complex regulations, and high fees associated with traditional banking systems. By leveraging stablecoins, Gusto aims to streamline these processes, making it easier and faster for businesses to pay employees located in different countries. The use of stablecoins, which are designed to maintain a stable value, may offer a more efficient alternative to traditional currencies that can fluctuate significantly.

This move is particularly significant as it aligns with a growing trend among companies seeking to adopt innovative financial technologies. The integration of cryptocurrency into payroll systems reflects a broader shift toward digital solutions that can provide real-time payment capabilities. Gusto's partnership with Zerohash highlights the increasing importance of blockchain technology in the financial services sector, especially in addressing the challenges associated with cross-border transactions.
