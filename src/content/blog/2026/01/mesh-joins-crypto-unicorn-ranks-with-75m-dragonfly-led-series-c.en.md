## Overview

Mesh, a crypto payments company, has successfully secured $75 million in a Series C funding round led by Dragonfly. This significant investment positions Mesh among the ranks of crypto unicorns and sets the stage for its expansion plans across Latin America, Asia, and Europe.

## Funding Details

The recent funding round, which amounted to $75 million, underscores the growing interest and investment in the cryptocurrency sector, particularly in payment solutions. With Dragonfly at the helm of this funding initiative, Mesh can leverage this capital to enhance its operational capabilities and reach new markets.

## Expansion Plans

Following this funding round, Mesh intends to broaden its network extensively. The company’s strategic focus will be on expanding its presence in Latin America, Asia, and Europe. These regions have been identified as key markets for crypto payments, where demand for innovative financial solutions continues to rise. By targeting these areas, Mesh aims to capture a larger share of the market and provide its services to a broader audience.

## Importance of the Investment

The investment from Dragonfly is not just a financial boost; it represents a vote of confidence in Mesh's business model and its potential for growth. The backing from a prominent venture capital firm signifies that the crypto payments sector is gaining traction among investors, who are increasingly recognizing the importance of digital payment solutions in a rapidly evolving financial landscape.
