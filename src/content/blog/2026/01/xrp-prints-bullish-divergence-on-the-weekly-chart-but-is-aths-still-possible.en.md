## Overview

XRP has recently attracted attention as a notable crypto trader highlighted a significant shift in its weekly chart. This shift, marked by a bullish divergence in momentum indicators, has reignited discussions about the potential for XRP to approach its previous all-time highs, despite the asset's recent price stability.

## XRP's Technical Analysis

After a prolonged period of compressed price action, XRP is currently situated within a defined weekly price range that has persisted since the peak of the 2018 cycle. This range spans from the low-$2 area to the low-$3 region and has acted as a structural equilibrium, allowing XRP to navigate through various market phases. Since late 2024, XRP has consistently tested this range's support and resistance levels without achieving a definitive breakout or breakdown.

The recent analysis provided by the trader points out a crucial difference in the current market dynamics compared to previous attempts at breaking out. Specifically, momentum indicators are forming higher lows, even as the price revisits established support levels. This behavior indicates that the selling pressure is diminishing, suggesting a weakening of distribution among sellers. The bullish divergence observed implies that sellers are exerting more effort for lesser downside results, reinforcing the notion that the market may be transitioning toward a more stable structure.

The chart shared by the trader illustrates this point, showing XRP's price maintaining its position above range support while the underlying momentum trends upward. This consolidation phase is interpreted as a sign of absorption rather than weakness, where short-term traders are gradually being replaced by long-term holders. This shift is viewed as a positive development for market stability.

While the presence of a bullish divergence does not guarantee a return to XRP's all-time highs, it does reintroduce the possibility in a manner that is technically credible. A sustained breakout above the upper boundary of the multi-year range would serve as a crucial confirmation of this potential. Until such a breakout occurs, the prospect of reaching previous all-time highs remains conditional. However, the emerging divergence indicates that the groundwork for a significant move may be in the process of formation.
