## Overview

Bitwise Asset Management has taken a significant step by registering the Uniswap ETF Trust. This move comes on the heels of regulators concluding their investigation into Uniswap Labs, transitioning the focus from enforcement actions to discussions surrounding liquidity in the decentralized finance (DeFi) space.

## What Happened

Bitwise Asset Management has formally registered the Uniswap ETF Trust, marking a noteworthy development in the cryptocurrency investment landscape. The registration signals an early step towards a potential filing for a cryptocurrency exchange-traded fund (ETF) centered around Uniswap, a leading decentralized exchange (DEX) known for its automated liquidity provision.

This registration occurs in the context of a recent shift in regulatory focus. Authorities had previously been scrutinizing Uniswap Labs, the organization behind the Uniswap protocol. However, the conclusion of this investigation has led to a pivot in attention from enforcement matters to more pressing questions about liquidity within the DeFi ecosystem.

The registration of the Uniswap ETF Trust by Bitwise suggests a growing interest in creating investment vehicles that provide exposure to decentralized exchanges. ETFs have gained popularity in traditional finance for their ability to allow investors to gain exposure to a basket of assets without directly purchasing individual tokens or cryptocurrencies. By registering this specific trust, Bitwise is positioning itself to potentially capitalize on the evolving regulatory landscape and the increasing demand for crypto-related investment products.
