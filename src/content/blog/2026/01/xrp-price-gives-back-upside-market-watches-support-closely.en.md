## Overview

XRP's price has faced a significant decline after failing to maintain momentum above key resistance levels. The recent price action has left XRP trading below crucial support zones, raising concerns among traders and market watchers about its future trajectory.

## Recent Price Movement

XRP price initially struggled to gain traction above $2.320, ultimately leading to a decline below $2.250. The cryptocurrency is currently facing resistance near the $2.20 level, indicating a bearish sentiment in the market. The price broke below a significant bullish trend line that had support at $2.245 on the hourly chart, which is a critical technical indicator for traders. This decline has pushed the price to trade below the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, suggesting a weakening market position.

As XRP continued its downward movement, it dipped below the $2.150 mark and formed a low at $2.141. This price action is notable as it indicates the potential for further bearish trends. Currently, XRP is showing bearish signs below the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level, calculated from the recent swing high of $2.416 down to the low of $2.141. 

If XRP manages to initiate a fresh upward move, traders should be cautious as resistance remains significant near the $2.20 level. The first major resistance level is at $2.250, and a close above this could indicate a potential recovery toward $2.30. However, the hurdles do not end there, as the next resistance levels are positioned at $2.320 and the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward movement.

## Possible Future Movements

The outlook for XRP remains cautious. Should the price fail to break through the $2.250 resistance zone, it may initiate another decline. Initial support on the downside is identified at the $2.150 level, with the next major support at $2.120. A critical threshold exists at $2.120; a break and close below this level could lead to a further decline toward $2.050. Should XRP fall below $2.050, the next significant support lies around the $2.00 zone, which, if breached, could propel the price down toward $1.880.
