## Overview

Ethereum (ETH) has begun the year with a recovery above the critical $3,000 mark amid a broader rally in the cryptocurrency market. However, it faces challenges at a significant resistance level of $3,400, entering a consolidation phase. Technical analyst Ali Martinez has highlighted potential on-chain indicators that could signal a bullish trend for Ethereum.

## Current Market Situation

As Ethereum approaches the new year, it has managed to surpass the $3,000 threshold, indicating a recovery phase. Despite this positive movement, the cryptocurrency is currently struggling to break through the key resistance level of $3,400. In recent weeks, Ethereum's price action has entered a consolidation phase, suggesting indecision among traders as they evaluate the next steps for the asset.

Ali Martinez, a technical analyst, has pointed out that the recent buying momentum could lead to a new rally for Ethereum if it continues. He notes that on-chain indicators are showing signs of a bullish sentiment among investors. A significant factor contributing to this sentiment is the surge in daily active addresses on the Ethereum network, which have reportedly doubled to exceed 800,000 within a two-week timeframe.

Additionally, the demand for Ethereum exchange-traded funds (ETFs) has been rising. Since late December, these investment vehicles have accumulated a substantial amount of ETH, contributing to a positive outlook for the altcoin. This increase in on-chain activity has established strong support levels for Ethereum's price, particularly between $2,772 and $3,109. These levels are crucial as they may prevent a significant drop below this range.

Martinez believes that maintaining these support levels, combined with sustained buying pressure, could facilitate a breakout above the $3,400 resistance. Such a movement could potentially set the stage for Ethereum to approach the $4,000 mark, which represents a notable increase from its current trading level.

## From author

The current dynamics surrounding Ethereum highlight the importance of on-chain metrics and investor sentiment in determining price movements in the cryptocurrency market. The doubling of active addresses suggests increased interest and participation among users, which could be a pivotal factor in driving future price action. Furthermore, the interplay between institutional investment through ETFs and retail investor sentiment could shape the trajectory of Ethereum in the coming weeks.
