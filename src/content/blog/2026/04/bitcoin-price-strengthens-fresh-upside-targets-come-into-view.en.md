## Overview

Bitcoin has initiated a new upward movement, successfully surpassing the $78,000 mark. The cryptocurrency is currently consolidating its gains and may target further increases above the $78,550 resistance level. This price action is significant as it indicates a potential for continued bullish momentum in the market.

## Bitcoin's Recent Price Movement

Bitcoin's recent performance has shown a positive trend, as it began to rise from a support level near $75,000. The price managed to break through several resistance levels, including $76,500 and $77,500, eventually reaching a high of $79,490. Following this peak, Bitcoin experienced a minor correction, dropping below the $78,000 level. However, it found support above $77,000, which has been a critical level for the upward movement.

Currently, Bitcoin is trading above $78,000 and the 100 hourly simple moving average, indicating a bullish trend. A bullish trend line is also forming with support at $78,000 on the hourly chart of the BTC/USD pair. The pair has the potential to extend its gains if it maintains stability above the key levels of $77,200 and $77,000.

The immediate resistance for Bitcoin is positioned at $78,550, with a significant resistance level at $79,000. A close above this resistance could pave the way for further increases, potentially testing the $79,500 level and, if the bullish momentum continues, reaching the $80,000 mark. The subsequent barrier for upward movement could be around $82,000.

However, if Bitcoin fails to break through the $78,550 resistance zone, there is a risk of another decline. Immediate support is noted around the $78,000 level, with major support at $77,000, which corresponds to the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent upward move. Additional support is located at the $76,250 zone, and further losses could lead to a drop towards the $75,500 support level. The primary support remains at $75,000, below which Bitcoin may struggle to recover in the near term.
