2. **Multi-Agent Flash Crashes**: This attack category focuses on the coordinated actions of multiple AI agents that could result in rapid and catastrophic failures. By orchestrating these agents to act in unison, hackers could exploit systemic vulnerabilities in the AI’s operational framework.
