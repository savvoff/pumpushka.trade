## Shiba Inu's Current Price Setup

The Shiba Inu token is navigating a critical price range that could lead to notable upward movement. An analyst has indicated that SHIB is situated within what he identifies as the “Support Zone (Accumulation Zone 1).” This zone has historically been a launching pad for substantial price increases. Notably, in 2021, when the token was last in this zone, it experienced a rally of 1,660%. A subsequent rally occurred in 2024, where the token rose by 746%. 

Currently, Shiba Inu is trading at approximately $0.000006, which is above a key support level at $0.000004. Should this support hold and buying momentum increase, the analyst projects a potential increase to around $0.00008789, which would represent a gain of approximately 1,364% from its current trading price. 

## Technical Analysis and Market Sentiment

In addition to the accumulation zone, the current price action of Shiba Inu is characterized by a tightening chart pattern. A descending resistance line has been applying pressure on SHIB’s price, leading to a narrowing trading range. The analyst suggests that this compression is nearing an end, and typically, such patterns resolve with significant price movement in one direction. However, the uncertainty lies in determining which direction that movement will take.

On-chain data provides additional context. Recent reports indicate that Shiba Inu's exchange netflow has turned negative, with a net outflow of 41.67 billion tokens. This trend suggests that more tokens are being moved to personal wallets, indicating accumulation among holders rather than selling activity. Despite this, a substantial amount—over 81 trillion SHIB tokens—remains on exchanges, overshadowing the recent outflow.

However, not all analysts share the same bullish outlook. Reports indicate that Shiba Inu remains trapped in a pattern of lower highs, with resistance levels forming between $0.0000073 and $0.0000079. A drop below the current support could potentially lead the price down toward $0.0000051, indicating a bearish sentiment in longer timeframes.
