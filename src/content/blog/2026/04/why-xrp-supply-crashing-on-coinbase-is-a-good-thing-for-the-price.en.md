## Overview

Recent reports indicate a significant decline in the supply of XRP on Coinbase, reaching historical lows. This development is tied to a perceived boycott by XRP investors and community members in response to Coinbase's stance on the CLARITY Act. As a result, the surge in withdrawals may lead to a supply crunch, potentially affecting XRP's market dynamics.

## XRP Supply Falls to Historic Lows on Coinbase

The XRP reserves on Coinbase have experienced a notable drop, as reported by XRP advocate Diana. The exchange's balance reportedly fell to approximately 101.86 million XRP due to a wave of withdrawals by holders. Estimates suggest that the supply on Coinbase has decreased by nearly 90% over a few months, marking a record low for the platform.

The boycott appears to be a reaction to Coinbase's pushback against the CLARITY Act, which has garnered widespread frustration among XRP holders. Coinbase has expressed "significant concerns" regarding the latest Senate compromise, particularly the phrasing that aims to prohibit passive yield on stablecoins. This concern is particularly relevant given that in 2025, Coinbase and its partner Circle generated around $2.75 billion in gross interest income from USDC reserves, with Coinbase estimated to have received about $1.35 billion from that total. Many in the XRP community suspect that Coinbase's opposition to the revised bill is motivated not by a desire to protect crypto users, but rather to safeguard one of its key revenue streams.

Additionally, anger within the XRP community has been fueled by leaked claims suggesting that Coinbase requested millions from Ripple to list XRP back in 2019. This perceived unfairness has further galvanized XRP holders, leading to significant net outflows from Coinbase, ranging from 21 million to 95 million XRP over the past 30 days. This indicates that holders are opting to move their tokens to self-custody solutions or other exchanges. If this trend persists, Coinbase could soon find itself with one of the lowest XRP reserves in recent years.

## From author

The recent developments surrounding XRP's supply on Coinbase reflect a broader narrative of community sentiment and market dynamics. As the community navigates its frustrations with Coinbase, the actions taken by XRP holders could set the stage for potential shifts in the market landscape.

## Impact on the crypto market

- A drastic reduction in XRP's supply on a major exchange like Coinbase could lead to a supply shock.
- If demand for XRP increases while the supply remains constrained, it may result in upward price pressure.
- The ongoing withdrawals indicate a robust sentiment among the XRP community, emphasizing their willingness to protest perceived injustices.
- Should the trend of net outflows continue, Coinbase may face challenges in maintaining liquidity for XRP trading.
- The situation underscores the potential for market dynamics to shift based on community actions and regulatory developments.
