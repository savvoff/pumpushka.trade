## Overview

Solana (SOL) is currently facing significant challenges after encountering a sharp rejection at the $92–$94 supply zone, which has halted its recent attempts to move higher. As momentum wanes, SOL's price is slipping back toward key support levels, indicating a growing risk of a deeper breakdown.

## Recent Price Action

The rejection at the $92–$94 supply zone has raised warning signs for Solana, leading to a noticeable decline in momentum. As the price retreats, SOL finds itself between a weakening structure and critical support levels below, suggesting that the upcoming price movement will be crucial for determining its short-term trajectory.

According to analyst Ali Martinez, Solana is currently trapped within a well-defined consolidation channel. The price action has been compressing following months of sustained pressure, and SOL has now drifted closer to the lower boundary of this channel. The next 48 hours are viewed as pivotal for establishing the broader trend for the remainder of April.

The structure of the current channel is clearly defined, with resistance identified at $96.04 and support at $76.66. At present, the price hovers around $79.11. Trading near support levels often indicates a critical moment, where buyers may step in to defend the level, or sellers may take control, leading to a potential breakdown.

If the support at $76.66 holds, there is a possibility for a classic double bottom or channel bounce scenario to materialize. Such a development could trigger a relief rally, with potential upside targets at $81.00 and $85.00. The 50-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) in this area may act as a crucial resistance zone that could impede momentum.

Conversely, if SOL closes decisively below $76.66, it would invalidate the current channel structure and confirm bearish pressure. In this scenario, downside targets would shift to the year-to-date low and potentially the psychological $50 level.

## Accumulation Phase

In a recent update shared on X, analyst R4 XBT noted that Solana remains firmly within an accumulation phase. Despite fluctuations in the broader market, SOL's price action is being supported at the 50-day Moving Average (MA50). This level serves as a critical foundation for the current price structure, helping maintain the long-term bullish outlook while the market consolidates.

The positioning at the MA50 represents a significant technical test for Solana. Market participants are closely monitoring this zone to assess whether the current accumulation has enough strength to facilitate a successful breakout. If Solana can clear the MA50 resistance, it could signal the end of the current consolidation phase and pave the way for more substantial upside potential.

Traders are actively seeking a decisive close above the MA50 to confirm that a sustained rally may be on the horizon.

The current situation for Solana illustrates the delicate balance between support and resistance levels, with the price action closely tied to broader market dynamics. The upcoming days are crucial for SOL as it navigates these technical challenges, and traders will be keenly observing how the asset reacts to its current accumulation phase.
