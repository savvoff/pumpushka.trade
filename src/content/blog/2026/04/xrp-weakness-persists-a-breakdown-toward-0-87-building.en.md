## Overview

XRP is currently experiencing notable weakness as bearish pressure mounts beneath the surface. Despite occasional relief bounces, the market remains firmly under seller control, with key resistance levels holding firm. The outlook suggests a potential move toward the $0.87 support level, heightening market caution.

## XRP's Current Performance

XRP has been showing persistent signs of weakness, according to crypto analyst CasiTrades. The price action indicates that a significant downward move is anticipated. The prevailing sentiment among market participants is one of caution as there is an expectation that XRP will eventually test established support levels. 

While the descent has been gradual, it has been described as frustrating for those monitoring the charts. The current trajectory remains pointed downward, indicating that the final target for this movement has not yet been reached. Each attempted bounce has been met with extreme weakness, with relief moves consistently stalling around a specific Fibonacci retracement level. This pattern serves as a technical indicator that sellers continue to maintain control over the market.

On a recent Monday, selling momentum increased within a one-hour period, suggesting that the slow market pace may not continue for much longer. As bearish influence reasserts itself, the market appears poised for a more volatile push toward lower support zones, as highlighted in the analysis.

## Key Resistance Levels

CasiTrades notes that XRP is currently trying to stabilize around the $1.31 level, which is identified as a crucial resistance area. This zone is characterized as the Wave 4 extreme within a broader market structure. The ongoing hesitation and lack of strong follow-through at this resistance level are not unexpected, as price typically struggles when testing significant resistance after a corrective move. 

The analyst points out that if the $1.31 resistance is broken, the downside could accelerate quickly. CasiTrades is tracking a developing Wave 3 move to the downside, with a primary target around $1.09 and possible subwave extensions that could reach as low as $1.06. Following this anticipated decline, a temporary Wave 4 relief bounce is expected, which could see prices retracing back into the $1.22 to $1.31 range before facing renewed resistance. Ultimately, the broader trend appears set to continue downward toward the $0.87 macro support zone.
