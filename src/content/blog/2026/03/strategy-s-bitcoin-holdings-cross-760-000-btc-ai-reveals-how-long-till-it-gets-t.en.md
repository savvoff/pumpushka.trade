## Overview

Strategy, formerly known as MicroStrategy, has recently surpassed the significant milestone of 760,000 Bitcoin holdings, reaching a total of 761,068 BTC. This development comes amid ongoing market volatility and downtrends, as the company continues its aggressive accumulation strategy led by its CEO.

## Recent Developments

As of March 16, 2026, Strategy's Bitcoin holdings have increased following a substantial purchase of over 22,000 BTC for approximately $1.57 billion. This latest acquisition highlights the company's commitment to expanding its Bitcoin treasury, even in a challenging market environment. Analysts have begun to analyze how long it might take for Strategy to reach the ambitious target of one million BTC.

Grok AI, an analytical platform created by notable figures in technology, has provided some insights into the potential timeline for achieving this goal. Based on the company's recent purchasing trends, Grok predicts that if Strategy maintains its current acquisition pace, it could reach the one million BTC milestone as early as September 2026. The analysis reveals that in the weeks leading up to mid-March, the company acquired between 3,015 and 22,337 BTC weekly, averaging around 14,450 BTC.

However, sustaining such an aggressive buying rate poses challenges. Grok notes that to maintain this momentum, Strategy would need to secure over $1 billion in capital each week, a requirement deemed unlikely. Currently, the company must acquire an additional 238,932 BTC to meet its objective.

A more conservative approach, considering historical averages of approximately 2,500 BTC per week through its preferred stock funding program, suggests a more realistic timeline for reaching the one million BTC mark around September 2026. This assessment factors in critical elements such as market liquidity, sustainable fundraising, dilution concerns, and market volatility.

In addition to Grok’s analysis, ChatGPT AI offers a different perspective based on historical buying patterns and market conditions. According to its forecasts, Strategy would need to acquire about 5,550 BTC weekly to achieve the one million BTC target by December 2026. This projection indicates a significant increase, approximately 50-100% above recent weekly averages. While this goal remains ambitious, the AI suggests that it could still be achievable by 2027, especially if Strategy ramps up its purchasing through equity issuance and funding initiatives.
