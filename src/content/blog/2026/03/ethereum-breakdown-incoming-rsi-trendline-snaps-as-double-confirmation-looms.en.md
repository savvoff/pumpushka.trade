## Overview

Ethereum is exhibiting early warning signs of potential weakness as momentum shifts in the market. A recent analysis indicates that the RSI trendline for the ETH/USDT pair has broken, suggesting a weakening strength, while the ETH/BTC pair is at risk of following suit. This situation raises concerns about a possible breakdown that could lead to a significant downward movement in Ethereum's price.

## Ethereum's Current Situation

According to an analysis from Umair Crypto, the RSI trendline for the Ethereum/USDT pair has already broken, indicating an initial change in momentum. The ETH/BTC pair is anticipated to experience a similar break shortly, with expectations that a new lower low is imminent. This pattern of behavior mirrors a recent occurrence with Solana, where the USDT pair's RSI trendline fractured first, while the BTC pair initially showed resilience. Ethereum's current behavior reflects this same sequence, suggesting a potential recursive breakdown.

While the ETH/BTC pair is currently maintaining its levels, the analysis indicates that this stability may be temporary. Once the BTC pair loses its strength, the absence of support across both trading pairs could trigger a sharp movement to the downside. This alignment is viewed as a high-risk scenario for Ethereum, representing one of the more volatile breakdowns that could occur in the market.

## Resilience Under Pressure

Despite the apparent signs of weakness, both Bitcoin and Ethereum have shown considerable strength amid broader macroeconomic challenges. This resilience, however, has not come without consequences. Instead of forming a solid base, the market appears to have been operating on borrowed time. The fatigue reflected in the charts suggests that the cost of maintaining this strength is beginning to manifest, making a move toward a lower low a plausible outcome.

Adding to the tension, a significant catalyst is on the horizon: over $2.1 billion in Bitcoin and Ethereum options are set to expire, coinciding with Wall Street's $5.7 trillion Triple Witching event. While large expiries do not directly dictate market direction, they often amplify existing momentum. In this instance, the underlying market structure points toward a downward trend, implying that any movement could be intensified under these conditions.

The breakdown sequence for Ethereum is becoming increasingly evident. The USDT pair was the first to exhibit weakness, losing its key structural support and signaling a shift in momentum. Now, the focus is on obtaining confirmation from the ETH/BTC pair. Historically, when such alignments occur, they tend to lead to more decisive and aggressive downward movements as bearish pressure takes control.
