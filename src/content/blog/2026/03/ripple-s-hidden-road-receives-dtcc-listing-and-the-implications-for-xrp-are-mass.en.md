## Overview

Ripple has made a significant advancement in its integration with traditional finance through its institutional brokerage platform, Hidden Road. Recently, Hidden Road was officially listed in the National Securities Clearing Corporation (NSCC) directory under the Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation (DTCC). This development is seen as a crucial step for Ripple and its cryptocurrency, XRP, as it positions the company within the infrastructure of conventional financial markets.

## Ripple's Strategic Move

On March 2, 2026, the Depository Trust & Clearing Corporation, a key player in the US securities market, added Hidden Road Partners CIV US LLC to its NSCC Market Participant Identifiers directory. This listing is noteworthy as it grants Hidden Road, now known as Ripple Prime following Ripple's acquisition, direct operational status within the post-trade workflows utilized by major financial institutions.

Ripple's acquisition of Hidden Road was a landmark decision in the crypto industry, marking one of the most ambitious moves by a cryptocurrency company. Prior to the acquisition, Hidden Road was already a prominent global prime brokerage, processing financing trades for over 300 institutional clients and handling substantial annual trade volumes. The NSCC listing empowers Ripple Prime to process over-the-counter trades through the NSCC's centralized clearing system, a capability that no other crypto company has achieved to date.

Ripple’s former Chief Technology Officer, David Schwartz, acknowledged the significance of this development on social media, indicating that it was a pivotal moment for the company. He noted that the update had been in progress even before the finalization of Ripple’s acquisition of Hidden Road.

## XRP Community Reactions

The XRP community has responded to this development with a blend of analytical insight and enthusiasm. Many holders view the DTCC listing as a clear indication that Ripple is not merely aiming for mainstream finance but is actively embedding itself within it. This sentiment was amplified by Schwartz's acknowledgment of the milestone on social media. 

Community members have expressed optimism that Ripple Prime's operations may eventually incorporate aspects of its post-trade processes onto the XRP Ledger. The expectation is that even a fraction of the institutional volume processed through Ripple Prime could lead to increased blockchain activity associated with Ripple's ecosystem.
