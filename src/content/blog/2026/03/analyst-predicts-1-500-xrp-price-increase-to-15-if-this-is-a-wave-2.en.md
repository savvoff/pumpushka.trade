## Overview

A recent analysis suggests that XRP could be poised for significant movement, hinging on its current position within an Elliott Wave cycle. The analyst, HovWaves, indicates that the potential for XRP to reach a price target between $15 and $20 remains unchanged, despite recent price behavior that may not align with explosive growth.

## Analysis of XRP's Current Situation

The analysis from HovWaves highlights a pivotal moment for XRP, suggesting that the asset may be at a critical juncture in its price cycle. According to his Elliott Wave chart, the macro primary expectation for XRP has not altered, with a continued focus on the $15 to $20 price target. HovWaves acknowledges that while the asset's price action this year has not shown strong indicators of an imminent surge into double digits, bullish sentiment persists among proponents based on both technical and fundamental analyses.

A key element of HovWaves’ prediction is the identification of the current market structure as potentially representing either a smaller-degree pullback or a deeper corrective phase. He notes that distinguishing between a Wave 2 and a Wave 4 correction is crucial, as these phases can appear similar in real-time but suggest different future price trajectories. If XRP is indeed in a Wave 2, the implications for potential price targets become significantly more optimistic, indicating that a move towards the $15 to $20 range could be feasible.

HovWaves’ analysis is supported by a broader Elliott Wave count that dates back to 2013. This historical perspective shows a completed five-wave impulse structure leading up to XRP’s peak in 2018, followed by a prolonged corrective phase that concluded in 2020. The current wave structure, according to the analysis, showcases a five-wave advance that began from the 2020 low, with Waves 1 and 2 appearing to be complete. The third wave culminated in an all-time high, and XRP is now in a Wave 4 consolidation phase. This phase is characterized by a downtrend and intermittent volatility, setting the stage for a potential final fifth wave that could propel XRP to the anticipated price range.
