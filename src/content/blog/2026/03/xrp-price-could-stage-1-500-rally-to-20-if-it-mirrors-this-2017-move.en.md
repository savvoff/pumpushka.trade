## Overview

XRP is currently at a critical juncture that could determine the trajectory of its market cycle. The cryptocurrency's price is hovering around $1.40, and new technical analyses suggest that the current market behavior may not indicate an impending collapse. Instead, it might reflect a familiar pattern reminiscent of the price movements observed before XRP's significant rally in 2017.

## A Familiar Breakout Structure for XRP

Technical analysis shared on the social media platform X reveals insights from a crypto analyst who compares XRP's current market structure to that of its 2017 rally. This analysis highlights that XRP is forming a symmetrical triangle-like consolidation pattern, similar to the one seen before its previous major price increase. 

In examining XRP’s historical price movements, the analyst notes that the cryptocurrency spent nearly three years from 2014 to 2016 developing a descending triangle. This structure was characterized by a series of lower highs that pressed against a stable or declining support level. During this period, XRP encountered multiple resistance points, resulting in lower highs that led many traders to perceive it as a declining asset. 

However, a pivotal moment occurred in late 2016 when XRP briefly dipped below the triangle's lower boundary, creating what is known as a false breakdown. This was followed by a dramatic rebound that resulted in a breakout from the entire structure, culminating in a remarkable rally of over 2,000% that propelled XRP to new all-time highs.

Currently, XRP appears to be in a similar phase. The cryptocurrency has been coiling between descending resistance and rising support from 2018 to 2024, and it printed another false breakdown near the end of this formation. The analyst suggests that this behavior indicates XRP may be poised for an upward breakout in the near future.

## The Potential for a Rally Above $20

According to the technical analysis, XRP's recent price retreat back to the $1.30s and $1.40 may be a temporary consolidation phase, similar to the brief cooling period observed after the 2017 breakout. The analyst posits that this current pullback is just a precursor to a significant upward movement that could push XRP well above the $20 mark. 

While the $20 target is highlighted, the analysis also suggests that there could be a much larger upward movement based on the historical patterns, with projections indicating a peak that may exceed $90, mirroring the heights reached during the 2017 rally.
