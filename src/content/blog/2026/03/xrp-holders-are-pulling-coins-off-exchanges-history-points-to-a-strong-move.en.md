## Overview

XRP is currently facing challenges at a price point of $1.35, amid expectations of a volatile market week. However, recent data from Binance reveals a noteworthy trend: the scarcity of XRP is increasing, indicating a potential shift in market dynamics despite the current bearish sentiment.

## Current Market Dynamics

XRP's price struggles at $1.35, as indicated by the recent market analysis. The data from an Arab Chain report highlights an important development in the supply dynamics of XRP on Binance. The scarcity indicator has reached a level of 0.59, marking its highest point since 2024. This figure is significant as it demonstrates that the supply of XRP available for immediate sale on the exchange is decreasing. 

Investors are actively withdrawing their XRP holdings from exchanges, opting to transfer them to private wallets. This behavior suggests a long-term investment strategy, as these participants are locking their assets away and reducing the liquidity available for immediate selling. Historically, this scarcity indicator had been in negative territory for an extended period, reflecting high selling pressure and significant exchange inflows. The recent positive shift towards a multi-year high indicates a reversal in market behavior, where sellers are pulling back, and long-term holders are stepping in.

## Accumulation Phase

The report emphasizes that the rise in the scarcity indicator is not merely a supply metric; it also represents a change in market participant behavior. The data illustrates that short-term sellers, who previously dominated the market, are being replaced by long-term holders who are accumulating XRP and withdrawing it from exchanges. This transition signifies the onset of an accumulation phase, a term used in market structure analysis to describe a period where buyers are gradually increasing their positions.

As short-term selling pressure diminishes, investor confidence appears to be rising among those withdrawing their assets. The market dynamics are shifting, potentially favoring buyers. However, the report notes that the accumulation thesis is contingent upon two key conditions: continued improvement in overall market sentiment and ongoing contraction of exchange supply. If these conditions are met, the groundwork for a more robust price movement may be established.

## Chart Analysis

As of the latest data, XRP is trading at approximately $1.3510, reflecting a modest daily gain of 1.75%. Despite this increase, the overall trend remains concerning, as XRP has been on a downward trajectory since peaking near $3.90 in late July 2025. The price has formed a descending staircase pattern, characterized by lower highs and significant selling pressure at each rally attempt.

Recent technical indicators reveal structural challenges. The 50-day moving average has crossed below the 100-day moving average, signaling a death cross on the intermediate timeframe. Both moving averages are trending lower, with the 200-day moving average positioned significantly above the current price level. For XRP to suggest a potential recovery, it would need to close above $1.45, indicating the formation of a base rather than a continuation of the downward trend.

## From author

The current situation surrounding XRP is a critical one, characterized by a blend of increasing scarcity and persistent selling pressure. The data suggests that while long-term holders are accumulating, the overall market sentiment remains fragile. The coming days will be pivotal in determining whether buyers are ready to step forward and support a price recovery or if the current bearish trend will continue.
