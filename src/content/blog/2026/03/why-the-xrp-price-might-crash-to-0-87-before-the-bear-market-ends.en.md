## Overview

XRP has been on a downward trend for several months, experiencing a significant drop from a yearly high to a level below $1.4. The current bearish sentiment in the crypto market raises questions about XRP's future movements, with analysts presenting varying forecasts. Notably, crypto analyst CasiTrades has outlined a potential scenario where XRP could decline to $0.87 before any meaningful recovery occurs.

## XRP's Current Price Action

As of the latest analysis, XRP is trading around $1.39, having spent 34 days within Wave 4 of an Elliott Wave structure. This phase is characterized by slow price movements and reduced volatility, indicating a prolonged consolidation period that can frustrate both bullish and bearish traders. The price has been relatively stagnant since early February, remaining trapped in a corrective structure that has not shown clear signs of direction.

CasiTrades emphasizes two critical price levels that will likely influence XRP's next move. The first of these is the $0.87 level, which aligns with the 0.854 Fibonacci retracement. If XRP reaches this level, it is expected to act as a strong support area, potentially marking the end of its ongoing correction and the beginning of a recovery phase.

The second key level identified is the resistance around $1.65, which corresponds to the 0.618 Fibonacci extension. If XRP can break above this resistance and hold it as support, it would invalidate the bearish outlook and indicate a shift back to bullish momentum.

## Potential Recovery After Wave 4

Should XRP follow the bearish scenario presented, CasiTrades suggests that once Wave 4 concludes near the $0.87 support zone, the market could transition into Wave 5. This phase is anticipated to be a strong recovery period. The analysis indicates that following a potential rebound from $0.87, XRP could move towards the next intermediate zone near the 0.786 Fibonacci retracement level around $1.085. 

From this point, the price may revisit its previous resistance area near $1.65, with further potential to climb to a second resistance level around $1.78. If XRP manages to break above this level with sufficient bullish momentum, it could lead to a significant price surge.
