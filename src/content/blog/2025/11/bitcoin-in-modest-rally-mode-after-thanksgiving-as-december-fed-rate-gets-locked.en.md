## Overview

Following the Thanksgiving holiday, Bitcoin has entered a phase of modest rallying. This uptick has been accompanied by a rise in crypto-related stocks, particularly those of bitcoin miners.

## What Happened

In the wake of Thanksgiving, there has been a noticeable increase in the price of Bitcoin, suggesting a positive momentum in the market. This rally is significant as it reflects broader trends in the crypto market, with many crypto-related stocks experiencing gains. Notably, bitcoin miners have seen the most substantial increases among these stocks, indicating a strong performance in that sector.

## Why It Matters

The performance of bitcoin miners can be indicative of the overall health of the cryptocurrency market. As these companies benefit from rising Bitcoin prices, their stock prices tend to reflect this increase, which can attract more investors to the crypto space. The current rally may also signal a shift in market sentiment as investors look toward the implications of upcoming economic factors, such as Federal Reserve decisions.
