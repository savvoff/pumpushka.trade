2. **Alternative Scenario**: This scenario carries a lower probability and posits that the current price structure could remain corrective. Historically, SUI has demonstrated a tendency to rally within a few weeks after both TVL and DAU metrics begin to trend upward.

BeLaunch emphasized the robust nature of the current technical and on-chain setup. Stabilizing fundamentals, shifting momentum indicators, and specific signals suggest that SUI may be on the verge of emerging from its current consolidation phase into a new bullish trend.
