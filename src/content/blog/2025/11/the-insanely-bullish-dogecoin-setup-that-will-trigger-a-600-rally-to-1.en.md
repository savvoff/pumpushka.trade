## Overview

Dogecoin has recently faced challenges in regaining its momentum, experiencing pullbacks that have brought its price down to the mid-$0.16 range. Despite this bearish trend, a technical analysis suggests that the cryptocurrency may be forming a long-term bullish setup.

## Current Price Action

Over the past few days, Dogecoin has struggled to maintain its upward trajectory, largely due to a series of pullbacks. The broader market conditions have also contributed to this instability, exerting additional pressure on Dogecoin's price. However, a closer examination of Dogecoin's higher-timeframe chart indicates that the recent decline has not invalidated a crucial price structure.

### Technical Analysis Insights

A technical analysis by XForceGlobal highlights that Dogecoin is within an “insanely bullish” long-term formation. The analysis focuses on the Elliott Wave theory, suggesting that Dogecoin is approaching the final stages of this formation. According to the analysis, Dogecoin's price action is part of a developing fifth impulse wave, which is critical for its potential future growth.

The chart shared by XForceGlobal illustrates an idealized Elliott Wave cycle that spans nearly a decade, indicating that Dogecoin has already completed the first four major waves. The fifth wave is believed to have technically begun months ago, although recent pullbacks raise the possibility that the fourth wave may still be in progress. Despite this uncertainty, the analysis emphasizes that the fourth wave low remains protected, and the current price action is still aligned with the fifth wave.

### Price Structure and Projections

The analysis further reveals that Dogecoin has been consolidating within a narrowing structure, with consistent support at recent lows. Regardless of the ongoing price fluctuations, both scenarios depicted in the analysis suggest that Dogecoin is poised for a new all-time high once the rally resumes.

XForceGlobal's projections indicate that cycle targets remain above $1, supported by the structural geometry of the fifth wave. This pattern mirrors formations observed during Dogecoin's significant price expansions in previous bull cycles, specifically in 2017 and 2021. 

However, it is noted that there is still potential for further distribution before a definitive upward movement occurs. The current resistance levels are situated below the anticipated fifth-wave target zone, and the broader market structure has not breached the critical wave-4 levels necessary for the bullish setup to remain valid.
