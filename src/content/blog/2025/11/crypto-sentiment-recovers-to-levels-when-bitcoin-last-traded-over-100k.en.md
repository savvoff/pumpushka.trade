## Overview

Crypto sentiment is showing signs of recovery as Bitcoin has recently surpassed the $90,000 mark. This improvement in sentiment comes despite Bitcoin still being under the $100,000 threshold.

## What Happened

Bitcoin's recent climb above $90,000 has positively influenced the overall sentiment in the cryptocurrency market. This shift in sentiment is noteworthy, especially considering Bitcoin has not yet reached the $100,000 level. The increase in sentiment indicates a growing optimism among market participants regarding the future of Bitcoin and potentially the broader cryptocurrency market.

## Why It Matters

The recovery of crypto sentiment can have several implications for the market. As Bitcoin approaches significant psychological levels, such as $100,000, the sentiment surrounding it can drive investor behavior and influence trading decisions. Positive sentiment can lead to increased trading activity and may attract new investors to the market.

- Improved sentiment could lead to increased buying pressure on Bitcoin.
- A rise in optimism may encourage investment in other cryptocurrencies.
- Enhanced market sentiment can result in higher trading volumes.
