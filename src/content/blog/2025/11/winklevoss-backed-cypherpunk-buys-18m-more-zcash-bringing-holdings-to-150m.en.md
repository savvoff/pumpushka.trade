## Overview

A digital-asset treasury firm, backed by the Winklevoss twins, has made a significant investment in Zcash, acquiring $18 million worth of the cryptocurrency. This purchase has increased its total holdings in Zcash to $150 million. 

## Investment Details

The firm’s recent acquisition of Zcash comes in the context of a notable rally in the cryptocurrency's price. As a result of this surge, the firm is currently reporting paper gains exceeding 100% on its investments in Zcash. 

## Importance of the Purchase

This investment highlights the growing interest in Zcash within the digital asset community, especially given the recent price movements. The firm’s substantial holdings indicate a strong belief in the future potential of Zcash as a valuable asset.
