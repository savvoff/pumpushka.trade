## What Happened

On Thursday, the S&P 500 faced a substantial wipeout, which has been attributed to several factors. Widening U.S. credit spreads have raised concerns among investors, signaling potential instability in the credit market. Additionally, overextended market gains have led to a reevaluation of asset values, prompting a sell-off. The overall macroeconomic landscape remains uncertain, further exacerbating investor anxiety.

- The sentiment in the crypto market has shifted to 'extreme fear.'
- Investor concerns are heightened due to the broader financial market instability.
- The decline in the S&P 500 may influence trading behaviors in the crypto space.
