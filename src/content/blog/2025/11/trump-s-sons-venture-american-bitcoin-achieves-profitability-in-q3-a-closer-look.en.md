## Overview

American Bitcoin (ABTC), co-founded by Eric Trump and Donald Trump Jr., has reported significant profitability for the third quarter (Q3) of the year. The company's impressive financial performance is attributed to improved margins and increased Bitcoin accumulation through mining and strategic purchases.

## Financial Highlights

In its latest financial report, American Bitcoin announced a revenue of $64.2 million for Q3, marking a notable increase compared to the previous quarter. The company achieved a net income of $3.5 million, slightly surpassing the $3.4 million earned during the same quarter the previous year.

Throughout the quarter, American Bitcoin acquired over 3,000 BTC through its mining operations and strategic purchases. As of September 30, the company held a total of 3,418 BTC in its reserves, positioning itself among leading public Bitcoin treasuries.

## Mining Capacity and Strategy

American Bitcoin significantly enhanced its Bitcoin mining capacity, increasing it by approximately 2.5 times quarter-over-quarter. The company added around 14.8 exahash per second (EH/s), bringing its total mining capacity to approximately 25.0 EH/s by the end of September. 

Eric Trump, in a post-earnings conference call, highlighted the company’s growth since its debut on Nasdaq and praised the efficiency of their scalable and asset-light mining operations, which allow them to generate Bitcoin below market rates.

CEO Mike Ho emphasized the cost efficiency of American Bitcoin's mining operations, noting that they mine at significantly lower costs compared to traditional methods of acquiring Bitcoin at spot prices. He also pointed out the company's accelerated growth in mining capacity, revenue, and gross margin improvements during the quarter.
