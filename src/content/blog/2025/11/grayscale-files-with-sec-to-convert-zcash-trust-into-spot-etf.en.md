## Overview

Grayscale has submitted a filing with the SEC to convert its Zcash Trust into a spot ETF. If approved, this would mark a significant milestone as the first exchange-traded fund associated with a privacy coin.

## What Happened

Grayscale's filing aims to transform its Zcash Trust into a spot ETF, which would allow investors to gain exposure to Zcash through a regulated investment vehicle. This move follows a series of ETF listings from Grayscale and other asset managers, showcasing the growing interest in cryptocurrency investment products.

The potential listing on NYSE Arca highlights the increasing acceptance and integration of cryptocurrencies into traditional financial markets. The Zcash Trust's conversion into a spot ETF could pave the way for more privacy-focused digital assets to be included in mainstream investment offerings.
