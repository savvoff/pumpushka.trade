## Overview

Self, a startup focused on zero-knowledge identity solutions, has successfully raised $9 million in funding. In conjunction with this funding, the company has launched a rewards program designed to encourage the adoption of on-chain verification.

## What Happened

Self has secured $9 million to further develop its zero-knowledge identity platform. This funding will support the company's efforts in enhancing its technology and expanding its reach within the identity verification sector. Alongside this financial boost, Self has introduced a new rewards program. This initiative is specifically designed to incentivize users to adopt on-chain verification methods, which are crucial for ensuring privacy and security in digital identity management.
