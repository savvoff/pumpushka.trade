## Overview

In the third quarter, venture capital activity in the crypto sector reached $4.6 billion, marking the second-best quarter since the FTX collapse. A significant portion of this capital came from a few major deals, highlighting the concentration of investment in the industry.

## Key Developments

During this quarter, half of the total capital raised was attributed to seven venture deals. Among these, Revolut emerged as a significant player, securing a $1 billion investment. This concentration of funding in just a few deals underscores the ongoing interest and confidence in the crypto market despite previous challenges.
