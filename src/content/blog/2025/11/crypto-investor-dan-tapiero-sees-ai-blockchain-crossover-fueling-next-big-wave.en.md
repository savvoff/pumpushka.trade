## Overview

Dan Tapiero, the founder of 10T, has expressed his belief in the potential crossover between artificial intelligence (AI) and blockchain technology. He is making significant investments in this area, specifically allocating a portion of his upcoming fund to startups that are yet to be established.

## What Happened

Tapiero, a notable figure in the investment community, has made a bold statement regarding the relationship between blockchain and AI. He referred to blockchain as "the money of AI," suggesting that he sees a strong connection and potential collaboration between these two technologies. This perspective has led him to take a proactive approach in his investment strategy.

In preparation for future developments in this sector, Tapiero is dedicating 20% of his next fund to startups that are not currently in existence. This indicates a forward-thinking approach and a belief in the transformative potential of combining AI with blockchain.

## Impact on the crypto market

- Increased interest in the intersection of AI and blockchain technologies.
- Potential for new startups and innovations in the crypto space.
- Shift in investment strategies among venture capitalists focusing on emerging technologies.
- Greater emphasis on foundational technologies that could support future advancements.
- Possible influence on market dynamics as new projects are introduced.
