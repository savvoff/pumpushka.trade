## Overview

Telegram's Pavel Durov has announced the launch of the Cocoon decentralized AI network. This platform is designed to prioritize user privacy and operates on The Open Network, allowing users to earn rewards for contributing computing power.

## What Happened

The Cocoon decentralized AI network has officially gone live, marking a significant development in the integration of artificial intelligence with blockchain technology. Built on The Open Network, this platform aims to enhance user privacy while providing an avenue for users to monetize their unused computing resources. By renting out their computing power, users can earn rewards in the form of TON.

## Impact on the crypto market

- The launch of Cocoon may increase interest in decentralized AI solutions within the blockchain community.
- Users can potentially benefit financially by participating in the network through computing power rental.
- The focus on privacy may attract users who prioritize data security and autonomy.
- The integration of AI with blockchain technology could lead to innovative applications and developments in the crypto space.
- The success of Cocoon may influence other projects to explore similar decentralized models.
