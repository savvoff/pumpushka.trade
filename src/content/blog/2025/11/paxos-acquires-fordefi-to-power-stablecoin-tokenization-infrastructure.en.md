## Overview

Paxos has acquired Fordefi, enhancing its capabilities in custody, tokenization, and institutional on-chain operations. This acquisition is significant as it integrates Fordefi's MPC wallet infrastructure into Paxos' offerings.

## Details of the Acquisition

The acquisition of Fordefi allows Paxos to expand its regulated capabilities. The integration of Fordefi’s MPC wallet infrastructure is particularly noteworthy. This addition strengthens Paxos' position in the crypto market by enabling more robust solutions for custody and tokenization.

## Importance of the Acquisition

The move to acquire Fordefi is important for several reasons. It indicates Paxos' commitment to enhancing its technological infrastructure, which is essential for providing secure and regulated services in the rapidly evolving crypto ecosystem. By broadening its capabilities, Paxos is positioned to better serve institutional clients and improve on-chain operations.
