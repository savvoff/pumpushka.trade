## Overview

KindlyMD, a company involved with Bitcoin treasury management, has seen its shares decline significantly following a missed earnings deadline for Q3. The company attributes this setback to complexities in merger accounting and has issued warnings about potential major losses in the near future.

## What Happened

KindlyMD's shares experienced a further drop of 10% after the company failed to meet its Q3 earnings deadline. The missed deadline has raised concerns among investors, particularly as the company has cited challenges related to merger accounting as a primary reason for the delay. In addition, KindlyMD has warned stakeholders about the possibility of significant losses ahead, which could further impact investor confidence and market perceptions of the company.
