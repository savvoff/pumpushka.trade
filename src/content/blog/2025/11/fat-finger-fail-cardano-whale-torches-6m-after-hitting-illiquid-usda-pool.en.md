## Overview

A significant error occurred when a Cardano whale mistakenly routed funds through an illiquid micro-cap stablecoin, resulting in a loss of $6 million. This incident highlights the risks associated with trading in less liquid markets.

## What Happened?

A Cardano whale, a large holder of the cryptocurrency, made a decision to route their assets through a specific stablecoin that is characterized by low liquidity. This choice has been labeled as potentially one of the most expensive mistakes of the year. The implications of this event serve as a reminder of the challenges and dangers that can arise in the cryptocurrency trading landscape, particularly when dealing with illiquid assets.

## Impact on the crypto market

- The incident underscores the risks associated with trading in illiquid markets.
- It may lead to increased caution among investors when considering micro-cap assets.
- The event could influence trading strategies and risk management practices within the crypto community.
- This loss may prompt discussions about the importance of liquidity in cryptocurrency transactions.
- It serves as a case study for potential pitfalls in the trading of stablecoins.
