## Overview

Crypto analyst Crypto Waterman has shared predictions regarding Bitcoin's price movements, drawing on insights from Chinese astrology. His analysis suggests a significant surge in Bitcoin's value, alongside altcoins, following a recent downturn.

## Bitcoin Price Predictions

In a recent post, Crypto Waterman indicated that Bitcoin is expected to experience a rebound starting December 5, following a potential bottom between November 28 and 29, coinciding with the end of Mercury retrograde. He noted that the market may see considerable fluctuations during the period from November 29 to December 5, likening the current market behavior to that observed in mid-July 2021.

Waterman anticipates a two-week price increase for Bitcoin from December 5 to December 18, projecting a rise to between $100,000 and $110,000. Following this surge, he predicts a three-week decline from December 18 to January 6, which could bring Bitcoin's price down to between $90,000 and $100,000. After this dip, he foresees a significant rally from December 6 to mid-February, potentially leading Bitcoin to achieve a new all-time high between $140,000 and $145,000.

Waterman also believes that altcoins will enjoy a final rally during this period, estimating that altseason may occur between January and February. He plans to exit most of his holdings by mid-February or early March as the market transitions into the next cycle.

## Contrasting Views from Other Analysts

In contrast, another analyst, Colin, expressed caution regarding the possibility of Bitcoin reaching a new all-time high. Following Bitcoin's recent recovery from lows near $81,000, Colin noted that while a rebound was expected due to oversold conditions, it may be premature to predict new highs. He emphasized the need for Bitcoin to reclaim key resistance levels before considering a new all-time high achievable, although he did suggest that the price could reach $100,000 during the rebound.
