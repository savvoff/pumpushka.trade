## Overview

Recent developments in the crypto market have highlighted several presale projects that are gaining traction as traders shift their focus back to risk assets. Bitcoin Hyper, SUBBD, and LiquidChain are at the forefront of this movement, each targeting different aspects of the evolving crypto landscape.

## Bitcoin Hyper: A Layer-2 Solution for Bitcoin

Bitcoin Hyper aims to integrate high-speed, low-fee smart contracts directly into the Bitcoin ecosystem through a modular design that combines Bitcoin settlement with Solana Virtual Machine (SVM) execution. This innovative approach allows for sub-second finality, ultra-low latency, and high-speed transactions for wrapped Bitcoin. The presale has raised significant funds, indicating strong investor confidence in Bitcoin Layer 2 solutions.

### Key Features of Bitcoin Hyper:
- **Modular Design**: Utilizes Bitcoin for settlement while employing SVM for execution.
- **Diverse Applications**: Supports full DeFi functionalities including swaps, lending, staking, NFT marketplaces, and gaming dApps.
- **Strong Investor Interest**: The presale has attracted considerable attention, with notable whale activity signaling growing conviction in the project.

## SUBBD: Merging AI and Web3 for Creators

SUBBD targets the $85 billion creator economy by combining AI tools with crypto payments and token-gated content. This platform empowers creators by enabling them to launch AI Personal Assistants and monetize their work without relying on traditional intermediaries. The presale has shown early signs of investor interest, underscoring the potential for AI and Web3 convergence.

### Key Features of SUBBD:
- **AI-Driven Platform**: Allows creators to generate AI voice clones and deploy fully AI-generated influencers.
- **Direct Revenue Streams**: Facilitates token-gated content and subscription layers, ensuring creators retain more revenue.
- **Incentives for Participation**: Offers a 20% first-year staking yield to encourage long-term engagement within the ecosystem.

## LiquidChain: A Cross-Chain DeFi Infrastructure

LiquidChain is designed as a Layer-3 blockchain that aims to unify liquidity across Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana. By eliminating the need for wrapped assets, it facilitates seamless capital deployment and cross-chain interactions. The presale is still in its early stages, but initial participation has already seen substantial interest.

### Key Features of LiquidChain:
- **Unified Liquidity Pools**: Simplifies interactions between BTC, ETH, and SOL without the usual friction.
- **High-Performance Virtual Machine**: Enables real-time cross-chain execution and trust-minimized proof systems.
- **Long-Term Vision**: The project is positioned as a multichain infrastructure bet with a planned mainnet launch in the future.
