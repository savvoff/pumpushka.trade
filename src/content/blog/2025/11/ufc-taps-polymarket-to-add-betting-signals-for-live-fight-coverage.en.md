## Overview

The UFC has partnered with Polymarket to enhance its live fight coverage by integrating onchain prediction data and fan-sentiment feeds. This collaboration aims to create a more interactive experience for viewers during UFC events.

## What Happened

Polymarket's technology will be utilized to provide valuable insights into fan sentiments and predictions related to UFC fights. This integration is set to bring a new dimension to how fans engage with the events, allowing them to access real-time data and insights as the fights unfold. By incorporating these features, the UFC is looking to boost viewer engagement and interaction during its events.

- Increased visibility for Polymarket could lead to higher interest in onchain prediction markets.
- Enhanced fan engagement may drive more users to explore blockchain-based solutions in the sports sector.
- The collaboration represents a shift towards integrating real-time data in traditional sports broadcasting.
