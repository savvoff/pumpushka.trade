## Overview

The UFC is collaborating with Polymarket to enhance its live fight coverage by incorporating onchain prediction data and fan-sentiment feeds. This integration aims to create a more engaging experience for viewers during UFC events.

## Integration of Polymarket's Data

Polymarket's technology will provide valuable insights through its prediction data and fan-sentiment analysis. By weaving these elements into UFC broadcasts, the organization seeks to offer a unique interactive layer that can enrich the viewing experience. This move highlights the growing intersection of sports and blockchain technology.
