## Overview

A recent hack of Upbit has been linked to North Korea's Lazarus group, leading to a formal investigation by authorities in Seoul. This incident has raised concerns about the security of cryptocurrency exchanges and the methods employed by state-sponsored hacking groups.

## Details of the Hack

The breach at Upbit has resulted in a significant review of the exchange's systems. As part of this process, regulators are focusing on the theft associated with Solana hot wallets. The involvement of Lazarus, a group known for its cybercriminal activities, underscores the ongoing threat posed by state-sponsored hacking.
