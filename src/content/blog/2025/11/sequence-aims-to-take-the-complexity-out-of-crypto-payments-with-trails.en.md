## Overview

Infrastructure firm Sequence is addressing the challenges of fragmentation in the cryptocurrency space. Their new platform, Trails, aims to streamline payments across multiple blockchain networks.

## What Happened

Sequence has introduced Trails, a platform designed to simplify the process of making payments in the cryptocurrency ecosystem. This initiative targets the existing complexity that users face when navigating different chains. By focusing on reducing fragmentation, Sequence hopes to enhance the overall user experience in crypto transactions.

## Why It Matters

The launch of Trails is significant as it tackles a key issue within the cryptocurrency market. Fragmentation can lead to inefficiencies and confusion for users trying to make transactions across various blockchain networks. By providing a solution that simplifies these processes, Sequence aims to foster greater adoption and usability of crypto payments.

- The introduction of Trails may lead to increased transaction efficiency for users.
