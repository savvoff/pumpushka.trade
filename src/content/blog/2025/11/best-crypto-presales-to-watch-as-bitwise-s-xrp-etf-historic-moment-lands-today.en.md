## Overview

Bitwise has launched its XRP ETF on NYSE Arca, marking a significant development in the cryptocurrency landscape. The ETF's introduction positions XRP as an asset ready for institutional investment, while also generating notable market activity around it. This event is closely tied to several presales that align with emerging trends in the market.

## Bitwise's XRP ETF Launch

Bitwise's XRP ETF has officially launched on NYSE Arca. The ETF features a management fee of 0.34%, which is waived for the first month on the first $500 million of assets. This strategic move aims to attract early institutional interest in XRP. Following the launch, XRP experienced a drop exceeding 9% before rebounding, indicating a reaction typical of market participants who often sell on news. The trading volumes and futures open interest have increased, suggesting renewed speculative and hedging activities.

The significance of this launch extends beyond XRP. Historically, spot ETFs have normalized exposure to leading cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. With XRP now receiving similar treatment, the market anticipates a shift in capital from established blue-chip tokens to altcoins and other higher-risk assets.

## Notable Crypto Presales

Three presales have emerged as noteworthy opportunities in the post-ETF landscape:

### 1. Best Wallet Token

Best Wallet Token is designed as a super-app catering to self-custody for new crypto users. It serves as a non-custodial, multi-chain wallet that incorporates fiat on/off-ramps, portfolio tracking, and cross-chain swaps. With a growing user base and plans to introduce additional features like a debit card and staking aggregator, the token aims to facilitate a seamless user experience. The presale has garnered significant interest, raising over $17.23 million.

### 2. Bitcoin Hyper

Bitcoin Hyper seeks to enhance Bitcoin's functionality by offering a Layer-2 solution that addresses the limitations of Bitcoin's base layer. It utilizes the Solana Virtual Machine to enable faster transactions and lower fees while maintaining Bitcoin's core security. The presale has raised around $28 million, attracting significant investments from large buyers.

### 3. Ionix Chain

Ionix Chain positions itself as an AI-native Layer-1 blockchain, utilizing Quantum AI Consensus to achieve high transaction speeds and low fees. It is designed to support AI-heavy workloads and aims to integrate seamlessly with existing ecosystems. The presale has raised more than $1.57 million, with incentives for stakers and plans for revenue sharing.
