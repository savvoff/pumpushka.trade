## Overview

Disgraced lobbyist Jack Abramoff has received probation following his involvement in a fraudulent cryptocurrency scheme known as AML Bitcoin. In addition to probation, Abramoff is required to repay $2.2 million to the victims affected by his actions.

## What Happened

Jack Abramoff, known for his controversial lobbying career, faced legal consequences for his role in the AML Bitcoin fraud scheme. Instead of serving prison time, he has been sentenced to probation. This decision comes alongside a financial obligation, as he must repay a significant amount of money to those who were defrauded. The scheme involved misleading activities related to cryptocurrency, ultimately resulting in financial losses for numerous individuals.
