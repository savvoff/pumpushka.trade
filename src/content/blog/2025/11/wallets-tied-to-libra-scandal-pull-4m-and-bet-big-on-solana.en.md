## Overview

Wallets associated with the Libra memecoin scandal have recently made significant moves in the crypto market. Despite facing asset freezes and fraud investigations, these wallets have acquired a substantial amount of Solana.

## Recent Developments

The wallets linked to the controversial Libra memecoin scandal have managed to pull in a total of $4 million and have invested approximately $61.5 million in Solana. This activity occurs against the backdrop of ongoing asset freezes and active fraud probes related to the scandal. The decision to invest heavily in Solana suggests a strategic move to redirect funds into a different asset, despite the legal challenges these wallets are currently facing.
