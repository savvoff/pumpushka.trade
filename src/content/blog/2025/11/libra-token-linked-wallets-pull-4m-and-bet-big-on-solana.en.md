## Overview

Wallets associated with the Libra token have been actively drawing liquidity, amassing significant investments in Solana. Despite facing asset freezes and ongoing fraud investigations, these wallets have made substantial purchases.

## What Happened

Wallets linked to the Libra token have successfully pulled in $4 million in liquidity. In addition to this, they have invested a notable sum of $61.5 million into Solana. This activity occurs amid challenges such as asset freezes and inquiries into potential fraudulent activities.
