## What Happened

The project, which was positioned as a promising contender in the stablecoin market, has faced several challenges. These elements have fostered uncertainty about whether the recent sell-off has reached its conclusion.

- Supply pressure indicates potential issues with liquidity and market stability.
- Sparse communication from the project team can exacerbate uncertainty, affecting investor sentiment.
