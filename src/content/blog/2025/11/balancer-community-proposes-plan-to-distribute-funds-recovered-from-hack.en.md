## Overview

The Balancer community has put forth a proposal regarding the distribution of funds that were recovered from a significant hack. This recovery involves a total of $8 million, which is part of a larger $116 million hack that occurred in November.

## Proposal Details

The proposal outlines a plan for how the recovered funds will be allocated to the victims affected by the hack. The focus is on ensuring that those who suffered losses during the incident receive compensation from the recovered amount.

## Importance of the Proposal

This initiative is crucial for the Balancer community as it addresses the financial impact suffered by victims of the hack. By proposing a structured distribution of the recovered funds, the community aims to restore trust and provide support to those affected.

## Impact on the crypto market

- The proposal highlights the importance of community-driven governance in responding to security incidents.
- Successful distribution of recovered funds could enhance the reputation of the Balancer platform.
- It may set a precedent for how similar situations are handled in the future within the crypto space.
- The initiative reflects a commitment to accountability and transparency in the aftermath of hacks.
