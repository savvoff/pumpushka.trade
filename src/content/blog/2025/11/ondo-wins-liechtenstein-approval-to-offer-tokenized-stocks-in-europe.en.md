## Overview

Ondo, a US-based tokenization platform, has achieved a significant milestone by receiving approval from Liechtenstein to offer tokenized stocks and ETFs across 30 European countries. This development marks a notable expansion for the platform in the European market.

## What Happened

Ondo has secured regulatory approval in Liechtenstein, which allows it to engage in the tokenization of stocks and exchange-traded funds (ETFs). This approval is crucial as it enables Ondo to broaden its services beyond the US and enter the European financial landscape. The company is now positioned to provide innovative financial products that leverage blockchain technology, catering to a diverse range of investors in multiple European nations.

## Why It Matters

The approval from Liechtenstein is significant as it highlights the growing acceptance of digital assets and tokenization within established financial frameworks. By offering tokenized stocks and ETFs, Ondo is not only enhancing accessibility for investors but also contributing to the evolution of financial markets in Europe. This move may set a precedent for other jurisdictions in the region to follow suit, potentially leading to increased competition and innovation in the financial sector.
