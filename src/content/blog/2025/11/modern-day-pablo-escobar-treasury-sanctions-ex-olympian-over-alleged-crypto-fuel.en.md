## Overview

A former Olympic snowboarder has been labeled a "drug kingpin" and is now subject to U.S. sanctions. The FBI is actively pursuing him in Mexico due to his alleged involvement in a crime empire that utilizes cryptocurrency.

## What Happened

The U.S. Treasury has sanctioned a former Olympic snowboarder who has transitioned into a criminal figure. This individual is accused of leading a drug trafficking operation that reportedly leverages cryptocurrency for its activities. The FBI is currently engaged in efforts to locate and apprehend him in Mexico.
