## Overview

Record Financial is making strides in the music industry by promoting on-chain royalties, which are increasingly appealing to labels and artists. This initiative aims to provide quicker and more transparent payment solutions.

## What Happened

Record Financial has been actively advocating for the implementation of real-time royalties on the Avalanche blockchain. This movement is resonating with music labels and artists who are looking for improved payment methods. The shift towards on-chain royalties represents a significant change in how music payments are processed, emphasizing efficiency and clarity in financial transactions within the industry.

## Why It Matters

The push for on-chain royalties is important as it addresses common issues in the traditional music payment system, such as delays and lack of transparency. By leveraging blockchain technology, Record Financial aims to modernize the way artists and labels receive their earnings, potentially transforming the landscape of music payments.
