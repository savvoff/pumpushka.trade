## What Happened

The DOGE office, which was tasked with improving governmental operations, has gone dormant months ahead of its scheduled closure. This development indicates a shift in strategy from the administration, as it evaluates simpler approaches to disband federal entities. The early winding down of the DOGE office reflects a broader trend within the government to reassess and optimize its functions.
