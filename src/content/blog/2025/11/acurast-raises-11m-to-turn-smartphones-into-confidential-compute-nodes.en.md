## Overview

Acurast has successfully raised $11 million to develop a smartphone-based confidential compute network. This innovative project aims to enable tamper-resistant execution on consumer phones.

## What Happened

Acurast's funding round will support the creation of a network that transforms smartphones into confidential compute nodes. The technology focuses on enhancing security and privacy by ensuring that computations performed on these devices are resistant to tampering.

## Importance of the Development

The launch of this smartphone-based network is significant because it leverages existing consumer technology to provide advanced security features. By enabling confidential computing on widely used devices, Acurast aims to broaden the accessibility of secure computing solutions.
