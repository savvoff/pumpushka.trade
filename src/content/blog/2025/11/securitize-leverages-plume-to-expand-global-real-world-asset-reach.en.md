## Overview

Securitize has formed a partnership with Plume to introduce institutional-grade assets on Plume's Nest staking protocol. This collaboration aims to enhance Securitize's presence in the decentralized finance (DeFi) space.

## What Happened

Securitize, a company specializing in digital asset management, has joined forces with Plume to leverage its Nest staking protocol. This partnership is significant as it allows Securitize to offer institutional-grade assets, marking a notable expansion of its operations in the DeFi sector. By utilizing Plume's technology, Securitize is positioning itself to tap into a broader market for real-world assets, which can attract more institutional investors looking for secure and compliant digital asset options.

## Impact on the crypto market

- The partnership signifies a growing trend of collaboration between traditional finance and DeFi platforms.
- It highlights the increasing interest in institutional-grade assets within the DeFi ecosystem.
- The use of staking protocols could enhance liquidity and yield opportunities for investors.
- Securitize's expansion into DeFi may encourage other firms to explore similar partnerships, driving innovation.
- This move could potentially increase the legitimacy and acceptance of DeFi solutions among institutional investors.
