## Overview

An advocacy group focused on the cryptocurrency sector has announced its intention to vet candidates for the 2026 midterm elections based on their positions regarding digital assets. This follows the crypto industry's notable influence on the 2024 US elections.

## What Happened

The advocacy group, which has not been named, aims to assess and support candidates who align with the interests of the cryptocurrency community. The decision to focus on the upcoming midterms comes as a strategic continuation of efforts that proved effective in the previous electoral cycle. By vetting candidates, the group seeks to ensure that the voices of those in the crypto industry are represented in political discussions and decision-making processes.

## Why It Matters

The initiative highlights the growing importance of cryptocurrency in the political landscape. As the industry has demonstrated its capacity to impact elections, the advocacy group's efforts could shape the future regulatory environment for digital assets. By actively engaging with candidates, the group aims to foster a political climate that is more favorable to cryptocurrency innovation and adoption.
