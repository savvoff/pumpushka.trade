## Current Situation

A crypto analyst has observed that Dogecoin is currently positioned at a critical support level, which previously marked the beginning of its last sustained rally. The latest technical analysis indicates that this setup closely resembles one that triggered a major breakout during the last cycle from 2023 to 2024. This historical context is essential for understanding the potential for a price increase.

### Historical Context

The analyst has pointed out that Dogecoin's weekly chart has settled on its support trendline for the third time within the current cycle spanning from 2021 to 2026. The chart shows that Dogecoin's price reached this key level after a prolonged pullback, forming a structure similar to one seen in late 2023. In that previous cycle, the same pattern marked the beginning of a gradual upward trend that persisted throughout 2024, ultimately leading to a peak in the mid-cycle range.

The historical comparison reveals that during the earlier cycle, Dogecoin completed three closes at the support zone before experiencing a sharp upward reversal. The current weekly pattern mirrors this setup, as the price continues to tighten around a rising trendline and form higher lows.

### Future Projections

The analyst has also indicated that there is a large boxed region on the chart that represents a projected phase from 2024 to 2025, suggesting that Dogecoin might still have room for upward movement. If historical patterns are to be believed, this could lead to another significant price increase above $1 by 2026.

This breakout is marked by the price line moving above the upper boundary of the channel, indicating a possible shift in short-term momentum.

Technical indicators, such as the Relative Strength Index (RSI) and the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), support this shift.
