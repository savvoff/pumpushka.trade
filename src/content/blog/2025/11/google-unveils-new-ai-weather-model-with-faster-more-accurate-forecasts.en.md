## Overview

Google has introduced its new AI-powered weather model, named WeatherNext 2, which enhances forecasting capabilities across several platforms. This model aims to provide faster and more accurate weather predictions, integrating seamlessly with various Google services.

## What Happened

The launch of WeatherNext 2 marks a significant advancement in Google's weather forecasting technology. This AI-driven model is designed to improve the quality of weather forecasts, which is crucial for users relying on accurate weather information. The new forecasts are now available across multiple Google services, including Search, Gemini, Pixel Weather, and Maps weather tools. 

The integration of this advanced forecasting system into widely used platforms signifies Google's commitment to leveraging artificial intelligence to enhance user experience and provide timely weather updates.
