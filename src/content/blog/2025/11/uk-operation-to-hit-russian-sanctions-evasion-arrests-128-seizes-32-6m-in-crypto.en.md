## Overview

A significant law enforcement operation in the UK has resulted in the arrest of 128 individuals connected to the evasion of Russian sanctions. This operation, known as Operation Destabilise, has also led to the seizure of $32.6 million in cryptocurrency and cash.

## Details of Operation Destabilise

Operation Destabilise has uncovered a money laundering network that was operating within the UK. This network was involved in illicit activities, including the purchase of a bank located in Kyrgyzstan. The operation highlights the ongoing efforts to combat financial crimes associated with sanctions evasion.

### Importance of the Operation

The arrests and seizures made during Operation Destabilise underscore the UK's commitment to enforcing sanctions and disrupting financial networks that facilitate illegal activities. By targeting such networks, authorities aim to uphold the integrity of financial systems and deter future violations.
