## Overview

Bitcoin has shown signs of recovery after experiencing four consecutive weeks of declines. The cryptocurrency has surpassed a significant threshold, which is crucial for a specific group of investors.

## Recent Developments

After four weeks of downturn, Bitcoin has managed to recover and move above a critical flow-weighted cost basis. This particular threshold is set at $89,600 and is especially important for Bitcoin ETF holders. This group represents a significant portion of the inflows into Bitcoin, indicating their influence on the market.

The recovery is noteworthy as it highlights a potential shift in market sentiment. The surpassing of this key level suggests renewed interest and confidence among investors, particularly those involved with Bitcoin ETFs.
