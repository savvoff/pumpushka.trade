## Current Price Movement

XRP price began its latest decline from a level of $2.350, moving below the $2.320 zone. Currently, the price is trading below $2.30 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a bearish trend. A short-term bearish trend line has formed, with resistance identified at $2.2550 on the hourly chart of the XRP/USD pair.

Despite an attempt to recover above $2.320, XRP failed to sustain upward momentum, similar to trends observed in other cryptocurrencies. The price has dropped below key levels including $2.30 and $2.250, and a low was recorded at $2.155. Currently, XRP is consolidating losses while exhibiting a bearish angle.

There was a minor move above the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level from the recent decline, but the price remains below $2.30 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. If an upward move occurs, resistance is anticipated near the $2.2550 level. The first significant resistance is near the $2.30 level, and if surpassed, it could lead to a test of $2.350.

## Potential for Further Decline

A close below this level could trigger further declines towards $2.050, with additional support at the $2.020 zone and potentially lower towards $1.880.
