## Overview

Visa has announced a partnership with crypto infrastructure company Aquanow to enhance stablecoin settlement in the CEMEA region. This collaboration aims to reduce cross-border costs and minimize friction in transactions.

## Partnership Details

Visa's new partnership with Aquanow focuses on expanding the use of stablecoins for settlement purposes. The initiative targets the CEMEA region, which encompasses Europe, the Middle East, and Africa. By leveraging Aquanow's infrastructure, Visa intends to streamline the process of cross-border transactions, ultimately making them more efficient and cost-effective.

This move reflects Visa's ongoing commitment to integrating cryptocurrency solutions into its payment network. The partnership is expected to facilitate smoother transactions, benefiting businesses and consumers who rely on stablecoin settlements.
