## Overview

Solana (SOL) has experienced a notable decline, dropping below the $145 threshold and currently trading under $140. The price has shown weakness, and further declines could take it below $130. This trend follows a broader market movement affecting major cryptocurrencies.

## Recent Price Movements

Solana's price recently fell below both the $145 and $140 support levels, indicating a loss of stability in the market. After failing to maintain its position above $155, SOL has been trading below the 100-hourly simple moving average, reflecting bearish momentum. A low was recorded at $128, prompting a consolidation phase for the cryptocurrency.

As Solana's price continues to linger below $140, it faces immediate resistance around the $136 mark. A key bearish trend line is forming, indicating potential challenges for any upward movements. The price did experience a minor recovery above the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level from the downward movement between the $143 swing high and the $128 low, but it remains under pressure.

Should the bulls manage to defend the $130 or $128 levels, there may be a possibility of a recovery wave. However, if the price fails to rise above the $140 resistance, further declines are likely, with initial support near $130 and significant support at $128. A breakdown below $128 could lead to a more substantial decline toward the $120 support zone.
