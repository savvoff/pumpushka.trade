## Overview

XRP has recently experienced a significant price increase, surpassing the $2.20 mark and showing signs of consolidation. The upward momentum has been driven by a break above key resistance levels, indicating potential for further gains.

## Price Movement Analysis

XRP price began a notable upward trend, moving above the $2.120 zone and continuing to rise past the $2.20 threshold. This movement is characterized by a break above a bearish trend line that had resistance at $2.00, as shown on the hourly chart of the XRP/USD pair. Following this, XRP formed a high at $2.286 and has since entered a consolidation phase above the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the upward move from a previous swing low.

Currently, XRP is trading above both the $2.20 level and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. If the price continues to rise, it may encounter resistance near the $2.280 level, with further resistance identified at $2.320. A successful move above $2.350 could lead to additional upward momentum, potentially targeting higher resistance levels.

Conversely, if XRP fails to breach the $2.280 resistance, there is a risk of a downward correction. Initial support is found near the $2.180 level, with further support at $2.080. A decline below the $2.080 mark could signal a continued downward trend, with subsequent support levels at $2.050 and $2.020.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD:** The MACD for XRP/USD is showing bullish momentum.
- **Hourly RSI:** The RSI for XRP/USD is currently above the 50 level, indicating positive market sentiment.
