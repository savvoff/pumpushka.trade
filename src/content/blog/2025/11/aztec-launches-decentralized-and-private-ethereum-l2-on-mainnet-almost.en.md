## Overview

Aztec has officially launched its Ignition mainnet, marking a significant development in the Ethereum Layer 2 ecosystem. The platform operates without centralized operators and has introduced community staking alongside a major token sale.

## What Happened

Aztec's Ignition mainnet launch signifies its commitment to decentralization within the Ethereum network. By eliminating centralized operators, Aztec aims to enhance the security and reliability of its Layer 2 solutions. The introduction of community staking allows users to participate actively in the network, potentially increasing engagement and investment in the platform. This launch coincides with a major token sale, indicating Aztec's strategy to bolster its financial resources while promoting decentralization.

## Impact on the crypto market

- The launch of Aztec's Ignition mainnet could encourage other projects to adopt decentralized models.
- Community staking may increase user involvement and investment in decentralized finance.
- The absence of centralized operators enhances trust and security in the Ethereum ecosystem.
- The major token sale could attract new investors and capital into the market.
- Aztec's positioning as a fully decentralized Layer 2 solution may influence the competitive landscape among Ethereum scaling solutions.
