## Overview

Bitcoin miners are experiencing a surge in pre-market trading, driven by strong guidance from NVIDIA. This positive sentiment contrasts with NAKA, which has reported delayed losses for the third quarter.

## NVIDIA's Impact on Bitcoin Miners

NVIDIA, a key player in the semiconductor industry, provided strong guidance that has positively influenced the pre-market sentiment among bitcoin miners. The company's optimistic outlook is creating a ripple effect, leading to increased interest and activity in the cryptocurrency mining sector.

## NAKA's Q3 Losses

In contrast to the positive news from NVIDIA, NAKA has reported delayed losses for the third quarter. This development highlights the challenges faced by some companies in the crypto space, particularly as they navigate the complex market landscape.

## Impact on the crypto market

- Increased pre-market activity among bitcoin miners due to positive sentiment from NVIDIA.
- Diverging fortunes between NVIDIA and NAKA, showcasing the variability in the crypto market.
- Potential for heightened investor interest in bitcoin mining operations following NVIDIA's guidance.
- Ongoing challenges for companies like NAKA, which may affect their market position and investor confidence.
- Overall market dynamics may shift as miners react to industry developments and earnings reports.
