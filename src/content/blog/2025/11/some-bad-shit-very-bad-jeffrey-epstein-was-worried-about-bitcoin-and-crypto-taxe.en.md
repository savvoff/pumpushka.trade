## Epstein's Concerns About Crypto Taxes

In the year leading up to his death, Epstein showed a keen interest in the regulatory landscape surrounding cryptocurrencies. His apprehension centered on the implications of cryptocurrency taxation, which he deemed significant enough to warrant attention and action.
