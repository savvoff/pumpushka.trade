Rumble's stock price rose significantly after Tether increased its investment by acquiring an additional 1 million shares. This action by Tether is noteworthy as it reflects confidence in Rumble's potential and aligns with a general upswing in related industries, specifically data centers and high-performance computing.

The increase in Rumble's stock is part of a larger trend where stocks in the data center and high-performance computing sectors have also been experiencing gains. This correlation suggests that investor sentiment may be shifting positively towards companies involved in data-centric technologies.
