## Overview

Ethereum is nearing a pivotal moment in its market trajectory, with several bullish indicators aligning. The emergence of an Inverse Head & Shoulders formation, alongside increasing accumulation and a waning trend rejection, suggests that a significant upward movement could be on the horizon.

## Inverse Head and Shoulders Signals Brewing Momentum

A recent update from crypto analyst Donald Dean highlights the potential for Ethereum to experience a notable price movement. He points out the formation of an Inverse Head and Shoulders pattern, which is recognized as a bullish reversal formation that often signals strong upward momentum. This developing structure indicates that ETH may be poised to enter a more aggressive bullish phase if confirmed.

Dean also notes that the weekly chart shows solid support near a key Fibonacci retracement level. Additionally, a hammer candle has appeared on the weekly timeframe, suggesting that buying pressure is returning after a period of downside movement. If the pattern is validated, Dean mentions that Ethereum's initial major target could lie at a significant price level that would enable ETH to challenge previous resistance and support zones. Reclaiming this area would represent a meaningful shift in momentum and enhance the bullish outlook for Ethereum.

## Growing Accumulation Suggests Bulls Are Preparing for Action

In a separate analysis, LSTRADER draws attention to a substantial price movement that occurred previously, indicating a surge from one price level to another. This analysis highlighted the importance of observing key structural signals that can forecast momentum shifts.

LSTRADER further explains that the current market structure shows multiple instances of trend rejection. Despite these rejections, the trend is gradually losing strength while accumulation continues to rise. This combination typically reflects increasing bullish interest and the potential for an upward breakout. However, LSTRADER emphasizes that a major price movement should not be assumed until the trendline itself is breached, indicating that confirmation is necessary. For the time being, traders are advised to exercise patience as they monitor the evolving structure and await a decisive change in trend direction.
