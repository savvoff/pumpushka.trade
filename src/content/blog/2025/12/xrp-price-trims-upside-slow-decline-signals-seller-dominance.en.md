Should the price break below the $1.8420 level, it could continue to decline, with further support levels at $1.8150 and $1.770.

Technical indicators reveal that the hourly MACD for XRP/USD is gaining pace within the bearish zone, while the hourly RSI is positioned below the 50 level, indicating a bearish sentiment in the market.
