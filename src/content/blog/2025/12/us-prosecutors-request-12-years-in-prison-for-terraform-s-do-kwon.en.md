## Overview

US prosecutors have made a significant sentencing recommendation for Do Kwon, the founder of Terraform. They have requested a prison term of 12 years, stating that Kwon's actions resulted in substantial financial losses.

## Details of the Case

The sentencing recommendation highlighted that Do Kwon's actions led to greater financial losses than those caused by other notable figures in the crypto industry, specifically mentioning Sam Bankman-Fried, Alex Mashinsky, and Karl Sebastian Greenwood. This assertion underscores the severity of Kwon's alleged misconduct and its impact on investors and the broader market.
