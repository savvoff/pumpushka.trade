## Overview

The Blockchain Game Alliance (BGA) has reported a significant increase in industry confidence, which has risen to 66%. This shift comes as developers in the blockchain gaming sector move away from speculative practices and embrace more sustainable business models. This transition follows a funding collapse that the industry experienced since 2021.

## Industry Confidence on the Rise

The BGA's findings indicate a notable change in sentiment within the blockchain gaming community. The reported rise to 66% in confidence suggests that developers are increasingly optimistic about the future of the industry. This optimism is pivotal as it signifies a collective move towards stability and sustainability, which has been lacking in the sector since the downturn in funding that began in 2021.

The previous years saw a surge in speculative investments, which led to unsustainable business practices among many developers. However, with the recent collapse in funding, there has been a critical reevaluation of strategies within the industry. Developers are now prioritizing sustainable models that not only ensure longevity but also foster a healthier ecosystem for both creators and players.

This pivot towards sustainability is essential for the long-term growth of blockchain gaming. By focusing on sustainable practices, developers can create more reliable revenue streams, which is crucial in an industry that has experienced volatility. Moreover, this approach may also enhance player trust and engagement, as consumers increasingly favor projects that demonstrate a commitment to ethical practices and long-term viability.
