## Overview

An IT worker utilized artificial intelligence to create a phishing page that ultimately revealed the identity of a scammer in Delhi. This innovative approach led to the scammer's desperate attempts to escape the situation.

## The Incident

An IT professional, leveraging AI technology, crafted a phishing page designed to lure in a scammer. This tactic was described as "vibe coding," a method that allowed the individual to manipulate the situation and extract information from the scammer. The result was a significant shift in the power dynamic, as the scammer, faced with exposure, resorted to pleading for mercy.
