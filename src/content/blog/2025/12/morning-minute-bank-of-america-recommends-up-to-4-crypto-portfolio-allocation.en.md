## Overview

The crypto market experienced a notable uptick following new guidance from Bank of America, which recommended a portfolio allocation of up to 4% in cryptocurrencies. This positive sentiment was further bolstered by Vanguard's launch of a crypto exchange-traded fund (ETF).

## What Happened

Bank of America released guidance suggesting that investors consider allocating a portion of their portfolios—specifically up to 4%—to cryptocurrencies. This recommendation was well-received by the market, leading to significant price increases among major cryptocurrencies, which surged by 7-10%. The positive market reaction coincided with the debut of Vanguard's crypto ETF, adding momentum to the overall bullish sentiment in the crypto space.

## Impact on the crypto market

- Major cryptocurrencies saw price increases of 7-10%.
- Bank of America's guidance may influence investor behavior towards crypto allocations.
- Vanguard's crypto ETF launch contributes to the growing acceptance of cryptocurrencies in traditional finance.
- The combination of these events indicates a shift in institutional perspectives on crypto investments.
- Increased interest from financial institutions could lead to broader adoption of cryptocurrencies.
