## Overview

Marketnode and Lion Global have launched a new investment fund that provides exposure to physical gold bars securely stored and insured in Singapore. This fund, which operates on the Solana blockchain, combines traditional custody methods with the innovative features of on-chain technology, including an option for in-kind redemption.

## Details of the Fund

The newly established fund is designed to give investors direct access to physical gold, a traditional asset known for its stability and value retention. By using physical gold bars that are vaulted and insured in Singapore, the fund ensures that investors' assets are safeguarded in a reputable location known for its stringent regulations and security measures.

One of the significant features of this fund is its integration with blockchain technology, specifically the Solana blockchain. This integration allows for enhanced transparency and efficiency in transactions, making it easier for investors to manage their holdings. The on-chain aspect of the fund provides a modern twist to traditional gold investment, potentially attracting a new demographic of investors who are more familiar with digital assets.

Additionally, the fund offers an option for in-kind redemption, which means that investors can redeem their shares in the fund for physical gold. This feature is particularly appealing to those who wish to have actual possession of their investment rather than merely holding a paper claim to it. This option aligns with the growing trend among investors who prefer tangible assets, especially in times of economic uncertainty.

## From author

The initiative by Marketnode and Lion Global reflects a significant trend in the investment landscape, where traditional assets are increasingly being integrated with blockchain technology. This fusion not only modernizes the way investors can access and manage their assets but also enhances the overall security and transparency of transactions. By vaulting gold in a secure location and offering on-chain management, the fund addresses some of the common concerns associated with investing in physical commodities, such as storage and security risks.

Moreover, the option for in-kind redemption provides an added layer of appeal, allowing investors to convert their holdings into physical gold easily. This flexibility can be particularly attractive in a market where asset liquidity and access are paramount.

The collaboration between a traditional financial entity and a blockchain-based platform signifies a shift towards more innovative financial products. It exemplifies how the financial services industry is evolving to meet the needs and preferences of modern investors, who are increasingly looking for secure and efficient ways to diversify their portfolios.
