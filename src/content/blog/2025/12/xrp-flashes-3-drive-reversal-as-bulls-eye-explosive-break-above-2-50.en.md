## Overview

XRP is currently navigating a critical price zone above $2.00, with analysts identifying a potential reversal pattern. Key price levels at $2.00, $2.22, and $2.50 are being closely monitored as indicators of future price movements.

## Analysis of XRP's Price Action

### Key Price Levels

Analysts Dom and Osemka have outlined a framework for XRP's price action, suggesting that the cryptocurrency is in a phase of consolidation. Osemka describes the structure as a flat correction based on the 2021 high, indicating that the current price movements are part of an accumulation phase. He notes that XRP has repeatedly tested support at the 2021 high, which he refers to as a “Reaccumulation” area.

### Market Structure

Osemka's analysis includes a detailed view of an A–B–C corrective sequence. The B leg of this sequence forms a dotted ascending channel, which he describes as a 3-legged “abc” wave. He emphasizes that the current C leg is contained within a downward “Corrective channel,” suggesting that even a deeper test of support could present a buying opportunity rather than a bearish signal.

### Recent Trends

Dom has focused on the last six weeks of price action, identifying a 3-drive pattern that could indicate a reversal. He points out that this pattern includes three downside pushes that fail to extend lower, followed by the formation of a higher low. This pattern could signal a potential trend change. 

### Key Indicators

Dom also highlights the importance of the monthly rolling volume-weighted average price (VWAP) at approximately $2.22 as a crucial pivot point. He suggests that regaining this level could lead to a rally towards the $2.50 area, which aligns with other significant VWAP clusters.
