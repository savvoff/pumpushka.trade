## Overview

Dogecoin has experienced a significant decline, trading below the $0.1250 mark against the US Dollar. The cryptocurrency is currently consolidating its losses and facing potential hurdles that could impede any upward movement.

## Recent Price Movements

Dogecoin's price began a fresh decline after closing below the $0.1300 level. This downward trend mirrors movements seen in other major cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. Following this trend, DOGE dropped below critical support levels, including $0.1280 and $0.1250, and has even traded under $0.1220. A notable low was recorded at approximately $0.1198, reinforcing the bearish sentiment surrounding the asset.

At present, Dogecoin is consolidating its losses below the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward move from the swing high of $0.1305 to the low of $0.1198. The price remains under the $0.1280 mark and the 100-hourly simple moving average, indicating a lack of upward momentum.

As DOGE attempts to recover, immediate resistance is observed near the $0.1235 level. If the price can manage to break through this barrier, the next significant resistance points for bulls may be around $0.1280 and at the 76.4% Fibonacci retracement level of the aforementioned downward move. A critical resistance level is formed at $0.1300, where a close above it could potentially lead the price toward $0.1350 and beyond.

Conversely, if Dogecoin fails to surpass the $0.1300 level, further declines are likely. Initial support for DOGE is located near the $0.1200 mark, with subsequent major support levels at approximately $0.1195 and a crucial support level at $0.1150. Should the price break below this support zone, there is a risk of a further decline, possibly down to $0.1050 or even lower.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for DOGE/USD is currently gaining momentum within the bearish zone, reflecting a continued downward trend.
- **Hourly RSI (Relative Strength Index)**: The RSI for DOGE/USD is positioned above the 50 level, indicating a mixed sentiment in the market.
- **Major Support Levels**: The key support levels are identified at $0.1280 and $0.1250.
- **Major Resistance Levels**: The principal resistance levels are seen at $0.1340 and $0.1350.
