## Overview

Fin, a company established by two former engineers from Citadel, has successfully raised capital to develop a stablecoin application aimed at facilitating cross-border payments. This initiative comes at a time when banks and fintech companies are increasingly venturing into the realm of digital assets.

## What Happened

Fin has secured funding to launch its stablecoin payments application. The company was founded by individuals with a background in Citadel, a well-known financial institution. The rise of digital assets has prompted banks and fintechs to explore new opportunities, particularly in cross-border transactions. Fin's focus on stablecoin technology positions it within this expanding landscape, catering to the growing demand for efficient and secure payment solutions.
