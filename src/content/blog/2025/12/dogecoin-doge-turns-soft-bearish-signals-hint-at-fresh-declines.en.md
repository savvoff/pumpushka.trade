## Overview

Dogecoin has experienced a significant decline, dropping below the $0.1420 mark against the US Dollar. As the cryptocurrency consolidates its losses, it faces potential resistance levels that could hinder any recovery attempts. This bearish trend raises concerns for traders and investors in the crypto market.

## Recent Price Movements

Dogecoin's price began a downward trajectory after it closed below the $0.1465 level, following a similar trend observed in major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. The decline saw DOGE falling below key support levels of $0.1440 and $0.140, even trading down to a low of approximately $0.1363. Following this drop, there was a minor correction as the price experienced a slight increase above the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level, which indicates a partial recovery from the previous decline.

Currently, Dogecoin is trading below the $0.1420 mark and the 100-hourly simple moving average, suggesting persistent bearish sentiment. A key bearish trend line is forming with resistance at $0.1440 on the hourly chart of the DOGE/USD pair. If the price remains below both the $0.1420 and $0.1440 levels, it could continue to extend its losses.

## Resistance and Support Levels

If the price of Dogecoin attempts to recover, it faces immediate resistance near the $0.1425 level. The first major resistance for potential bulls lies around the $0.1440 area, coinciding with the key bearish trend line. Further resistance is anticipated near the $0.1490 level, which also aligns with the 76.4% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward movement from the $0.1530 swing high to the $0.1363 low. A close above the $0.1490 resistance could indicate a shift in momentum, potentially moving the price toward the $0.1530 resistance level.

Conversely, if DOGE fails to surpass the $0.1440 level, it may continue its descent. Initial support is observed near the $0.1380 level, followed by major support at approximately $0.1360. The primary support level is situated at $0.1320, and a break below this mark could lead to further declines, with potential targets around $0.1250 or even $0.1240.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for DOGE/USD is currently losing momentum within the bearish zone, indicating a weakening downward trend.
- **Hourly RSI (Relative Strength Index)**: The RSI for DOGE/USD is above the 50 level, suggesting that the asset is not yet oversold.

## From author

The current state of Dogecoin presents a challenging scenario for traders. The bearish trend, coupled with significant resistance levels, indicates that any upward movement may be short-lived unless a substantial shift occurs in market sentiment. Observing how DOGE responds to these resistance and support levels will be crucial for understanding its near-term trajectory.
