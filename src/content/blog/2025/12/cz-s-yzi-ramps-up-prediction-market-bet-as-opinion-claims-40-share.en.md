## Overview

The YZi Labs-backed prediction market known as "Opinion" has achieved significant traction shortly after its launch, recording notable weekly trading volume. This milestone highlights the growing interest in prediction markets within the crypto space.

## What Happened?

The prediction market platform "Opinion" has logged $1.5 billion in weekly trading volume. This impressive figure comes just weeks after the platform's launch. Notably, "Opinion" has outperformed other established prediction markets, such as Kalshi and Polymarket, indicating a robust demand for its offerings. The rapid growth of "Opinion" is a clear sign of the expanding market for prediction platforms, which allow users to speculate on various outcomes.
