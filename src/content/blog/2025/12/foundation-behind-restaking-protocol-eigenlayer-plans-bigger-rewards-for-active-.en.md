## Overview

EigenLayer, the foundation responsible for the restaking protocol, has announced plans to enhance rewards for active users. This initiative aims to better allocate resources and incentivize participation within the EigenCloud ecosystem.

## Incentives Committee and Token Emissions

The core of this new strategy involves the establishment of an Incentives Committee. This committee will oversee the programmatic emissions of tokens, ensuring that there is a structured approach to how rewards are distributed. The focus will be on participants who play a crucial role in securing Autonomous Validator Services (AVSs) and contributing to the broader EigenCloud ecosystem.

The decision to implement an Incentives Committee reflects a growing trend in decentralized finance (DeFi) and blockchain projects, where community engagement and active participation are vital for the success and sustainability of the ecosystem. By directing token emissions based on contributions, EigenLayer aims to create a more dynamic and responsive rewards system that aligns the interests of users with the long-term goals of the project.

Moreover, the emphasis on securing AVSs highlights the importance of these services within the EigenLayer framework. AVSs are integral for maintaining the integrity and performance of the network, so incentivizing users to secure these services can lead to a more robust and reliable ecosystem. This approach not only rewards users for their contributions but also encourages a more secure and efficient network operation.
