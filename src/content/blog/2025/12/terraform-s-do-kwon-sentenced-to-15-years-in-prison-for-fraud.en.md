## Overview

Do Kwon, the co-founder of Terraform Labs, has been sentenced to 15 years in prison following his guilty plea to charges of conspiracy and wire fraud. This significant development comes as a result of the legal actions taken against him in connection with his role in the collapse of the company’s cryptocurrency projects.

## The Legal Proceedings

In August, Do Kwon entered a guilty plea regarding the charges brought against him, which included conspiracy and wire fraud. These charges stemmed from allegations that he was involved in fraudulent activities that contributed to the downfall of Terraform Labs and its associated cryptocurrency projects.
