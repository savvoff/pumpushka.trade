## Overview

Ethereum has begun a significant upward movement, surpassing the $2,900 mark and showing potential for further gains as it approaches the $3,000 level. This price action is crucial for traders and investors, as it indicates a bullish trend and possible continuation of positive momentum in the market.

## Ethereum's Recent Price Movement

Ethereum's price has recently started a recovery wave after maintaining support above the $2,880 pivot level. This upward movement mirrors trends seen in Bitcoin, suggesting a broader market recovery. As the price climbed above the $2,920 resistance level, Ethereum entered a more favorable trading zone. 

Currently, Ethereum is trading above $2,950 and has surpassed the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a bullish trend. A bullish trend line is forming with support at $2,930 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD. If Ethereum can maintain its position above the $2,950 level, it may continue to rise.

Immediate resistance is observed near the $3,000 level, which coincides with the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent downward move from a swing high. The first significant resistance level lies around $3,030, followed by a major resistance at $3,050. A clear move above the $3,050 resistance could propel the price toward the $3,120 region, and further gains could lead to a target of around $3,200 or even $3,220 in the near term.

However, there is a cautionary note regarding potential declines. Should Ethereum fail to break through the $3,000 resistance, it could initiate a fresh decline. Initial support on the downside is located near $2,950, while the first major support sits near $2,920. A decisive move below $2,920 could push the price down toward the $2,880 support level, with further losses potentially leading to a decline toward the $2,800 area and a key support level at $2,720.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for ETH/USD is currently gaining momentum in the bullish zone, indicating positive sentiment among traders.
- **Hourly RSI**: The Relative Strength Index (RSI) for ETH/USD is above the 50 zone, further supporting the bullish outlook.
