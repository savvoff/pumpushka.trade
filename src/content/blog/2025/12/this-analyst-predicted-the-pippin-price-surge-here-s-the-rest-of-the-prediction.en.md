This surge followed a prolonged accumulation phase that lasted seven months, allowing the coin to establish a structural bottom conducive for expansion. The breakout that initiated this price movement allowed PIPPIN to overcome multiple resistance levels, marking what the analyst described as a significant bullish shift in the market.

The ability of PIPPIN to stay above these levels is seen as an indicator of sustained bullish momentum. Furthermore, the recent price action has shown signs of strong buying interest, characterized by rising volume and impulsive candles without sharp rejections, suggesting that the rally is not merely a liquidity grab.
