## Overview

Crypto analyst Cryptollica has identified a Dogecoin cycle fractal, suggesting a potential bullish trend for the meme coin. The analysis indicates that this may be an opportune moment for investors to consider acquiring Dogecoin, as the price could be on the verge of a significant upward movement.

## Dogecoin Cycle Fractal Analysis

In a recent post on X, Cryptollica shared insights regarding Dogecoin's market behavior, indicating that the cryptocurrency is currently positioned just before a potential bull run. The accompanying chart illustrated a cycle fractal that has previously mirrored Dogecoin's price movements at a macro level. This fractal highlights four distinct structural phases, which may be critical for understanding future price action.

Cryptollica specifically noted that Dogecoin is at Point 4 in this cycle fractal, suggesting a strong resemblance to historical accumulation phases preceding past bull runs. The analyst detailed the patterns observed in this fractal, beginning with the initial phases labeled as 'Rounding Bottoms.' Zones 1 and 2 represent periods of low volatility, characterized as "boredom phases," where smart money tends to accumulate assets. Zone 2 is particularly significant, as it served as the launchpad for Dogecoin's substantial price increase in 2021.

Currently, the price action in Zone 4 is indicative of a similar rounding-bottom formation. The analyst emphasized that Dogecoin is stabilizing and forming a robust base, akin to previous periods before notable price surges.

## Relative Strength Index Insights

In addition to the cycle fractal, Cryptollica analyzed Dogecoin's Relative Strength Index (RSI), pointing out that the 32 level has historically acted as a floor for the asset. Each time the weekly RSI has approached or hovered near this critical support level, Dogecoin has formed a macro bottom. The RSI has recently reset to this level, signifying potential exhaustion among sellers and positioning the asset for a momentum shift.

## The "Golden Pocket" for Accumulation

Further reinforcing the bullish outlook, Cryptollica highlighted that Dogecoin is currently situated in what is referred to as the "Golden Pocket" for accumulation. This term suggests that the current price action is not random but part of a cyclical reset. If the fractal continues to play out as it did in 2020, the current price behavior may be merely a calm before an impending surge.

The analyst reiterated that a bullish rounding bottom is forming, with the RSI indicating an oversold condition, which is traditionally viewed as a favorable buying opportunity. Cryptollica described the market conditions as a "spring loading," implying that a significant price movement could be on the horizon, although patience is required.
