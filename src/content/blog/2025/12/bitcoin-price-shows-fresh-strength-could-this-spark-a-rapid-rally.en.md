## Overview

Bitcoin has demonstrated notable strength, recently rising above $92,000 and consolidating its gains. This uptick is significant as it highlights potential bullish momentum, particularly if Bitcoin can surpass key resistance levels.

## Bitcoin's Recent Price Action

Bitcoin's price action indicates a recent increase above $92,000, with a potential aim to continue climbing if it successfully breaks through the $93,400 resistance level. Following a downside correction from the $94,500 zone, Bitcoin is currently trading above $92,000 and the 100 hourly Simple Moving Average. A bullish trend line has formed, providing support at $91,500 on the hourly chart of the BTC/USD pair.

The price managed to hold above the $91,000 zone and initiated a fresh upward movement. Bitcoin gained momentum, surpassing both the $92,500 and $94,000 levels. However, selling pressure emerged near the $94,500 mark, where a high of $94,583 was recorded before a price correction occurred. This correction saw a decline toward the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the upward movement from a swing low of $89,545 to the high of $94,583. Despite this pullback, buying interest remained robust near the $92,000 support level.

Currently, Bitcoin is trading below the $92,000 mark and the 100 hourly Simple Moving Average, with the bullish trend line still intact. If buying pressure persists, the price could attempt another rally. Immediate resistance is observed near the $92,800 level, while key resistance levels are positioned at $93,200 and $94,000. A close above the $94,000 resistance could potentially lead to further price increases, with targets set at $94,500 and possibly $95,500. Additional barriers for the bulls could emerge at $96,200 and $96,500.

Conversely, if Bitcoin fails to break through the $94,000 resistance zone, it may initiate another decline. Immediate support is near the $92,000 level, followed by major support at $91,500, which coincides with the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level of the prior upward move. Should the price drop further, the next support could be near the $90,750 zone, with significant support sitting at $88,800.
