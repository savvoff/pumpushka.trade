This change is significant as traders explore Layer 2 infrastructure solutions to enhance Bitcoin's performance.

## Bitcoin Trading Strategies

The market is witnessing a transformation in how traders approach Bitcoin. Notably, the decision by Strategy to keep Bitcoin sales "on the table" signals a broader evolution in trading tactics. This shift suggests that even long-term Bitcoin holders are now incorporating more active risk management into their strategies. 

As Bitcoin's base layer faces challenges related to low throughput and high fees, traders are increasingly looking at Layer 2 solutions. These solutions aim to provide leveraged expressions of Bitcoin's potential upside. Bitcoin Hyper, in particular, targets the speed and programmability issues associated with Bitcoin by utilizing an SVM-powered Layer 2 infrastructure.

The emergence of "green dots" in trading signals a notable change in conviction among long-term accumulators. While some traders interpret this as a cue for more Bitcoin purchases, others see it as an opportunity for asset restructuring or buybacks. This nuanced approach allows traders to maintain a core Bitcoin position while also exploring high-beta ecosystem plays.

## The Role of Bitcoin Hyper

Bitcoin Hyper is positioned as a solution to the limitations of Bitcoin. It combines Bitcoin's liquidity and security with the performance capabilities of a Solana Virtual Machine (SVM) Layer 2. This integration aims for fast transaction confirmations and high throughput, addressing the slow block times and fluctuating fees that Bitcoin users often encounter.

The system includes a Canonical Bridge, which facilitates secure transfers of Bitcoin into the Layer 2 ecosystem. This feature ensures that Bitcoin can be utilized for high-speed transactions, lending, and staking without undermining its foundational role as a settlement asset.
