## Overview

XRP is currently trading in a zone reminiscent of the conditions preceding its last significant breakout, attracting attention from market analysts. Key indicators suggest that selling pressure is diminishing, while long-term holders seem to be steadily absorbing the available supply, hinting at a potential shift in market dynamics.

## What Happened

XRP has found itself in a familiar trading zone, which has prompted discussions among market watchers about a possible upcoming breakout. Analysts have noted a significant technical development on XRP's high-timeframe charts. Specifically, the Stochastic RSI on the 3-week interval has dropped to a value of 0.00. This level indicates a state of total compression for the oscillator and signals a rare momentum shift within the asset's trading history.

The occurrence of this particular signal is noteworthy, as it has only happened once before during the depths of the 2022 bear market. When the Stochastic RSI reaches zero, it typically marks the exhaustion of prevailing selling pressure. This suggests that the energy behind the downward trend has diminished significantly. However, it is crucial to understand that this does not ensure an immediate price recovery.

In the past, similar technical phenomena have led to prolonged accumulation phases. During these phases, prices stabilize as strategic investors, often referred to as "smart money," begin to build positions. This accumulation creates a solid foundation for potential upward movements in the future. The current situation indicates that XRP's downside risk is limited at its present valuation, suggesting a transition from a distribution phase to one of strategic accumulation.

## From Author

The current trading conditions of XRP, characterized by a unique technical signal and notable market patterns, have led to an intriguing narrative among traders and analysts. The re-emergence of the Stochastic RSI at such a critical level could be interpreted as a warning sign for those still holding short positions. The similarities between the current market cycle and the notable 2016–2017 cycle raise questions about whether XRP is on the verge of another major rally. The ongoing correction appears to align with previous patterns, indicating that XRP might be nearing the end of a challenging shakeout phase.
