## Overview

Disney has recently taken a stand against Google, alleging that the tech giant's artificial intelligence (AI) has utilized its characters without authorization. This move comes alongside Disney's announcement of a new partnership with OpenAI, which will enable character generation through a platform named Sora.

## Allegations Against Google

Disney's allegations against Google center around the unauthorized use of its intellectual property, specifically its iconic characters. The company claims that Google's AI has incorporated these characters without obtaining the necessary permissions, raising significant concerns about copyright violations in the rapidly evolving landscape of AI technology. This dispute highlights the ongoing challenges that content creators face in protecting their intellectual property as AI systems become increasingly capable of generating and manipulating creative works.

The implications of these allegations extend beyond just Disney and Google. As AI technologies advance, the potential for misuse of copyrighted material has become a pressing issue within the entertainment industry. Disney's actions signal a broader concern among content creators about the need for clearer regulations and protections against unauthorized use of their intellectual property in AI applications.

## New Deal with OpenAI

In a significant development, Disney has also signed a deal with OpenAI. This partnership is aimed at leveraging OpenAI's technology to facilitate character generation through a platform named Sora. This collaboration represents a strategic move by Disney to embrace AI in a way that aligns with its interests, enabling the company to generate content while maintaining control over its characters and intellectual property.

The Sora platform is expected to provide Disney with the tools necessary to create new and innovative content involving its beloved characters. By working with OpenAI, Disney aims to harness the power of AI to enhance its creative processes and explore new storytelling possibilities. This partnership may also serve as a countermeasure to the concerns raised by the allegations against Google, as it demonstrates Disney's proactive approach to utilizing AI in a manner that respects its copyrights.
