## Overview

Bitcoin experienced a sudden decline, falling below 88,000 as the weekly close approached. This downturn is attributed to heightened nerves surrounding an upcoming Federal Reserve interest-rate decision.

## What Happened

As the week drew to a close, Bitcoin's price dipped, bringing the $87,000 mark back into focus. Analysts suggest that this price movement is closely linked to the uncertainty surrounding the Federal Reserve's upcoming interest-rate decision. Market participants are often sensitive to such economic indicators, leading to fluctuations in cryptocurrency prices.
