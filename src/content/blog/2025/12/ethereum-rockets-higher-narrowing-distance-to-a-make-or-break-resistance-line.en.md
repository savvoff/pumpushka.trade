## Overview

Ethereum's price has begun a notable ascent, surpassing key resistance levels and currently aiming to break through a significant barrier. This movement is crucial as it could dictate future price trends for the cryptocurrency.

## Recent Price Movement

Ethereum's price has initiated a fresh increase, moving above the $2,920 and $2,950 levels. It is currently trading above the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a positive trend. A break occurred above a short-term bearish trend line that had resistance at $2,825 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD.

The price has now reached a critical point, testing the $3,050 resistance level after forming a high at $3,047. Currently, Ethereum is consolidating above the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent move from a swing low of $2,718 to the $3,047 high. 

If Ethereum manages to settle above the $3,050 zone, it could potentially continue to rise. The next resistance levels are noted at $3,080 and $3,120, with a significant upside move above $3,120 possibly leading to further gains.

## Potential Decline

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to overcome the $3,050 resistance, it may begin to decline. Initial support is identified near the $2,970 level, while the first major support is around the $2,880 zone or the 50% Fibonacci retracement level from the recent swing low. A clear decline below the $2,840 support could push the price down toward the $2,800 support level, with further losses potentially leading to the $2,750 region.
