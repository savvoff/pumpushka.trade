## Overview

Grayscale has provided insights into Bitcoin's market trajectory, suggesting that the sell-off experienced in 2025 appears to be a local bottom rather than an indication of a new cycle peak. The firm highlights the importance of Federal Reserve policy and upcoming U.S. cryptocurrency legislation as critical factors influencing Bitcoin's performance in 2026.

## Analysis of Current Market Conditions

Grayscale's assessment indicates that the recent downturn in Bitcoin's price is not a signal of a long-term decline. Instead, it suggests that the market may be poised for a recovery. The firm emphasizes that the Federal Reserve's monetary policy will play a significant role in shaping the market environment for Bitcoin in the coming year.

Additionally, the potential impact of U.S. cryptocurrency bills is noted as a crucial element that could affect investor sentiment and market dynamics. These legislative developments may provide clarity and regulatory framework, which could foster a more favorable environment for cryptocurrency investments.
