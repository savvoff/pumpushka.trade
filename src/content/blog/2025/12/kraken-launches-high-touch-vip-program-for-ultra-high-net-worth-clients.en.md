## Overview

Kraken has introduced a new VIP program aimed at ultra high net worth clients. This initiative is designed to provide personalized support and exclusive access to the full range of Kraken's offerings.

## Program Details

The Kraken VIP program pairs members with a specialist relationship manager. This professional is dedicated to catering to the unique needs of high net worth individuals. Additionally, members benefit from 24/7 support, ensuring they have assistance whenever necessary. The program also offers early insights into Kraken’s complete product ecosystem, allowing members to stay ahead of developments and opportunities within the platform.
