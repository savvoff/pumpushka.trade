## Overview

XRP has recently experienced a decline, dropping below the $2.00 mark and facing significant resistance near the $2.020 level. This price movement is crucial as it indicates the cryptocurrency's struggle to maintain upward momentum amidst bearish market conditions.

## Recent Price Movements

XRP price initiated a fresh decline, moving below the $2.00 threshold. The current trading position is below both the $2.00 level and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. This decline is further characterized by the formation of a bearish trend line, which presents resistance at the $2.020 level on the hourly chart of the XRP/USD pair, as reported by Kraken.

In its recent attempts to recover, XRP briefly rose above $2.120 but was unable to sustain this upward trajectory. This failure is notable, especially as it mirrors the struggles of other major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. Following this attempt, XRP moved below critical support levels of $2.050 and $2.020, eventually reaching a low of $1.9525. Although there was a subsequent upward correction, the price remains under pressure from bearish forces around the $2.00 and $2.020 levels.

The technical indicators suggest a challenging environment for XRP. The price is currently trading below the $2.00 mark and is under the influence of a bearish trend line. If XRP does not surpass the $2.020 resistance, it risks further declines. Initial support levels are identified at approximately $1.9650, with major support situated near the $1.950 level. Should the price close below this level, it could potentially decline further towards $1.920, and even lower to the $1.880 zone.
