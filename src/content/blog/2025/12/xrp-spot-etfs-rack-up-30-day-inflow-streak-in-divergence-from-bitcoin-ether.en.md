XRP spot ETFs have demonstrated a remarkable trend by accumulating fresh capital every trading day since their inception. This consistent inflow has resulted in a cumulative net inflow totaling approximately $975 million. The sustained demand for these products indicates a significant investor interest in XRP, suggesting a shift in market dynamics.

The significance of this inflow streak cannot be understated. This divergence raises questions about the factors driving investor sentiment and the potential implications for the broader cryptocurrency landscape.
