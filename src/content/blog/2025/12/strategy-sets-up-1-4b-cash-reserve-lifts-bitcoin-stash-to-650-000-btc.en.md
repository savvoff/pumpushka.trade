## Overview

A strategy has been implemented that has resulted in the establishment of a substantial cash reserve and an increase in Bitcoin holdings. This move is significant amid the prevailing market volatility.

## Details of the Strategy

The strategy involved funding a new cash reserve through stock sales. This reserve is intended to cover at least 12 months of dividends, ensuring financial stability and liquidity for the company. Alongside this, the strategy has also led to an increase in the company's Bitcoin holdings, raising the total to 650,000 coins. This accumulation of Bitcoin highlights a proactive approach to asset management in uncertain market conditions.
