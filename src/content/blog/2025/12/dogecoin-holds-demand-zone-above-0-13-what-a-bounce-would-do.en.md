## Overview

As 2025 approaches its conclusion, analysts are closely examining the price trajectory of Dogecoin, particularly in light of recent market movements. ## Current Market Situation

BitGuru's analysis highlights that Dogecoin has found support above $0.13, indicating that demand at this price level remains robust. Following a recent downturn, the price stabilization above this threshold suggests that buyers are returning to the market, which is a positive sign for the cryptocurrency's future. The analyst points out that this demand zone is crucial, especially after a prolonged downtrend, as it reflects a rising interest in Dogecoin and the establishment of new support levels.

The formation of this base, as described by BitGuru, could serve as a launching point for a potential rally in Dogecoin's price. For such a rally to materialize, it is essential for Dogecoin bulls to maintain their position above the identified demand level. If this support is upheld, there is speculation that Dogecoin could begin to recover, potentially leading to significant price increases in the near term.

However, the recent performance of Dogecoin has not been encouraging. The last quarter of the year has historically been a bullish period for the meme coin, but 2025 has deviated from this trend. Currently, the quarter is reported to be 41.8% down, indicating a challenging market environment. In December alone, Dogecoin has seen a decline of over 7.5%, contributing to the overall downturn that has characterized the previous months.

October and November also reflected negative trends, with losses of 20% and 21.3%, respectively. If the current trend persists, it is likely that Dogecoin's price may continue to struggle as the year comes to a close.
