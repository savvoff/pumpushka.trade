## Overview

Coinbase and Robinhood have joined a newly formed initiative known as the "Tech Force." This initiative aims to recruit around 1,000 engineers to develop artificial intelligence (AI) infrastructure for various federal agencies in the United States. The collaboration signifies a notable intersection between the tech industry and government efforts to enhance technological capabilities.

## What Happened

The "Tech Force" initiative, which includes participation from Coinbase and Robinhood, is focused on addressing the growing demand for advanced AI capabilities within the U.S. government. By recruiting approximately 1,000 engineers, the initiative aims to bolster the technical expertise available to federal agencies, enabling them to better harness the potential of AI technologies.

The involvement of major players like Coinbase and Robinhood highlights the commitment of the tech industry to collaborate with government entities. This partnership is critical as it reflects a recognition of the importance of AI in modern governance and public service. The initiative is expected to facilitate the development of AI infrastructure that can improve efficiency, enhance decision-making, and ultimately lead to better service delivery for the public.

The recruitment of engineers is a strategic move, as the demand for skilled professionals in AI and related fields is rapidly increasing. By directing talent toward federal agencies, the initiative aims to ensure that the government is well-equipped to navigate the complexities and challenges posed by technological advancements.
