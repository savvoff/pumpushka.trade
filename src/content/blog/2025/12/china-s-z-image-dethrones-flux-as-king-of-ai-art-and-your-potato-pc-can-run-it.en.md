## Overview

China's Z-Image model has emerged as a dominant force in the AI art landscape, surpassing the capabilities of Flux2. Notably, Z-Image can operate effectively on hardware from 2019, which has generated considerable excitement within the open-source community.

## What Happened

The Z-Image model boasts six billion parameters, allowing it to outperform its predecessor, Flux2, in generating AI art. This advancement is particularly significant as it can run on older hardware, making it accessible to a broader audience. The ability to utilize such a powerful model on less advanced technology has sparked enthusiasm and engagement among enthusiasts and developers alike.
