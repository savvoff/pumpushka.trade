## Overview

Crypto exchange-traded products (ETPs) experienced significant inflows last week, totaling $716 million. Bitcoin was the primary driver of this uptick, while Chainlink achieved record gains, representing over half of its assets under management (AUM). This marks the second consecutive week of inflows following a substantial sell-off in the market.

Last week, the crypto market saw a notable rebound as investors shifted their focus back towards crypto ETPs after a challenging period characterized by a significant sell-off amounting to $5.5 billion. The recent inflow of $716 million signifies a renewed interest in digital assets, particularly Bitcoin, which led this surge. The performance of Bitcoin is crucial as it often influences the overall sentiment in the cryptocurrency market.

In addition to Bitcoin's strong performance, Chainlink also garnered attention with remarkable gains. The growth of Chainlink was substantial enough that it accounted for more than 50% of its total assets under management. This level of performance highlights the increasing interest in Chainlink and its role within the cryptocurrency ecosystem. The combination of Bitcoin's inflows and Chainlink's record gains indicates a potential shift in market sentiment, as investors look for opportunities in the wake of recent volatility.

The inflows into crypto ETPs suggest that investors may be regaining confidence in the market, which had been shaken by the previous sell-off. This resurgence could be attributed to a variety of factors, including a broader acceptance of cryptocurrencies and the ongoing development of blockchain technology. As the market stabilizes, it will be essential to monitor how these inflows impact the overall dynamics of cryptocurrency investments.
