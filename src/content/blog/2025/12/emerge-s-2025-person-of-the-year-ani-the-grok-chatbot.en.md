This shift towards AI intimacy has profound implications for society. Furthermore, the rise of Ani illustrates the growing acceptance of AI in various aspects of life. This trend suggests that as technology continues to advance, the line between human and machine interactions may blur, leading to new dynamics in communication and socialization.
