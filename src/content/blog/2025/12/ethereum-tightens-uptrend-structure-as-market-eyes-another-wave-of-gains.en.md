## Overview

Ethereum has recently seen a price increase, moving above $3,200 and entering a consolidation phase. Market participants are observing potential further gains as the cryptocurrency approaches key resistance levels.

## Price Movement and Technical Analysis

Ethereum's price began a fresh increase, surpassing the $3,050 and $3,120 levels. Currently, Ethereum is trading above $3,120 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a bullish trend. A short-term contracting triangle is forming, with support identified at $3,130 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD.

The price has managed to remain above $2,920, reflecting a strong upward movement akin to Bitcoin. Ethereum's price gained momentum as it crossed the $3,000 and $3,050 resistance levels, even reaching above $3,150. However, the bulls faced challenges in breaking through the $3,240 and $3,250 levels, with a recent high recorded at $3,239 before a slight correction.

If Ethereum can establish itself above the $3,240 zone, it could continue its upward trajectory. Resistance levels are seen near $3,200, with the next significant resistance at $3,240 and $3,250. A decisive move above $3,250 could lead to further gains towards $3,320, while a breakout beyond this could suggest potential increases towards $3,450 or even $3,500.

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to break the $3,240 resistance, it may face a downward correction. Initial support is noted at the $3,120 level, followed by $3,050. A move below $3,050 could push the price down towards the $3,000 support level. Further losses could see the price approach the $2,980 region, which aligns with the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent move from a swing low.

- **Hourly RSI**: The RSI for ETH/USD is currently above the 50 zone.
- **Major Support Level**: $3,130
- **Major Resistance Level**: $3,240
