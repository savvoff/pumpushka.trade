## Overview

ETHZilla has made a significant move by acquiring 20% of the AI lending platform Karus in a deal valued at $10 million. This partnership aims to tokenize auto loans, with the initial portfolios projected to be released by early 2026.

## What Happened

In a strategic investment, ETHZilla has purchased a minority stake in Karus, an AI-driven lending platform. The focus of this collaboration is to innovate the auto loan sector through tokenization. This approach is expected to enhance the efficiency and accessibility of auto loans by leveraging blockchain technology.

The tokenization of auto loans could potentially transform traditional lending practices, making it easier for consumers to access financing solutions. The companies are working towards launching the first portfolios by early 2026, indicating a clear timeline for their project.

## Impact on the crypto market

- The investment highlights the growing intersection between blockchain technology and traditional finance.
- Tokenizing auto loans could attract more participants to the crypto market, particularly those interested in decentralized finance (DeFi).
- This partnership may signal increased institutional interest in blockchain applications beyond cryptocurrencies.
- The development of AI-driven lending platforms could pave the way for more innovative financial products in the crypto space.
- The timeline for portfolio availability may influence market sentiment and expectations around future tokenization initiatives.
