## Overview

Fanatics, a prominent player in the sports clothing and collectibles industry, has recently introduced Fanatics Markets. This new platform allows users to engage in trading outcomes related to sports, politics, and more. Additionally, the company has plans to expand its offerings to include cryptocurrency and IPO bets by 2026.

## What Happened

Fanatics has officially launched its prediction market application, Fanatics Markets, which is currently available in ten states. This platform enables users to predict and trade on various outcomes, diversifying the ways fans can interact with their favorite sports and political events. The introduction of this app signifies a notable expansion into the prediction market sector, a space that has been gaining traction in the broader financial landscape.

The company's future plans include the integration of crypto and IPO betting options, set to roll out by 2026. This move highlights Fanatics' commitment to evolving its offerings and tapping into emerging market trends, potentially attracting a wider audience of users interested in prediction markets.
