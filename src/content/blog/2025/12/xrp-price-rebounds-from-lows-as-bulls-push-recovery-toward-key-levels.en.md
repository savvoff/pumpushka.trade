## Overview

XRP has begun a recovery phase after hitting a low at $1.9840. The price has moved above $2.120 and is currently facing resistance near the $2.250 level, marking a significant shift in market momentum.

## Price Movement and Technical Analysis

XRP price started its recovery from the $1.9840 zone, demonstrating resilience as it trades above $2.120 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. The price action indicates a break above a key bearish trend line that had resistance at $2.150 on the hourly chart of the XRP/USD pair.

During its recent decline, XRP fell below the $2.050 support level, similar to trends observed in Bitcoin and Ethereum. The price even dipped below $2.00 before the bulls stepped in, forming a low at $1.9844. Following this, the price managed to recover by surpassing both the $2.050 and $2.120 levels.

Furthermore, XRP has moved above the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward move from a previous swing high to the recent low. This bullish momentum has allowed the price to rise above $2.150 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average.

If XRP maintains its upward trajectory, potential resistance levels are anticipated near $2.20 and the 76.4% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward move. The first major resistance is identified at $2.250, and a close above this level could lead to further upward movement toward $2.320. Additional resistance levels are noted at $2.350 and $2.40, while the next significant hurdle for the bulls may be around $2.50.

Conversely, if XRP fails to surpass the $2.250 resistance zone, it may initiate a decline. Initial support is near the $2.120 level, followed by a major support level at $2.10. A break below $2.10 could lead to a continued drop toward $2.050, with further downside potential toward the $2.00 zone and possibly $1.920.

### Technical Indicators
- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for XRP/USD is gaining momentum in the bullish zone.
- **Hourly RSI**: The RSI for XRP/USD is currently above the 50 level.
- **Major Support Levels**: $2.120 and $2.10.
- **Major Resistance Levels**: $2.20 and $2.250.
