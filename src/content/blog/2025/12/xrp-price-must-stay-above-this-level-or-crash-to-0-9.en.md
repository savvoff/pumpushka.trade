## Overview

A recent analysis highlights a critical support level for XRP, which, if breached, could lead to a significant decline in its price. The findings indicate rising volatility and a cautious market sentiment that may impact both short-term traders and long-term investors.

## XRP Price Faces Significant Support Challenge

A crypto analyst known as ‘Guy on the Earth’ has shared insights regarding XRP's current market position, emphasizing the importance of a specific price level for the cryptocurrency's future. According to the analysis, XRP has recently closed below the $1.95 monthly support zone, marking the first occurrence of this in 13 months. This development raises concerns about the potential for a sharp sell-off, with implications for market participants.

The breakdown below the $1.95 support level is particularly significant as it suggests an increased downside risk for XRP. The analyst noted that this situation mirrors previous market stress, specifically referencing a similar drop that occurred in April due to U.S. tariff-related pressures. The historical context serves as a cautionary tale, indicating that failure to hold the current support level could lead to a downturn, pushing XRP's price down toward $0.90.

To stabilize its price, XRP must reclaim the $1.95 level promptly. The analysis indicates that an attempt to recover above this threshold was recently rejected, resulting in the formation of a lower high that reinforces a bearish market structure. If the price does not recover soon, the analyst warns that XRP's downside momentum may intensify.

For traders who are wary of the current market conditions, the analyst recommends reducing exposure until a confirmed daily close above the $1.95 level occurs. This strategy aims to limit potential losses while keeping options open for re-entry into the market should a recovery materialize.

In the event of continued price declines, the analyst has identified potential accumulation zones, which include key levels at $1.61, $1.42, and the $0.90 target. The initial breakdown area from the previous rally is noted at $0.75. Furthermore, increased selling pressure from Bitcoin could exacerbate downward moves for XRP.

## Analyst's Perspective on Recovery Potential

Despite the bearish outlook, the analyst maintains that a bullish recovery is still possible. He points out that XRP is just under $0.04 from a rectangle resistance level and that Bullish Divergence has yet to manifest across multiple timeframes. This suggests that while the current price action appears concerning, the potential for a recovery and subsequent rally remains viable as sellers are reportedly becoming exhausted.

Nevertheless, caution is advised, especially considering the recent consecutive weekly closes below crucial support levels.
