## Overview

Ethereum has begun a recovery wave above a key price level, showing signs of potential upward movement. The cryptocurrency is currently consolidating, and if it can surpass a specific resistance level, it may continue its upward trajectory.

## Current Price Action

Ethereum's price initiated a notable increase from a lower level, moving above the $2,950 mark and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. The bulls have managed to push the price past the $2,850 and $2,880 resistance levels, indicating a shift into a positive zone. This upward movement comes after a significant decline from a previous swing high, suggesting a recovery phase for the asset.

A critical aspect of this recovery is the break above a bearish trend line, which had resistance at $2,920 on the hourly chart of ETH/USD. As Ethereum now trades above $2,950, it is poised for further gains if it can settle above the $3,050 zone. Should the price continue to rise, it may encounter resistance near the $3,020 level and the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level of the previous downward movement.

The immediate next resistance level to watch is around $3,050, followed by another key resistance near $3,080. A clear move above $3,080 could catalyze further upward momentum, potentially leading to price levels around $3,150 and beyond. In this scenario, Ether could aim for higher resistance zones, possibly approaching $3,220 or even $3,250 in the near term.

## Potential Decline

Conversely, if Ethereum fails to breach the $3,050 resistance, it could face a fresh decline. The initial support level on the downside is positioned near $2,950, with the first major support located around $2,915. A decisive move below $2,915 might trigger further downward pressure, potentially pushing the price toward the $2,880 support level. Should losses continue, the next key support level to monitor is $2,800.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD**: The MACD for ETH/USD is losing momentum while still in the bullish zone, indicating a potential weakening of the current upward trend.
- **Hourly RSI**: The RSI for ETH/USD is currently above the 50 zone, suggesting that the asset is in a bullish phase.
