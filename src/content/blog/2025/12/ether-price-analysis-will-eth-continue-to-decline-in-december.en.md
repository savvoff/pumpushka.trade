Additionally, the presence of a bearish pennant pattern signals a possible continuation of the downward trend.

## Why It Matters

The potential decline of Ether could have implications for traders and investors, as the cryptocurrency market often reacts sharply to such price movements. The weakening of MVRV support may suggest a loss of confidence among holders, while the bearish pennant formation indicates that sellers may gain the upper hand in the near term.
