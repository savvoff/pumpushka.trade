## Overview

XRP has recently experienced a significant decline in price, struggling to maintain upward momentum after failing to break through key resistance levels. This downturn is notable as it reflects broader market challenges that may affect investor sentiment and trading strategies.

## Price Movements and Technical Analysis

XRP price initially attempted to recover above the $1.950 mark but was unable to sustain its gains, leading to a sharp decline. Currently, the price is struggling below the $1.880 level and has encountered resistance near $1.90. A bearish trend line is forming, indicating potential further declines, with resistance noted at $1.9350 on the hourly chart for the XRP/USD pair.

After a failed recovery attempt above the $1.920 level, XRP's price has continuously dropped, breaching the $1.90 and $1.880 thresholds. The price fell below the support level of $1.8650, reaching a low of $1.8473. This decline has placed XRP below the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent downward movement, further signaling bearish trends.

As the price trades below both $1.90 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, the outlook for XRP appears increasingly negative. If the price does not manage to rebound, it may continue to decline, particularly if it settles below the critical $1.850 support level. Should a downward break occur below $1.8320, further declines towards $1.80 and potentially $1.720 could follow.

On the resistance side, XRP faces immediate hurdles at the $1.880 level. The first significant resistance is at $1.9150, which corresponds to the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent downward movement. A close above this level could provide a path toward $1.9350 and the bearish trend line. If the price manages to break through the $1.950 resistance, it may target $2.00 and potentially higher levels.
