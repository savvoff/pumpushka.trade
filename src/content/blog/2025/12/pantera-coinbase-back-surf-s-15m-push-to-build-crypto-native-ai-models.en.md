## Overview

Pantera Capital and Coinbase have jointly backed Surf in a funding round aimed at raising $15 million. This investment will facilitate the development of Surf's upcoming AI model, which is designed to enhance on-chain analysis and streamline research processes for crypto companies and traders.

## What Happened

Surf has secured a significant funding boost from notable investors, including Pantera Capital and Coinbase. The $15 million raised will be allocated towards the development of a new AI model that focuses on providing in-depth on-chain analysis. This model is expected to cater specifically to the needs of crypto companies and traders, automating research tasks that are crucial for decision-making in the rapidly evolving cryptocurrency market.

The emphasis on on-chain analysis is particularly important in the crypto space, where understanding blockchain data can provide insights into market trends, investor behavior, and potential investment opportunities. By automating research processes, Surf aims to enable companies and traders to access critical information more efficiently, potentially leading to more informed trading strategies and business decisions.

The involvement of established players like Pantera Capital and Coinbase underscores the growing interest in the intersection of artificial intelligence and blockchain technology. As AI continues to evolve, its application in the crypto sector could transform how data is analyzed and utilized, ultimately affecting trading behaviors and market dynamics.
