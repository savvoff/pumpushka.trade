## Overview

Ethereum has recently experienced a fresh increase, surpassing the $3,000 mark and showing signs of potential further gains. Currently, the price is consolidating, with key resistance levels identified that could influence the next movements in the market.

## Recent Price Movements

Ethereum's price began an upward trajectory, breaking through the $3,000 and $3,020 resistance levels. As of now, it is trading above $3,050 and the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average, indicating a bullish trend. However, a key bearish trend line has formed, presenting resistance at the $3,140 mark on the hourly chart of ETH/USD.

Despite a recent setback from the $3,240 zone, where the price witnessed a correction, Ethereum has managed to maintain a position above $2,920. The bulls have remained active in this vicinity, allowing the price to stabilize above the critical thresholds. Should Ethereum manage to settle above the $3,150 zone, it could pave the way for further upward movement.

## Resistance and Support Levels

The current technical analysis indicates that if Ethereum continues its upward trajectory, it may encounter resistance near the $3,140 level due to the established bearish trend line. Following this, the next significant resistance levels are positioned at $3,200 and $3,250. A decisive move above the $3,250 resistance could potentially lead to a surge toward the $3,320 region.

Conversely, should Ethereum fail to breach the $3,140 resistance, it may initiate a decline. Initial support is noted at the $3,050 level, with the first major support situated near the $3,000 zone. A clear drop below this threshold could direct the price toward the $2,950 support area, with further losses potentially leading to the $2,920 region and the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level from the upward wave that began at $2,718 and peaked at $3,240.

## Technical Indicators

- **Hourly MACD:** The MACD for ETH/USD is currently gaining momentum within the bullish zone, indicating a positive outlook.
- **Hourly RSI:** The Relative Strength Index (RSI) for ETH/USD has risen above the 50 mark, further supporting the bullish sentiment.
