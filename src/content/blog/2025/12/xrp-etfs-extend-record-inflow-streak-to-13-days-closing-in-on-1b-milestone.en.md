## Overview

U.S. spot XRP exchange-traded funds (ETFs) have achieved a remarkable milestone, extending their record inflow streak to 13 consecutive days. This sustained interest has brought the total inflows close to a significant benchmark.

## Recent Developments

On December 3, U.S. spot XRP ETFs experienced a net inflow of $50.27 million. This latest influx has contributed to a cumulative total of $874.28 million in net inflows over the streak. The continuous inflow highlights growing investor interest in XRP ETFs, reflecting a broader trend in the cryptocurrency market.

## Why It Matters

The ongoing inflow into XRP ETFs indicates a strong demand for this particular asset class. As investors increasingly turn to ETFs as a way to gain exposure to cryptocurrencies, the record inflow streak signifies confidence in XRP's potential. This trend could influence market sentiment and pave the way for further developments in the cryptocurrency sector.
