## Overview

VanEck has submitted a new filing for an Avalanche ETF, aiming to include staking rewards for AVAX investors. This move highlights the growing interest in integrating staking mechanisms within cryptocurrency investment products.

## Details of the ETF Filing

The newly proposed Avalanche ETF by VanEck is set to utilize Coinbase Crypto Services as its initial staking provider. This partnership will facilitate the staking of AVAX, the native token of the Avalanche blockchain. By employing Coinbase, VanEck aims to leverage the established infrastructure and expertise of a major player in the crypto services sector.

A significant aspect of this ETF is its fee structure. The fund will incur a 4% service fee associated with the staking process. This fee is standard in the industry and reflects the costs of managing staking activities through a third-party provider. Notably, the rewards generated from staking will accrue to the fund itself. This means that investors in the Avalanche ETF can expect these rewards to be reflected in the net asset value of the fund.

The decision to include staking rewards is particularly relevant in the current cryptocurrency landscape, where staking has become a popular method for investors to earn passive income on their holdings. By integrating staking rewards into the ETF, VanEck is positioning the fund to attract investors who are looking for more than just price appreciation of AVAX.
