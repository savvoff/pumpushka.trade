## Overview

XRP has recently experienced a significant decline in its price, failing to maintain momentum above key resistance levels. This downward movement raises concerns about the asset's stability and potential future performance in the market.

## Price Decline and Current Position

XRP price struggled to surpass the $1.950 mark, leading to a notable decline. Following this, the price entered a corrective phase and is currently finding it challenging to remain above the $1.850 level. The asset has tested the $1.850 zone and is now trading below $1.880, which is also below the 100-hourly Simple Moving Average. 

Additionally, there is a bearish trend line forming, with resistance identified at $1.870 on the hourly chart for the XRP/USD pair. The recent price action mirrors broader trends in the cryptocurrency market, as XRP's decline coincides with similar downturns seen in Bitcoin and Ethereum.

XRP's price began its latest downward correction after failing to maintain levels above $1.9350. It has now dipped below both the $1.90 and $1.880 thresholds, indicating a shift into a negative trading zone. The price has also fallen below the 50% Fibonacci retracement level of the upward move from a swing low of $1.770 to a high of $1.9578, further emphasizing the bearish sentiment.

Resistance levels are crucial for any potential upward movements. If XRP manages to recover, it might face significant resistance around $1.870, coinciding with the trend line. The first major resistance level is at $1.880, and should the price break above this, it could test the $1.90 level. A decisive move beyond the $1.90 resistance could signal a potential rally toward the $1.950 area, with further gains possibly pushing the price toward the $2.00 level.

Conversely, if XRP fails to clear the $1.90 resistance zone, it may initiate another decline. The initial support level is around $1.8420, which corresponds with the 61.8% Fibonacci retracement level of the recent upward move. Should the price decline further, it could approach the key support level of $1.80. A close below this level may lead to more significant losses, with the next major support level being around $1.770, followed by $1.750 and potentially $1.720.
