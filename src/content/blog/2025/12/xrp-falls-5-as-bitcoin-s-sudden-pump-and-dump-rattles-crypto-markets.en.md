## Overview

XRP experienced a decline of 5% amidst a turbulent period in the cryptocurrency market, primarily influenced by Bitcoin's recent volatility. This sudden shift in Bitcoin's price dynamics has created ripples across various cryptocurrencies, prompting concerns among investors and traders.

## XRP's Price Action

XRP's current price trajectory has encountered significant resistance at levels that were previously viewed as support. The immediate line of defense for XRP is set at $1.90. This level is critical as it marks a threshold that traders are closely monitoring to gauge the asset's future movements. 

The decline in XRP's price reflects broader market sentiments that are reacting to Bitcoin's sharp fluctuations. Such price actions can often lead to cascading effects across the crypto landscape, where movements in major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin tend to influence altcoins, including XRP. 

When Bitcoin experiences a sudden "pump and dump," it creates an environment of uncertainty, leading to increased volatility in the market. As traders react to the rapid changes, altcoins that were once stable can face abrupt price corrections, as seen with XRP's recent performance.
