## Overview

Dogecoin has recently experienced a notable decline, slipping below key support levels against the US Dollar. This downward movement indicates potential challenges for the cryptocurrency, raising questions about its future momentum and market position.

## Recent Price Movements

Dogecoin began its fresh decline after it closed below a significant price level. Initially, the price fell below the $0.1450 mark, leading to further losses as it dipped below $0.140. The decline was marked by a break below a crucial bullish trend line that had established support at $0.1450 on the hourly chart of the DOGE/USD pair.

As the price continued its downward trajectory, it formed a low near $0.1372. This movement is significant as it suggests bearish signs, especially since Dogecoin is now trading below the 23.6% Fibonacci retracement level of the downward move from a previous swing high. The price's struggle to maintain above the $0.140 level and the 100-hourly simple moving average further emphasizes the bearish sentiment surrounding DOGE.

If Dogecoin attempts a recovery, immediate resistance is noted near the $0.1410 level, with further resistance at $0.1450 and the 50% Fibonacci retracement level. The potential for a price climb exists, but it is contingent upon overcoming these resistance levels.

## Potential for Further Losses

The outlook for Dogecoin remains cautious. If the price fails to break above the $0.1450 level, it could continue its downward movement. Initial support is identified near the $0.1375 level, with subsequent support levels at $0.1350 and a critical level at $0.1330. A significant downside break below the $0.1320 support could lead to further declines towards lower price points.

Technical indicators suggest a bearish trend as well. The hourly MACD for DOGE/USD is gaining momentum in the bearish zone, while the Relative Strength Index (RSI) is positioned below the neutral 50 level. These indicators reinforce the notion that the market sentiment is leaning towards further declines unless significant support or reversal signals emerge.
