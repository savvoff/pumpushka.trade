## Overview

Shiba Inu has experienced a significant increase in spot trading activity across multiple exchanges in the past week. This uptick is noteworthy as it may signal a shift in market sentiment for the second-largest meme coin by market capitalization, which has previously struggled in the current market cycle.

## Spot Trading Volume Surge

Recent data from CoinGlass indicates a remarkable 154% increase in Shiba Inu's USD spot trading volume on Kraken over the last seven days. Other prominent exchanges, including Binance, Bybit, OKX, and Gemini, have also reported substantial increases in trading activity during the same timeframe. This surge suggests that spot buyers are potentially stepping in to protect the SHIB price at a critical support level amidst a broader decline in the cryptocurrency market.

In the last week, Shiba Inu has emerged as one of the few altcoins showing positive performance, with reports indicating an increase of almost 7%. This rise is occurring despite Bitcoin's fluctuating price movements, which have created a challenging environment for many cryptocurrencies. Additionally, data from CoinGlass reveals that the majority of leverage traders are currently optimistic about Shiba Inu, with a long/short ratio exceeding 1. This indicates that many traders are betting on a price increase for the meme coin.

However, there are mixed signals regarding Shiba Inu's future. The derivatives market has seen a decline, with trading volume down by over 10% and open interest decreasing by nearly 4%. These metrics could suggest a bearish outlook for the coin, contrasting with the positive trading activity observed.

## Positive Market Influences

Another factor contributing to the bullish sentiment around Shiba Inu is the anticipated interest rate cut by the Federal Reserve during the upcoming FOMC meeting. A reduction in interest rates could lead to increased liquidity in the crypto market, which may benefit altcoins like SHIB. Additionally, Bitcoin's efforts to maintain its position above the psychological $90,000 threshold could have a favorable impact on Shiba Inu, given the positive correlation between the two assets.

## Community Developments

The Shiba Inu community has also been active, with reports from a community member known as Shibizens highlighting recent developments. Over 45 billion SHIB tokens have been moved off exchanges, indicating that holders are accumulating the asset. Furthermore, there has been mention of a $35 million whale transfer to a private wallet, suggesting confidence among larger investors.

Coinbase is preparing to launch Shiba Inu futures on December 12, targeting both institutional and retail investors, which could enhance the coin's adoption. Additionally, NYSE Arca has submitted a filing for T. Rowe’s Shiba Inu ETF, moving the proposed ETF closer to realization. Shibizens have also pointed out upgrades to the Shibarium network, including an RPC upgrade and a confirmed full privacy upgrade using encrypted technology, which are expected to roll out next year.
