# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described below.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you contact us through the form or by email:

* Name
* Email address
* Message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

### 1.2. Automatically Collected Data

During your visit, we may collect:

* IP address (stored only for security purposes)
* browser type
* pages visited
* date and time of access

This is standard technical data used for analytics and security.

### 1.3. Cookies

The website may use cookies for:

* basic site functionality,
* analytics,
* saving language preferences.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data may be used to:

* respond to user messages,
* improve website functionality,
* analyze visitor statistics,
* ensure site security.

We do **not** sell or share personal data with third parties, except for trusted services providing essential technical infrastructure (such as analytics or hosting providers).

## 3. Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party tools, such as:

* web analytics (e.g., Google Analytics),
* advertising platforms (e.g., Google AdSense),
* anti-spam tools.

These services may use cookies or collect anonymized technical data.\
We do not control their privacy policies.

## 4. Data Protection

We implement standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data.\
However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* request a copy of your personal data,
* request deletion of your data,
* withdraw consent to data processing.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: **[contact@pumpushka.trade](mailto:contact@pumpushka.trade)**.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.\
The date of the last update is shown at the top of this document.
